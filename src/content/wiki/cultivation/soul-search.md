## Uses and Drawbacks

In order to use this technique, the target must first be subdued.

The risks for the target vary, at worst their soul will be destroyed and they will die, lighter cases however will at best become mentally damaged by the experience.

Risks for the user are dependent on their Cultivation level, anyone below Core Formation will run the risk of suffering the same damage as the victim when using this technique on a mortal and even at Core Formation the user will still experience a single drop in Cultivation level at the very least and as a result they can only use it three times in their lifetime.
