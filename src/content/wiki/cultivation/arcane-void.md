## Ascension

Breaking though to this level requires an Ethereal Essence. The reason there are so few of them, therefore, is because of the difficulty in comprehending something that cannot be seen, only noticed.

Most of those that break through to this step therefore had their Joss Flame Souls cultivate until one eventually comprehended the Essence they needed and absorbed it themselves. [ 1 ]

It is, however, also possible for a cultivator to just cultivate an Ethereal Essence directly, however this is significantly more difficult.

### Progression

The standard method for progression at this stage is to use Joss Flames to further refine the Ethereal Essence.

Should the cultivator not practice Joss Flame Cultivation and instead seeks to refine Essence on their own, they will be able to reach this level once they have attained Nine Essences, and then from there they can reach all the way up to the Great Circle by fusing those Essences until there are at least 3 sets of 3 fused into one.

### Arcane Tribulant

When an Arcane Void cultivator reaches their peak and can step into the Void Tribulant stage, nine tribulations called Arcane Tribulations shall befall them. If they survive these tribulations, they will enter Void Tribulant.

Undertaking these tribulations is optional to a degree, and many cultivators choose to stop at the Peak of Arcane Void rather than attempt them.

The first three Tribulants are External, the next three are Internal, the last three affect the soul.

Similar to Nirvana Shatterer's 5 Heavens Blights, the cultivator's power increases after every Tribulation and hence this is considered an independent sub level. Yet while passing any single tribulation will increase an individual's power, passing several at once will yield even greater benefits, passing all nine at once therefore is the most rewarding, but also risky, result.

There is a 10% chance of death at the First Tribulation, and a 90% chance of death at the last.

### Second (Tornado Tribulant)

Third (Thunder Tribulant) - If one passes three in a row, origin soul doubles in size

### Fifth (Blood Shadow Tribulant)

Sixth (Unknown Tribulant) - If one passes six in a row, origin soul triples in size

### Eighth (Ten Breath Life Soul Tribulant)

Ninth (Reincarnation Tribulant) - Enters Void Tribulant; If one passes nine in a row, origin soul increases several fold

### Trivia

According to the White Tiger General , the inhabitants of the Cave World should have been bound by The Ancient Celestial Emperor's nine laws. One such law forbade the Cultivation of Ethereal Essences, meaning Cave World Cultivators could not reach this level when the laws were in place.

The non-Joss Flame method of reaching and mastering this step makes it essentially similar to the Dao Realm Paragons of Er Gen's later novel I Shall Seal the Heavens, in that it takes a full nine Essences to complete that realm.
