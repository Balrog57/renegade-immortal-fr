## Ascension

There are multiple ways to form one's core seen in the story:

The proper method, which involves turning Qi into a solid core inside one's Dantian point.

the Underworld Ascension Method practiced by Rank 6 Country Demonic Cultivators.

Each method is viable, albeit with different results and chances of success.

### False Core/Green Core

Green Cores are a type of fake core found in Rank 4 countries and created using Demonic Techniques. The major difference between them and normal cores is that while a proper Core Formation Cultivator can only have a single core, one can possess tens of thousands of False Cores. These Cores are extremely dangerous when used to self destruct.

The Corpse Sect , or at least their branch in the Country of Zhao , the Corpse Yin Sect, is notable for practicing this.
