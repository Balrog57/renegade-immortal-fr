## Description

Finger of Death is one of the three low-grade Celestial Spells taught by Situ Nan to Wang Lin during their journey to the Trading Planet . The technique was created from tens of thousands of years of Situ Nan’s cultivation insights, making its power extraordinary and difficult to match. Throughout his entire life, Situ Nan passed down this spell only to Wang Lin .
