**Catégories :** Cultivation, Second Step

## Cause and Effect

At the Second Step, a cultivator can transform Heaven and Earth Origin Energy into Life Force. Therefore, they can theoretically live forever. The Heavens won't allow this and will directly send punishment. There are 5 desolations/tribulations in this level. With each passing of desolation, the practitioner's power will increase, this is what came to be called the "Five Broken Fingers" and later the "Five Heavens Blights".

To a "broken fingers" era Cultivator, the First Step represents the palm and the Second Step represents the palm line, this line is natural law. The "Broken Fingers" then is the level which cultivators collect the fingers of the hand. After passing the fifth Blight, the hand will be complete, being able to open the door to the Third Step.

Fifth Heaven's Blight Cultivators can use Third Step Spells at the cost of their own vitality due to the Great Void Gate being open for them. This is the real reason they are boasted as being the strongest beneath the Third Step.

This entire step can be skipped over if the practitioner has a method of forcefully opening the Great Void Gate.
