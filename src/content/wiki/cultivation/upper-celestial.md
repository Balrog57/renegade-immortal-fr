## Process

At the peak of Soul Formation a Cultivator would experience a tribulation. Those who survive would be brought into the Celestial Domain or one of its four subsidiary realms, which originally served as passageways. They would be given a true Celestial Body and would then achieve this form.

This process would be equivalent to the Soul Transformation stage in its entirety and takes only a very short time to undergo.

The sole demerit for a modern Cultivator undergoing this process would be the loss of their Domain as Qi Cultivators from the Celestial era didn't Cultivate Domains, they would instead become able to Cultivate Celestial Origin Qi.

### Trivia

The Thunder Celestials of the Allheaven Star System were able to undergo a conversion process to enter this path at the expense of giving up on their Qi Cultivator path.
