## Creation and Refining Process

The process of creating a celestial guard involves four distinct and deeply invasive stages that fundamentally alter the cultivator being refined. The first stage involves hardening the physical body to an extreme degree, making it capable of withstanding celestial spiritual energy and powerful magical treasures without sustaining damage. This transforms ordinary flesh into something closer to living metal, granting the guard extraordinary physical durability. The second stage targets the origin soul, which is torn apart, ground into pieces, massively expanded, and then reintegrated back into the body. Rather than destroying the soul, this process amplifies it, making it far more powerful and capable of defending itself independently.

The third stage involves deliberately withering the internal organs so they no longer function in the normal cultivator sense. By cutting off the body's connection to celestial spiritual energy through its organs, the guard is effectively severed from all five senses and the ability to experience pain. The fourth and final stage wipes out the divine sense entirely while carefully preserving raw intelligence. The result is a being that can think, strategize, and act with purpose, but feels nothing — no fear, no hesitation, no loyalty born of emotion. Whatever obedience remains is purely a product of the control mechanisms embedded during refinement.

### The Requirement of Willingness

One of the most critical and often overlooked prerequisites for successfully refining a celestial guard is the absolute willingness of the person undergoing the process. This is not merely a formality — it is a fundamental requirement without which the entire refining process will immediately collapse. Even the slightest internal resistance from the subject is enough to cause outright failure, making consent not just important but indispensable. Wang Lin himself emphasized that only through the person's genuine willingness can the second stage of the refining process be carried out successfully.

This requirement places those being refined in a uniquely grim position. Du Jian, for example, understood that his fate had already been decided and that resistance would only guarantee failure and death. Faced with no viable alternative, he chose to gamble on survival by agreeing to the process, accepting transformation over certain destruction. This illustrates the desperate and involuntary nature of much of the consent involved — willingness in this context is often less a free choice and more a final act of self-preservation made under impossible circumstances.

### Control and Loyalty

Celestial guards are kept loyal through seals and control spells woven into their very being during the refining process. These mechanisms allow the master to project a portion of their own divine sense or origin soul directly into the guard, effectively piloting it from within or commanding it remotely. Celestial Emperor Qin Lin was particularly renowned for his mastery of this technique, embedding multiple layers of control into his guards to prevent any possibility of rebellion or loss of loyalty. Because the divine sense of the guard itself has been erased, there is no inner resistance to overcome — the guard simply executes commands with complete and unwavering compliance unless the master chooses to release them.

### Ranks and Combat Power

Celestial guards are not uniform in power and are categorized into four distinct ranks, determined by the quality of the base cultivator used in the refining process as well as the rare materials incorporated into the body during refinement. Treasures such as Xuan gold are used to further reinforce and strengthen the physical form. Listed from weakest to strongest, the ranks are:

Iron — the lowest tier of celestial guard Copper — a step above iron, with greater durability and strength Silver — a high-tier guard with considerable combat power Gold — the highest and most formidable tier, radiating an overwhelming aura and possessing combat power capable of matching or overwhelming high-level cultivators, including those at the Nirvana Scryer level. Their physical attacks carry devastating force, with strikes capable of generating sonic booms and explosions powerful enough to shake entire star fields.
