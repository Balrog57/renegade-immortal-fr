**Catégories :** Cultivation, Second Step

## Essence

At this step, a Cultivator can use their insight into Law to grasp and Cultivate an Essence, this is a necessary step to Cultivate to the Third Step. With a completed Essence, one can summon the Great Void Gate , however if the practitioner has not reached Fifth Heavens Blight they'll need to use force to open it.

A Cultivator's Essence cultivation is itself directly related to their actual cultivation level, for example after devouring the Eternal Thunderbolt and completing his Thunder Essence, Wang Lin's Thunder Essence' Cultivation level was equal to a late stage Nirvana Shatterer while his other four Essences were each still only at mid stage. This is also why it's easier to practice one Essence and not waste years maturing more.

### Heaven's Blight

The five Heavens Blights are five brutal trials that allow the user to grow stronger if they survive. These five trials serve to unlock the Void Gate, allowing the user to reach the Third Step. At the 5th Blight, a Cultivator can use Third Step spells regardless of if they've achieved a compete Essence and reached the Third Step proper, but it will consume their life force. It is a common practice for a Heavens Blight Cultivator to seal their Cultivation at Peak Nirvana Shatterer to conserve and store up life force and vitality.

### Fourth

Fifth - Can invoke Third Step spells at the cost of Vitality.
