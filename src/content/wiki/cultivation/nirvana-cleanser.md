## Trivia

Early on it was stated that Cultivators who fail to reach Nirvana Shatterer will eventually still face a calamity akin to the Five Heavens Blights in this stage, due to their functional immortality. Their punishment however will be "far harsher" and they most likely will not survive. This concept was then never mentioned again.
