## Trivia

This is the last level shown by Wang Lin in the story of Renegade Immortal.

This level was first mentioned by Daoist Water , who believed that Wang Lin was the reincarnation of a cultivator who had reached the 4th Step, Heaven Trampling.

While the Grey-Robed Old Man states he and Wang Lin are the first and second 4th Step Cultivators to reach that step through Heaven Trampling, he also states that others have achieved the 4th step long before them, and many have achieved a 5th or even 6th step over time.

### ISSTH Spoilers

In I Shall Seal the Heavens, which is Er Gen's later novel set in the same universe, the Fourth Step is called Transcendence, in which Heaven Trampling, alongside its equivalents from PotT and ISSTH are interchangeably, its first realm.

In the Mountain and Seas Realm, the nine bridges used to reach this realm are known collectively as the Pāramitā Heaven Trampling Foundation.

In AWWP, Er Gen's 5th novel, information regarding Heaven Trampling is expanded. Wang Lin was able to expand the power of Heaven Trampling Bridge, thereby making Heaven Trampling's level equal to the entire 4th Step.
