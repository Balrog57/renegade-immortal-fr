## Uses

The technique is considerably more effective than refining on one's own as shown when Wang Lin used it to refine a Core Formation

Treasure that was infinitely close to Nascent Soul under the guidance of Situ Nan . However it comes with a few drawbacks. The user will feel much more significant levels of harm whenever the weapon is damaged and furthermore the weapon will forever release a blood-like smell, which will only get worse as it claims more lives.
