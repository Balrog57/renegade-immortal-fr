**Catégories :** Spells, Ancient Clan

## Creation and Source of Power

This Ancient Immortal Finger/Body was a powerful spell he had comprehended by borrowing what he had experienced in his Immortal Celestial Body!

It didn’t need celestial energy, all it needed was ancient power, but its effect was no weaker than the Immortal Celestial Body!

### Name Origin

This amount of recovery force could be called “immortal” by Wang Lin. This was enough to show how terrifying it was! “Immortal” meant that the recovery force could offsent all destruction from the heavens and earth. Only then could it be considered immortal!

### Trivia

With Wang Lin’s cultivation level, he couldn’t make this entire body immortal at first; he could only make one finger become immortal! Only later on, after he formed his Seventh Essence that allowed him to become a late stage Spirit Void Cultivator and that he became a 24 stars Ancient Dao did he have the power to make his body "immortal".
