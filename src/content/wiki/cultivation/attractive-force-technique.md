## Uses

The Attractive Force Technique is used to manipulate objects, moving them in a manner that can be called telekenetic. It is typically used to manipulate flying swords. However, when mastered to an absurd degree, it allows its user to bind, throw and directly harm others and at its peak can manifest large hands to carry out these tasks. It however will always have a limited range, beyond which it becomes weaker and eventually inert.
