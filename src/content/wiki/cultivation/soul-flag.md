## Uses

Captured Souls can be refined and utilised at the wielder's whim. The souls of relatives can be used to track down individuals. Further to this, if a person is attacked using their parent's souls abd their Cultivation is below the Foundation Establishment Stage, they will die instantly by experiencing their souls breaking.
