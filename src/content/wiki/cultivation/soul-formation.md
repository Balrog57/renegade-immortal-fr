## Ascension

In order to step into this stage the practitioner must integrate with the world to comprehend the heavens and obtain a domain. This is impossible for Ji Realm Cultivators as their path is too extreme, but it is conversely extremely easy for Dao Realm Cultivators.

An Origin Soul is then formed by fusing the newly established Domain with the Cultivator's divine sense into their Nascent Soul.

The most significant difference between a Nascent and Origin Soul is that the former resembles an infant while the latter is fully grown.

At this level a Celestial Body is given to all cultivators.

To then progress through this stage, one requires Celestial Qi, this is a scarce resource in the Sealed Realm and can only be obtained from the Four Celestial Realms or the Celestial Domain. This Qi however is abundant in the Outer Realm and Immortal Astral Continent, making it far easier to progress.

### Breakthrough Method

In truth, there are two methods to reach the Soul Formation stage. The first method is to follow someone else’s footsteps and copy their domain. This method is much easier than the second one, but the difficult part is that not many Soul Formation cultivators are willing to display their domain. Only peak Nascent Soul cultivators in large sects are given the privilege to see them.

The second method is to completely rely on yourself to try to comprehend the heavens to find own domain. This method is very difficult. It requires a lot of perseverance and luck to succeed, but once one have found own domain, ones be much stronger that those that went with the first method.

In general, only those who can’t obtain their own domain after trying for a long time choose the first method. After all, there is a huge difference in power between the two methods.

This gap is something that is almost impossible to close, whether it is at the start, or far in the future.

However, not many people can comprehend their own domain, so a majority of cultivators choose the first method. [ 1 ]

### Next Stages

During ancient times the Cultivator would experience a tribulation at the peak of Soul Formation and if they managed to survive it they would ascend to the Celestial Realm. They would turn into a celestial and gain a true Celestial Body while their Cultivation would reach the Upper Celestial level. This was to provide them with an alternative path so that they could eventually reach the Third Step without access to Joss Flames.

In modern times there is no Celestial Realm and thus no tribulation. The Cultivator must instead undergo the process of turning their mortal vessel into a Celestial Body manually without aid from a celestial being, this is known as the Soul Transformation stage. Once that process is complete the Cultivator will reach the Ascendant stage, which is equivalent to Upper Celestial.
