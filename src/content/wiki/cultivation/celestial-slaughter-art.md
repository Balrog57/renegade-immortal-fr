## Overview

The Celestial Slaughter Art is a powerful and unique celestial spell that revolves around the concept of slaughter to create life seals and harness vitality. It involves converting the energy from slaughter and killing into life force, forming protective life seals that provide strong defense. The more life seals one has, the stronger the defense becomes, to the extent that with enough life seals (such as one trillion), one could be safe even if a cultivation planet collapses

Wang Lin learned this spell from the grey-robed All-Seer, who taught it to him because of Wang Lin's cultivation of the life and death domain. The Celestial Slaughter Art is not a true celestial spell but an imitation of a celestial spell of unknown rank. To master it, Wang Lin had to promise that once he used the Celestial Slaughter Art, he must not leave any enemies alive [ 1 ]

The art requires experience through actual slaughter to be comprehended. Wang Lin entered a scarlet world filled with endless slaughter and blood pools to cultivate and understand the Celestial Slaughter Art deeply. In this place, continuous killing and absorbing killing intent allowed him to comprehend the heart of slaughter, which is the cycle where death creates life seals, embodying the transformation between life and death, or reincarnation [ 2 ]

The Celestial Slaughter Art harvests life force during slaughter, turning it into grey gas that can form life seals around the user. It has offensive capabilities and can be stored and used many times. However, cultivating it is extremely challenging because the slaughter energy can overwhelm the user's body if overused, potentially turning the user into a strand of slaughter energy if they reach too high a comprehension level without proper control

Wang Lin reached the small completion stage of the Celestial Slaughter Art after years of practice, being able to control several strands of slaughter energy. The art's essence is linked to the balance of life and death and reincarnation, creating a cycle where death leads to the creation of life seals. This understanding gave Wang Lin significant enlightenment and power, allowing him to wield the art with considerable killing intent

The Grey-robed All-Seer possessed a profound mastery of the Celestial Slaughter Art, able to condense up to 10 million strands of slaughter energy, and Wang Lin narrowly avoided becoming absorbed into the All-Seer's slaughter energy by giving up his cultivation at one million strands. Wang Lin also realized that the All-Seer taught him the art specifically because of his life and death domain cultivation

### Life Seals

Life seals are special type of seal formed through the Celestial Slaughter Art. It involves relying on slaughter to gather vitality and surrounding oneself with vitality to form life seals. The more life seals a person has, the stronger their defense becomes. The life seals provide both offensive and defensive capabilities, although not extremely powerful individually, they can be used many times and stored.

The defensive power of life seals is so strong that with a sufficiently large number of them, even the collapse of an entire cultivation planet wouldn't harm the bearer. For example, it is said that if one had one trillion life seals, they would be safe even if their cultivation planet exploded. This makes life seals an extremely powerful form of defense.

Wang Lin himself utilized life seals, accumulating tens of thousands of them, each carrying endless vitality, which helped his body recover rapidly from injuries. When under attack, the number of life seals could quickly swell to one million, providing a formidable protective shield.Life seals can be created from the grey gas that emerges from the Celestial Slaughter Art, which has both protective and offensive elements
