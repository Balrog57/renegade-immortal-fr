**Catégories :** Techniques

## Cultivation

The technique earns its name from the fact that cultivating it puts one at the very brink of death and one slip up can cause them to fall into the underworld for real. It is practiced by absorbing Yin Qi into the user's body and is split into three separate stages that are themselves also split into three more, meaning one must experience nine breakthroughs to complete the process.

### Stages

The first stage is the Ancestral Meridian, the second is the Dantian, and the third is the Qi Sea. Each one has to be opened three times to complete the construction of a Cold Core. To practice the next level, the user has to re-open the Meridian, Dantian or Qi Sea a second and then third time each. This act risks failure and would force them to start over from the first level of each of the three stages. The 1st, 4th and 7th Breakthroughs are the hardest because they corrospond to the furst time each of these key points is opened, each stage is also extremely painful. When the stage is complete a Cold Core will be formed within the user's body and when all three Cold Cores are formed one can attempt to fuse them, which requires him to re-break all three to fuse them together into a Core and reach the Core Formation Stage.

### Risks

There are several risks involved with the technique. Aside from general failure and the risk of death from practicing a technique which goes so far as to stop the user's heart and make them half a corpse each time he cultivates it, the technique is also highly unpredictable. Wang Lin for example experienced a very unusual situation where he was forced to complete all three openings of his Ancestral Meridian and even then attempt the fourth Breakthrough into the Dantian step because his body became unable to stop absorbing Yin Qi, the sole reason he didn't die was because this act constantly failed and thus expelled the Qi he was absorbing, which let him hang on until the source of the Qi stopped flowing in. This also caused him to develop the Ji Realm, which, while making him extremely powerful, caused him to have great difficulty breaking through to the Nascent Soul stage.
