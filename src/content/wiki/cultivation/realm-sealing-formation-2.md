## Background

It was the time of the Ancient Qi cultivators who had received the teachings of the Ancient Celestial Realm . They were the first wave of sentient life in the cave .

This formation was most likely set up by people from the Outer Realm 's Ancient Celestial Realm as its goal was to restrict Joss Flames from entering the Sealed Realm which would make it near-impossible for Inner Realm cultivators to reach the Third-Step since no one had ever managed to reach the Third-Step without Joss Flames, before Wang Lin . This slowly created a vicious cycle for tens of thousands of years until the Inner Realm couldn’t compare to the Outer Realm at all.

The only way that Inner Realm cultivators could reach the Third-Step would be to step into the Outer Realm long enough to be able to steal its Joss Flames. However, this was extremely tough as the Realm-Sealing Formation not only kept out the Joss Flames but also acted as a cage that kept the Inner Realm cultivators inside the formation. In fact, hundreds of thousands of Inner Realm cultivators died during the First and Second Sealed-Outer Realm Wars while trying to breakthrough the Realm-Sealing Formation.

### History

Daoist Water beseeched the Ancient Celestial Realm to provide him with a treasure that could strengthen the Realm-Sealing Formation to withstand the Third Sealed-Outer Realm War. The Ancient Celestial Realm provided him with a Golden Bell that could allow the formation to hold steady for 100 years. However, instead of fusing the bell with the formation, he intended to cause the Golden Bell to self-destruct and destroy the entire Cloud Sea Star System before defecting to the Sovereign Council . Fortunately, Wang Lin had the Nether Beast devour the Golden Bell's destructive energy which averted that crisis.

During the first battle of Third Sealed-Outer Realm War, Dao Master Blue Dream , Fifth Concubine , Sixth Concubine , Ice Boy and Qing Jiuyan each launched a strike upon the Realm-Sealing Formation which resulted in it tearing just outside the Cloud Sea Star System and allowed Outer Realm cultivators to enter the Inner Realm.

Later, Wang Lin was injured by a blow from Li Guang's Bow which forced the Nether Beast to appear and discharge the Golden Bell's destructive energy upon the Realm-Sealing Formation which resulted in an unsealed gap in the formation. Still, it also ended the battle and forced the Outer Realm to retreat.

Many years later, after Wang Lin ended the final battle in the Allheaven Star System , he destroyed the Realm-Sealing Formation and used his Life-Death, Karma, True-False Wheel Formation to create the Unnamed Wheel Formation which restricted Outer Realm cultivators from entering the Inner Realm yet allowed the Inner Realm cultivators to absorb the Ancient Primordial Star System 's Joss Flames.
