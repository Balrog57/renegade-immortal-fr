## Overview

The gate appears before a Nirvana Shatterer or Heaven's Blight Cultivator who has successfully cultivated an Essence to completion.

In the event that all Five Heaven Blights havent been endured, the gate will be firmly shut and the practitionar will have to forcibly break it open. The act of doing so however is beyond difficult and will certainly require more than just that one Essense worth of power. The sole example is Wang Lin, who broke open the gate without ever enduring a single Heaven's Blight. Situ Nan was also able to do something similar with help from Celestial Emperor Qing Lin .

When the gate is opened, Void Energy is bestowed upon the Cultivator, allowing them to enter the Nirvana Void level. However if the practitioner so chooses they can refuse to absorb that energy right away, this results in further gates appearing, each stronger than the last. Wang Lin for example refused to absorb the Void energy released to become a Third Step Cultivator through only one Essense, and he instead shattered five Void Gates, one for each Essense.

Should the practitioner persist in this then eventually the Ten Thousand Bone Great Void Gate will appear as it did for Wang Lin's sixth and final Slaughter Essense, this is a special kind of Void Gate that appears only before people of exceptional talent.

With every subsequent gate he broke Wang Lin skipped a stage in Nirvana Void, eventually allowing him to reach the middle level of Spirit Void .
