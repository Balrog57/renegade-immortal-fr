## Background

Like the God Sect , it was founded by the Lord of the Sealed Realm to combat the spatial crack that led to the Outer Realm . After the Lord of the Sealed Realm disappeared, the Demon Sect broke off from the God Sect and gained sole sovereignty over itself but it continued to fulfill its mission of combatting the fierce beasts that entered through the spatial crack.

### History

Every few thousand years, the Demon Sect calls forth young cultivators from the Rank 9 Region and lower to exterminate the accumulated fierce beasts. The best among these cultivators are taught the arts of the Demon Sect. Wang Lin entered the spatial crack to find Li Qian Mei who had agreed to a suicide mission in exchange for the sect helping deliver her life-blood to him. After exiting the spatial crack Wang Lin attracted the Infinite Retribution near the Demon Sect. He used it attack the Sealed Realm Formation and then used the Sealed Realm Jade to allow the Lord of the Sealed Realm to takeover the Sealed Realm Formation. Mysterious Brilliant , the Head Elder of Demon Sect, saw the Lord of the Sealed Realm and decided that Wang Lin was his inheritor.
