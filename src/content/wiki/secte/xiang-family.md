## Background

Xiang Family is a branch of the ancient Dong Ling Family.

### History

Xiang Family controls Planet Dong Ling and the Xu , Lu , and Liu families on it.

In the past, Dong Lingtai , the strongest Ancestor of the Xiang Family, stole Wang Lin 's vitality by tricking him by saying that he could revive Li Muwan . In fact, he couldn't do so and his actions only served to harm Li Muwan's already scattered nascent soul. Initially, he was going to suck all of Wang Lin's vitality and kill him but he suddenly received a message from the Dong Ling Family stating that he is to take some of Wang Lin's aura send it to them. This was due to the fact that one of their Ancestors, Elder Shen , was a 7-star Ancient God and they hoped that Wang Lin's aura could help awaken her.

After Wang Lin returned from the Outer Realm , he stormed the Planet Dong Ling and slaughtered the Lu Family that attempted to stop him before capturing, soul-searching, and killing Dong Lingtai. After he killed Dong Lingtai, a member of the Dong Ling Sect suddenly appeared through a portal and attempted to pass a death sentence upon him but was almost killed by a single punch from Wang Lin. He was spared by Wang Lin as he sensed the aura of the 7-star Ancient God from inside the portal.
