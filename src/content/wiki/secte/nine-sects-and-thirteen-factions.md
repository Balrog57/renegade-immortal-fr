## Strength

Each of the 21 Sects are all extremely powerful, with multiple Void Tribulant cultivators in their ranks. To the rest of the Immortal Astral Continent, Arcane Void would be a powerful level, but to them it is simply not much. While a large Sect would have a Middle Stage Void Tribulant Master, these Sects have plenty of Cultivators at that level. The Sect Ancestors are at least at the Golden Exalt level, while Sect Masters are at least Late Stage Void Tribulant. The Purple Sun Sect even possesses a Grand Empyrean , Gemini, who was considered the strongest Grand Empyrean before she split into two during her latest reincarnation cycle.
