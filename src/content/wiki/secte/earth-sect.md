## Overview

Unlike most large sects, the Earth Sect only has around 30,000 disciples, as focus on quality rather than quantity.

Disciples are ranked from 1 to 9, depending on ability, there are as few as 100 Rank 9 disciples and about 20,000 Rank 1 disciples. As a box standard, the 5th Rank disciples appear to be around the Second Step's Nirvana Cleanser level.

### Book Twelve

Wang Lin visited the Earth Sect to get the map of the Meng Earth Continent. While there, he was pleasantly surprised to sense the aura of Zhou Yi and Qing Shuang . He even informed the Earth Sect's Golden Exalt Ancestor to look after the two of them.
