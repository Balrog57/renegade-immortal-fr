## Background

In the past, Master Hollow Wind attempted to kidnap Ling'er and use her as a cultivation incubator as she has a water spirit body. However, he dreaded the master of the Three Chen Brothers.

### History

The Three Chen Brothers took the job of guarding the Ghost Eye City on Planet Tian Yun . They attempted to embarrass Wang Lin as he had unwittingly spread his divine sense over the city. However, Wang Lin soon turned the tables on them and injured them. They all came to apologize and Wang Lin accepted their apology.

Afterwards, they invited Wang Lin to tea and Master Yi Chen introduced his granddaughter, Ling'er, to him. Wang Lin felt a sense of goodwill towards her for unknown reasons. At the time he thought that it must be because she has a water spirit body like Liu Mei which caused him to be slightly sentimental. He gifted her the hat Yun Quezi gave him and two small chiming bells that she put on her wrist.

Soon after, All-Seer attacked Wang Lin because he took the Extreme Yang from the dying Bai Wei . Master Yi Chen, along with the City Lord of Ghost Eye City, went close to witness their battle but two were pushed back and injured from the shockwaves of Wang Lin using the Summon the Rain crystal Qing Shui gave him.

Afterwards, they met Wang Lin when he was passing through their home planet after subduing Master Hollow Wind. They invited him in and he accepted their offer. There, he met Ling'er and Master Yi Long's disciple.

A few days later, Wang Lin refined the Azure Ancient God Shield and caused a scene as a enormous Ancient God silhouette appeared in the sky.
