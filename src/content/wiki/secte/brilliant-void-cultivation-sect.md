## Background

In the past, they teamed up with the Cultivation Alliance to defeat the Four Divine Sect . After many conspiracies, they were able to bring the Four Divine Sect to their weakest point.

In the middle of the war, the most powerful Azure Dragon Divine Emperor went missing and the news of the most feared Vermillion Bird Divine Emperor entering his third Heaven’s Blight was leaked. This opened the Four Divine Sect for an unprecedented crisis. Nevertheless, the Four Divine Sect was still strong enough to fight against the Cultivation Alliance, and they didn’t fear the Brilliant Void Realm.

Brilliant Void Realm attacked in full-force, breaking its agreement of only indirectly participating. Still the Alliance and Brilliant Void were unable to completely annihilate the Four Divine as they dreaded their Four Divine Beast Treasures.

### History

Mu Bingmei is current Saintess of the Brilliant Void Realm. Liu Mei was one of her nine avatars. She took Zhou Ru as her disciple most probably due to the latter's connection with Wang Lin .

Mu Bingmei's junior disciple sister entered the East Demon Spirit Sea alongside Middle-Aged Woman and Master Void, an elder of the Cultivation Alliance and Master Zhongxuan 's younger brother.

It was destroyed by Tuo Sen after he escaped from the Land of the Ancient God. Its Saintess, Mu Bingmei, was seriously injured and forced to flee while its Ancestor, Lu Yundao , was sealed into one of Tuo Sen's Ancient God stars.
