## History

After Master Scarlet Soul was released by Wang Lin from Ye Mo's seal during Wang Lin's battle with the Sovereign on the false Suzaku, Master Scarlet Soul hid himself for the duration of the war to regain his lost cultivation.

After Wang Lin left for the Immortal Astral Continent Master Scarlet Soul recruited a descendant of the Wang Clan , rumoured to be Wang Lin's own descendant, as a disciple and founded this sect.

He then launched an assault on the New Celestial Realm , hoping to use the blood essence of the Cave World 's strongest Cultivators to break through to Golden Exalt . His disciple was later killed by Thirteen , who had recently arrived with the Half Heaven Trampling Wang Lin, Liu Jinbiao , Xu Liguo and the Golden Exalt Sea Dragon.
