## Background

During the war against the Cultivation Alliance and the Brilliant Void Cultivation Sect , the Azure Dragon Divine Sect's Emperor disappeared; the Black Tortoise Divine Sect's Emperor turned traitor; and the White Tiger Divine Sect's Emperor died in battle. Only the Vermillion Bird Divine Sect's Emperor survived.

### History

Vermillion Bird Divine Sect came out of seclusion when Wang Lin achieved first awakening of his Vermillion Bird mark. Lu Yun sent six elders and most of the second step cultivators to the East Demon Spirit Sea to escort Wang Lin back to the sect, safely.

When Wang Lin achieved second awakening of his Vermillion Bird mark, Lu Yun personally went in his origin soul form to escort Wang Lin back to the sect, safely. Along the way, he even brought along the elders of the three other divine sects and the Holy Weapons of the three other divine sects.

After Wang Lin was safely back at the Holy Vermillion Bird Planet , Lu Yun had him absorb fire origin energy from the Vermillion Bird Holy Weapon along with his own origin energy for three years. This allowed him to achieve the first of the Nine Mysterious Transformations which is something each Vermillion Bird Divine Emperor is required to achieve to be crowned.
