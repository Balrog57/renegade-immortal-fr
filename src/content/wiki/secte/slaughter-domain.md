## Background

The Slaughter Realm created their own world as their base. Countless years ago, the founder of the Slaughter Realm took over a crack left over from the collapsed Rain Celestial Realm. He also gathered lot of heavenly metal to create the 99 Heaven Raising Swords. These 99 Heaven Raising Swords became buildings unique to the Slaughter Realm.

In the Cultivation Alliance, the Slaughter Realm’s name is illustrious, and over the tens of thousands of years, it has completed countless exploits for the Cultivation Alliance. Whether it was the first battle against the Four Divine Sect or the later exchanges with the Corpse Sect , all of those conflicts had the shadow of the Slaughter Realm.

### Members

Over tens of thousands of years, Slaughter Realm has maintained a core of 99 cultivators. If anyone dies, they would immediately be replenished. Aside from these 99 cultivators, there are also the outer disciples. There are many outer disciples, and they are led by these 99 cultivators.

Aside from these 99, there is the Vice Realm Master and the Realm Master, Ling Yunzi.
