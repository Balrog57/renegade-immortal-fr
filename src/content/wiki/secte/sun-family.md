## History

When Wang Lin (disguised as Xu Mu) was visiting Planet Ran Yun for the first time. The Sun, Zhao and Ran families were entangled in a predicament that was like a knife dangling above their heads (about to fall at any moment).

Four years ago, the three families pursued and killed an injured cultivator that was passing by their planet and ended up taking 5 rank 8 pills that were extremely valuable for Ascendant cultivators.

However, they also saw that this injured cultivator was part of the Huan family from Planet Thousand Illusion. It was too late for regrets since they already killed the cultivator.

Knowing that the Huan family will always seek revenge for their family members, they were planning to pass this hot potato to Wang Lin (saying that this pill will be sold at their Treasure pavilion).
