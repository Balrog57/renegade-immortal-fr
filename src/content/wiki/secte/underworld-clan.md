## History

They were gifted a planet in the Summoned River Star System by the Sovereign Council after they earned some achievements during the Third Sealed-Outer Realm War.

After Wang Lin appeared to liberate Summoned River, he killed the Underworld Clan's Fifth Heaven's Blight Patriarch and all the thousands of its members.
