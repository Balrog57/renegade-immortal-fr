## History

One of the Demonic Sect of Zhao, their demonic cultivation is around the Demonic Flame.

After 400 years, they were able to stay in the first-rank sect of Zhao, unlike Wu Feng Valley, Jie Mie Sect or the orthodox, apart from Xuan Dao Sect, who fell to second-rank.
