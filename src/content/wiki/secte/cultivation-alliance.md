## Appearance

It is a giant cultivation planet with countless smaller planets surrounding it. A monstrous blood light is constantly emitted from below the planet. There is spatial rift below the cultivation planet and the blood light is coming from that rift.

### Background

Long ago, Cultivation Alliance barely managed to defeat the Four Divine Sect for the position of overlord of the Brilliant Void Star System. They only managed to do this due to:

News of the Vermillion Bird Divine Emperor, Lu Yun , facing his third Heaven's Blight being leaked.

Azure Dragon Divine Emperor disappearance.

Black Turtle Divine Emperor turning traitor and attacking Vermillion Bird Divine Emperor before escaping.

Brilliant Void Cultivation Sect attacking in full-force, breaking its agreement of only indirectly participating.

### History

After Lu Yun saved Wang Lin and killed the Celestials that came to kill Qing Lin, news of the Alliance's miserable defeat spread across the entire Star System.

Most of the sects and clans located in Brilliant Void declared independence. This includes Corpse Sect which took the entire southern region of the star system as their territory. Also, Four Divine Sect took the eastern region of the star system as their territory.

Moreover, the most of the Alliance's Rank 7 Cultivation planets and all four of the the Alliance's Rank 8 Cultivation planets that included Country of Rising Cloud , Country of Reed Flute , Temple of Heaven , and Evil Earth Sect declared their independence.

Finally, after two years, the Alliance's HQ opened and declared that all the "independent sects" that resist Allheaven for 60 years will be spared of any punishment and they will be allowed to enter the Elder Council. Also, the Alliance sent out a kill order for Wang Lin .
