Treasure Jade Sect is the strongest and wealthiest sect in the Rank 5 Region of Cloud Sea Star System . Every 30 years, the Treasured Jade Sect holds an auction where comprehensive list of treasures, pills, pill recipes, and spirit beasts are up for sale.
