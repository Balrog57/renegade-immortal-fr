## Background

When the country of Suzaka arrived on this planet, there were six major sects within it.

Then the disciples of those six sects spread out and formed the various cultivation countries on the planet. At the same time, the core disciples of those six sects were allowed to form rank 5 cultivation countries

### Soul Refining Sect was one of them

Whenever a rank 5 cultivation country was about to reach the point of becoming a rank 6 country, they would be beaten back to their original state in just one night. The country of Suzaku wouldn’t allow another rank 6 cultivation country on planet Suzaku

Soul Refining Sect has had three chances to reach rank 6 throughout these countless years, but we were beaten back every time. Now we have long lost our country and can only exist as a sect

Back before there was a single cultivator on planet Suzaku, back when the country of Suzaku was only a rank 5 cultivation country on planet Four Saints, all six sects worked hard together. Through bitter struggle, we reached rank 6 and broke free from planet Four Saints. Then we were given planet Suzaku by the cultivation union

Soul Refining Sect had three Ascendant cultivators. The other five sects all had several as well. The country of Suzaku was so powerful, but when we arrived here, what we found was not good. There was already another race here that used the power of tattoos, and they were very powerful

### History

Soul Refining Sect is a sect in the Rank 5 country of Pilu. The leader of the sect Dun Tian, a Peak Stage Soul Transformation cultivator. He took Wang Lin as his disciple and passed down Soul Refining Sect’s inheritance to him including the One-Billion-Soul Flag. They had three time opportunity to become Rank 6, but failed because of the suppression of the Rank 6 country of Suzaku. They come from Planet Four Saints and became a Rank 5 country after arriving at Planet Suzaku, they had 3 Ascendant cultivators at that time.

After receiving the One-Billion-Soul Soul Flag, Wang Lin had to promise Dun Tian to protect the Soul Refining Sect. If the sect were to be destroyed on Suzaku, Dun Tian hoped Wang Lin would revive the sect somewhere else. Dun Tian’s lifetime ultimate goal is for the sect to be a rank 6 sect or even higher. As long as the One-Billion Soul Flag still exists, there is a hope for the sect to always be revived.

When Wang Lin gets sent to the East Demon Spirit Sea, he founded a new tribe called Soul Refining Tribe and taught them the cultivation techniques of the Soul Refining Sect. By doing this, he becomes the new ancestor of the tribe. Wang Lin altered the soul refining spell a little bit, so that he had full control over all soul fragments. He did this partly to prevent the new sect from using their spells against him, but also so he can collect all soul fragments from them to revive the One-Billion-Soul Soul Flag.

### Outer Sect

Everyone can become an outer disciple of Soul Refining Sect by simply pay registration fee and monthly room rental free after that, the sect won't do any background check to the cultivators that register, this is why Soul Refining Sect attract many rogue cultivators that search a place for closed door cultivation.

Every three year an outer sect competitions will be held. A disciple can choose to join this competition with a chance to become an inner sect disciple by winning the competitions. When the competition ended, only less than half of the outer sect disciples would remain alive. The losers would have to pay more spirit stones to ensure that they could stay until the next competition begins again.

### Inner Sect

The Inner Sect is separated into three factions: Soul Refining Mountain, Soul Extracting Mountain and Soul Sealing Mountain. Behind these three mountains are nine giant rings of golden light that represents a Soul Formation cultivator residing in there. Inner Sect disciple will be placed in one of three factions. There no rule in this sect, anyone can kill or steal, but if one of the parties surrender and given up all their belongings, the other parties are forbidden to kill them or an elder would have to interfere. There will be punishment if someone were to break this rule.

### Techniques

Soul Refining Sect practice Soul Flag Production Method as the main cultivation technique. This technique split into three parts: Soul Refining, Soul Extracting, and Soul Sealing. The first two can be learn by all inner sect disciples, and the third one can only be learned by a core disciple or the next sect master.

### Soul Refining

Condense - With this technique, one can make the ethereal soul fragment take form and put it inside a soul flag.

Soul Vortex - It is a very rare technique. At least, this is the case in the Soul Refining Mountain. It require at least Nascent Soul level cultivation to learn, but not all Nascent Soul cultivators can learn it.

Blood Sacrifice - Very few Nascent Soul are able to learn it. Using this technique would increase the power of the soul flag by several fold.

### Soul Extracting

Soul Calming - It is the core of the soul extracting technique. If soul calming isn’t used first, the success rate of soul extraction will be very low. After being used, the success rate increases greatly.

Soul Luring - It is the technique of pulling a soul out of a living person.

Soul Poison - It is the most vicious and most powerful of the three techniques, on par with the Soul Refining Mountain’s Blood Sacrifice. Soul Poison use a mysterious method to turn a living soul into poison that could silently kill. Once it has been refined enough, it will turn into a poisonous soul with the power of a Core Formation cultivator.

### Soul Sealing

The Core technique of the Soul Refining Sect, permit the cultivator's soul to not be affected by external things.

Only by learning it, can it be possible to wield the One-Billion-Soul Soul Flag, that would grant the wielder with the combat prowess on par with any Ascendant Stage cultivator.

### Trivia

A long time ago, a disciple of the Soul Refining Sect betrayed the sect and escaped. Using Soul Poison Technique, he went unopposed in a Rank 4 cultivation country and made his own sect called the Demonic Pill Sect. This person used some mysterious method to refine the poisonous soul into a pill and then inject death aura into it to turn it into a green core. He called this the Green Core Method and this allowed him to reach the Soul Formation stage. He was considered a genius of his time. A green core and a golden core are very different. You can only have one golden core, but you can have many green cores. He used this method developed from the soul poison technique to reach the Soul Formation stage. He had tens of thousands of green cores at that point. When he attacked, he would send out thousands of green cores to explode. His power was something even Soul Transformation cultivators wouldn’t want to mess with carelessly.
