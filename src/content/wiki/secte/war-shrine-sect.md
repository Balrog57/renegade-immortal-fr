## Background

War God Shrine is a sect in the Rank 3 country of Hou Fen . It is famous because their ancestor found the Divine Path Technique that create an Avatar to help cultivators breakthrough to Nascent Soul Stage. The sect has six Nascent Soul Stage cultivators.

### Plot

Wang Lin assume Ma Liang identity as War God Shrine Sect disciple, after he seized the disciple corpse in Foreign Battleground when he was a soul devourer. Later he use his identity as the sect disciple to learn the Divine Path Technique after back from Sea of Devil with Duanmu Ji.
