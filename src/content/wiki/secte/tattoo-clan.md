## Planet Suzaku

Suzaku has a tribe called the Forsaken Immortal Clan . This branch was created by Tuo Sen . When the savages of Suzaku first saw him, they thought he was a god and started to worship him. Tuo Sen gave them the Tattoo Clan cultivation method.

### Allheaven Star System

In the Western Domain, also known as the Strange Domain, there is a tribe called the Chosen Immortal Clan that practice the same cultivation method as the Forsaken Immortal Clan of Planet Suzaku . The members of this tribe were under Celestial King Carefree and had for role to guard the Mysterious Yin Furnace inside the Nether Beast .
