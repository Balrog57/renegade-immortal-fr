## Book 7

Wang Lin found the Forsaken Immortal Clan in the Thunder Celestial Realm . When they first saw him, they believed him to be a Celestial Messenger. They had thought that they were the servants of the Celestial Clan and that a Celestial Messenger had come back for their salvation. Wang Lin helps them fight off Demonic Fog Beast. Ta Shan, a 13 leaf tattoo cultivator, then challenge Wang Lin because he didn't believe in the Celestial beliefs of the clan. After losing, he became more respectful toward Wang Lin. When they entered the cave where the Mysterious Yin Furnace was held, Ta Shen asked Wang Lin if he was actually a Celestial Messenger. Wang Lin told him the truth about himself as well as the Celestial Clan and realm: The Celestial Realm had long collapse and there were very few Celestials left. Wang Lin also told him about the history of the Celestial and Immortal Clan and how the Celestials won the battle and enslaved the Immortal Clan.

Wang Lin stays with the Forsaken Immortal Clan for a while and after he went out of closed door cultivation, he finds out that Ta Shen is severely injured and on the brink of death. Wang Lin choose to risk refining Ta Shen into a Celestial puppet in hope of one day recovering his consciousness. Wang Lin then leads the Forsaken Immortal Clan out of the Thunder Celestial Realm into the Western Domain of the Allheaven Star System .

### Book 8

Ta Shan regains his consciousness thanks to the Tattoo clan’s Holy Ancestor.
