## Overview

Like their hated rivals Ancient Clan , the Celestial Clan worships a singular ancestor known as the Celestial Ancestor . However, unlike the Ancients, their cultivation method does not revolve around replicating their ancestor's power, but rather finding their own path, which was the Celestial Ancestor's intention (The main cultivation path seen in the novel is the Celestial Clan's).

### Imperial Family

There are those within the clan who bear the Celestial Ancestor's direct bloodline and are known as the Imperial Family. Their blood itself holds great power, and spells under their influence shine golden. They have three Celestial Marks upon their bodies which are the foci of their true powers. Those with the royal bloodline also possess the Immortal Celestial Body , which is immortal in the literal sense. No matter how thoroughly destroyed, they will manage to regenerate from almost anything.

### Organization

The clan is a monarchy, albeit a very loose one in comparison to the Ancient Clan . The Celestial Emperor can issue edicts that are for the most part obeyed, but are sometimes not followed to by the letter. The emperor also has very loose control over the clan's Grand Empyreans, who may be relied upon in the event of an attack by the Ancient Clan, but otherwise cannot be ordered around easily. In the event of an attack on the Celestial Emperor and the capital, most Celestials often do not interfere unless they have a connection or unique loyalty to the emperor. Even the death of the emperor due to an usurpation by another Celestial would not instigate the wrath of the majority of the Celestial Clan, with the exception of those who were loyal to the former emperor.

The Imperial Family is stationed in the Central Continent and issues all imperial edicts there with the Mourning-Death Clan as their advisors (whom they enslaved). The Celestial Emperor for the majority of the novel was Lian Daozhen . However, after his death at the hands of Wang Lin and the other Celestial Grand Empyreans, the title was passed to his brother Lian Daofei .

### Territory

The Celestial Clan rule over the Immortal Astral Continent's Southern and Eastern Regions, and those continents are home in turn to the 72 Continents, which the Celestial Ancestor created by sealing away 72 powerful beings foreign to the Immortal Astral Continent, such as the Heavenly Bull.

These 72 Continents are distributed amongst five great continents, which are each governed by a Grand Empyrean , with the exception of the Western Continent.

The Central Continent is special as it is where the Imperial Capital lies. It is the main residence of the Celestial Emperor and the Imperial Family, as well as their advisors the Mourning-Death Clan . Imperial Edicts are issued from the Central Continent and within the Imperial Palace is the seal containing the 72 Foreign Beings, as well as a special domain that contains the Celestial Ancestor's inheritance.

### Trivia

Old Ghost Zhan mentioned early on that there are Cultivation Levels unique to Celestials, such as a Rank 7 Celestial or an Earth Celestial. However by the time Xuan Luo sees Wang Lin in the Cave World, this idea appears to have been scrapped in favor of the main Cultivation System in Renegade Immortal itself belonging to the Celestial Clan.

It is also possible Zhan was not talking about Cultivation Ranks, but some kind of Military style rank instead.

Those in the Imperial Family bear the surname Lian, stemming from the late Celestial Ancestor Lian Yunjue .
