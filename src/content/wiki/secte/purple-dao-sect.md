## Background

Over 3,000 years ago, Purple Dao Sect was a minor sect in the Rank 5 Region but today its one of the strongest sects there. This was all done due to the efforts of Lu Yuncong .

### History

Two elders and over a hundred disciples of Purple Dao Sect invaded the Alliance Star System while it was warring with the Allheaven Star System . They managed to lure over two dozen of Wang Lin's mosquito beasts into a trap but they were killed by a fuming Wang Lin , soon after.

Rudy , the young master of Purple Dao Sect, invaded the Origin Sect soon after its Ancestor passed away. He did so in an attempt to kidnap Lu Yanfei and use her as his cultivation furnace. However, he, along with his attendant, Song Wude , were killed by Wang Lin.
