## History

After Little Red escaped from the Fallen Land, he ran quite far before stopping and establishing his own sect. All the sect members were those weaker than Little Red and were coerced into joining the sect.

Every few days, Little Red would set up his own congregation and have them march through the Outer Realm while carrying him and shouting chants of his praise. Little Red mapped out the route the congregation took very cautiously and made sure to not attract the attention of any cultivator stronger than itself.

Unfortunately, one day he met Wang Lin and was forced to become his slave.
