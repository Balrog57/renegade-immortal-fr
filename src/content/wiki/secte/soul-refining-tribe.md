## Background

In the past, Huang Long helped people of the village in The Valley . He set up formations so that the wild demon spirits would not be able to enter the territory of the village.

### History

The Valley was the first sign of civilization Wang Lin saw after he entered the East Demon Spirit Sea. After he bested Ouyang Hua , he was invited inside by the latter. He gave some spirit pills in exchange for absorbing the sliver of demon spirit energy inside the village. Later, he saved the village from the attack of another cultivator and established the Soul Refining Tribe.

Wang Lin then ordered the tribe members to attack all the small villages in the vicinity and absorb their members. The tribe members make the defeated people drink a memory-wiping drink and then assimilate them into their own tribe. Through this process, Soul Refining Tribe became the strongest power in the Sky Demon Country 's lands outside of the Sky Demon Country itself.

After returning from the Sky Demon Country's capital, Wang Lin ordered the tribe members to enter the Ancient Battlefield and absorb the resentful souls from there. Then he ordered them to participate in the war between the Sky Demon Country and the Fire Demon Country.

After a few centuries when Wang Lin returned to the East Demon Spirit Sea, he found that the tribe controls the land of what was formerly the Fire Demon Country and stands on equal standing with the other eight countries.
