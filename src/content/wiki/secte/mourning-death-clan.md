## History

This clan came from beyond the Immortal Astral Continent and were enslaved by the Immortal Ancestor. They're also the origin of Joss Flames. Seeking freedom, they set up a plan involving Wang Lin.

Empyrean Exalt Hai Zi is currently the only known living member of this clan in the Immortal Astral Continent.

### Note

It is currently (and hopefully temporarily) translated as Dao Wang Clan in the official translation.

### Spoilers

- This clan appears in Pursuit of the Truth and is mentioned in I Shall Seal the Heavens

Pursuit of the Truth Spoiler: - They originated from one of the races in Pursuit of the Truth in which this clan was exiled from the world MC reside.

I Shall Seal The Heaven Spoiler: - Shui Dongliu was said to be the mixture of this clan and Nine Seals’s discarnate soul.
