## Appearance

He has a gloomy face and wears deep blood-red robes for clothes.

### Personality

He has a twisted, cruel and bloodthirsty personality developed due to the tens of thousands of murders he has committed. He is also supremely arrogant as he is a member of the Slaughter Realm under the Cultivation Alliance.

### Background

In the past, he committed tens of thousands of murders along with another person and together the two earned the moniker of "Soul Killer Duo."

### History

He was sent to kill Xu Mu (Wang Lin) after he left Planet Suzaku . In the beginning, he held the advantage against Wang Lin and his entourage ( Big-Head , Ta Shan , Lei Ji ) due to his superior cultivation and seven early-stage Nirvana Scryer avatars. However, Wang Lin fused with his original Ancient God body soon after and severely injured Yu Fei. Yu Fei even managed to escape to the Slaughter Realm but Wang Lin killed him inside the Slaughter Realm through the portal Yu Fei escaped through.
