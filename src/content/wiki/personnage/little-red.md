## Background

He followed Lian Daofei when the latter entered the Cave World to steal the Heavenly Dao from the Ancient Celestial Sovereign . He was separated from Lian Daofei after the latter had his divine sense devoured by the Heavenly Dao.

Afterwards, he was lured to the Fallen Land by the Vermillion Bird Ancestor and enslaved by him.

### History

Wang Lin saw Little Red when he met the Second Vermillion Bird as he witnessed the Second Vermillion Bird lure Little Red out from his lake and steal his Dragon Heart blood.

After the Young Emperor Trial ended, the Second Vermillion Bird freed Little Red and allowed it to leave. In fact, he had placed a seal upon him which would allow Wang Lin to control it and gain a Third Step protector when he left the Fallen Land. However, Ye Mo 's Ancient Tomb opened up soon after Wang Lin left the Fallen Land which resulted in him forgetting about Little Red. This allowed Little Red to escape and gain freedom for almost two centuries. He even set up his own Celestial Dragon Sect .

172 years later, Wang Lin met him just a few hours before the Four Generals descended from the Ancient Celestial Realm . He learned of Little Red's identity then but put him away as he needed to have a calm state of mind for the things to come and couldn't be distracted by Little Red's revelations.
