## Overview

The practice involves creating a Joss Flame Realm. One way to achieve this is by capturing souls and turning them into Joss Flame Souls by storing them in the space used as a Joss Flame Realm and forcing them to worship the practitioner. This worship shall produce Joss Flames for the practitioner.

Another method, used by the Immortal Astral Continent 's Celestial Clan is to create a Cave World and have Cultivators and mortals born within worship them, under the guise of worshiping the heavens.

Joss Flames are extremely powerful and contain a hint of Third Step power, but if they are produced by such a Cave World practitioner then they will be poisonous to the user for the benefit of the Cave World's owner.

To reach the Arcane Void level, many Third Step Cultivators will choose to have their Joss Flame Souls cultivate, and when one forms an Ethereal Essence, they will steal it.

Many Joss Flame Souls have over the ages wondered if their world is just a realm created by someone else, and tried to escape, sadly, however, in the unlikely event that they do escape, they will lose their cultivation and become mortals, because their cultivation was simply never real to begin with.

Joss Flames are also unnecessary if the practitioner is willing to go the harder route and cultivate Essence with their own insight instead, this method is preferable for someone who intends to practice multiple Essences, as after reaching the Third Step with however many Essences one has at the time of the initial breakthrough will permanently prevent any additional Essences obtained afterwards from ever reaching the Third Step themselves.

### Warning Spoiler

The Joss Flame are only produced by a beyond heaven's Clan who was subdued by the Immortal Ancestor.

This clan produce and use the Joss Flame without drawback, unlike the Celestial Clan member who find it poisonous. This clan can also transform other being so they can absorbe the Joss Flame but they can't be perfect that lead to the Joss Flame being poisonous. Other Being who can absorb the Joss Flame without drawback are being born in the Joss Flame Realm created by their Clan technique.

### ISSTH SPOILERS

The practice of Joss Flames seems to have mostly died out by the events of I Shall Seal the Heavens, Er Gen's third series set countless years after Renegade Immortal, likely due to its numerous demonstrated drawbacks, most notable being the way it limits how many Essences one can Cultivate, as multiple Essence cultivation has visibly become the mainstream in that era. This is shown by how even Immortal God and Devil Realm Continents seem to possess Cultivators belonging to the Dao Realm. However the Black Seive Sect's Dao Reserve does seem to still use Joss Flames, but even then he is only a First Step Cultivstor and thus is likely using them differently as he would not yet possess an Essence.
