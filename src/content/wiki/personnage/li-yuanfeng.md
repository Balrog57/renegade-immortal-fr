## Background

His cultivation was forced to the early Soul Transformation stage through a lot of medicine and pills. He was the weakest of the Soul Transformation cultivators.

### History

Li Yuanfeng wanted to get revenge for Red Butterfly 's constant demise against Wang Lin . He was also afraid of Wang Lin's growth. As a result, he schemed against Wang Lin to kill Wang Lin. He contacted the Giant Clan's ancestor to help him set a restriction to trap Wang Lin so he couldn't teleport away. When they attacked Wang Lin, they almost killed him but Wang Lin was saved by Sun Tai . Sun Tai was able severely injure Li Yuanfeng and Li Yuanfeng escaped the battle. After Wang Lin recovered from his injury, he came to the Xue Yue Sect and killed Li Yuanfeng and prohibited the entire Xue Yue Sect from having anyone higher than a Nascent Soul cultivation.
