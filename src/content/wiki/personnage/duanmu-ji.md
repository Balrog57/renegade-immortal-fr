## Appearance

Green robed old man with an Golden Plum Flower on his shirt. He has a green gourd.

### Plot

He need Wang Lin help with his Death Spell to pass Land of the Ancient God third trial. In panic of Desolate Beast attack, he enter first trial together with Wang Qingyue and got separated with others. Wang Lin see the two again in third trial fighting Wandering Souls. After that the two captured by demonized Hunchback Meng and thrown them away to a rift that lead to Blood Sea where they will turned into a demon by Tuo Sen.
