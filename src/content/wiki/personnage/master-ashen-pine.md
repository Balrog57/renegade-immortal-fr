## Background

In the past, he accidentally found the Seven-Colored Realm .

Later, he entered along with quite a few of his friends but over half of them died there. Once again, he entered the seven-colored realm and this time only he, Master Cloud Soul and Pang Decai made it out alive.

Although the seven-colored realm is an extremely dangerous place, it is also laden withs riches ripe for the taking such as thousands of origin stones that have gone untouched for centuries, among other things.

### History

Master Ashen Pine invited Wang Lin along with Master Cloud Soul, Pang Decai, Duanmu , the Battle Beast Sect 's Chen Tainjun , and Old Woman Zhao .

Inside the seven-colored realm, the six, with the exception of Master Cloud Soul who was made to teleport elsewhere by Wang Lin, defeated a rank 12 fog dragon and obtained its treasures.

Afterwards, Duanmu was captured by one of the Lost Ones while Chen Tainjun left soon after.

Master Ashen Pine then helped Wang Lin obtain tens of thousands of origin crystals by luring the fog beasts away with crushed celestial jades. Then he led the group to Sima Mo 's cultivation cave.

On the way, the group faced an ancient restriction which no one was able to break through. Finally, Wang Lin stepped up and broke through. At the same time, he acted as if he had been gravely injured in the act.

This led to Master Ashen Pine leaving him alone with Pang Decai to recover while he moved on with Old Woman Zhao. Soon after the two left, Pang Decai attacked Wang Lin but contrary to expectation Wang Lin was perfectly fine and easily turned the tables on Pang Decai. He killed Pang Decai, stole all his treasures, and soul-searched his soul which allowed him to learn the way to leave the seven-colored realm.

Meanwhile, unaware that Wang Lin was secretly following him, Master Ashen Pine led Old Woman Zhao to the main temple where he sacrificed quite a few of his items to allow her to open the portal sealing the temple. After entering the temple, he trapped Old Woman Zhao while he stole the main treasure of the temple: a jade bottle filled with black liquid.

As he was escaping, Old Woman Zhao went mad and attacked him. Just as he was about to escape the temple, Wang Lin swooped in and stole the jade bottle from him. He pursued Wang Lin and started a deadly battle with him which led to both of them being seriously injured. He even lost his body and was only left with an injured origin soul.
