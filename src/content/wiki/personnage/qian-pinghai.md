## Overview

13th geneartion Suzaku. Master of Zhuque Zi and Yunque Zi. Favored Yunque Zi but at the end Zhuque Zi took the Cultivation Planet Crystal and became the 14th Suzaku. Always hated by Zhuque Zi. Because of Zhuque Zi's hatred, his corpse sold to Corpse Sect. He is ancestor of Qian Feng .
