## Ji Du  mentions that he and the other  Ancient Clan

Grand Empyrean 's were keeping tabs on the Celestial Clan and had been researching all of their notable powerhouses, especially the rising ' White Haired Ascendant Empyrean '.

Using a unique Devil Divine Sense, he witnessed Wang Lin's breakthrough into Half Heaven Trampling and the condensing of his Grand Empyrean Sun. However, his divine sense could not escape Wang Lin 's notice and was paralyzed by a mere glance by him. Panicking, he told Chi Man to open up the Origin Array and went to retrieve the special war axe that was bestowed upon him by Gu Dao , which when combined together, could manipulate law (Possibly to the degree of those who have crossed the First Heaven Trampling Bridge).

Even though he knew that Wang Lin's power completely surpassed him, he challenged Wang Lin to a duel stating if Wang Lin could beat him, Ji Du would be appointed as the next emperor of the Ancient Shi. Before this, he intended to appoint his disciple Chi Man as the next emperor of the Ancient Shi.

After Wang Lin completely suppresses his trump card, the entire Origin Mountain and takes it for himself, he admits defeat. He appoints Ji Du as the next emperor of the Ancient Shi and allows his descendant Song Yu to marry him. He then escorts Wang Lin to the Ancient Shi Temple so he could overcome the final trials of the Ancient Clan and awaken his full Ancient power.

When the Immemorial God Realm began to open, he was present along with the rest of the chosen members of the Ancient Clan . Like the rest, he returned to the Ancient Clan when Gu Dao recalled them due to the unnaturally strong rejection force that even the Grand Empyrean 's could not handle.
