## Description

The Giant Demon Clan Ancestor was one of the most fearsome Soul Transformation cultivators in the southeastern region of Planet Suzaku , hailing from the nearly extinct and once-mighty Giant Demon Clan (巨魔族). In a desperate attempt to restore his clan’s former glory, he formed an alliance with Li Yuanfeng , the Sect Head of Snow Country , plotting to seize Wang Lin ’s body and the mysterious artifact hidden within him—the legendary Heaven Defying Bead (天逆珠). To ensure secrecy and eliminate any witnesses, he ruthlessly slaughtered those who knew of the bead’s existence, including members of his own clan and the Zhao State envoy Lin Yi . Wielding the Giant Demon Clan’s ancestral weapon, the infamous Sky-Cleaving Axe (开天巨斧)—a relic said to be stained with the blood of ancient gods—he sought to dominate the battlefield. Wang Lin called in the help of Sun Tai , which cause him to retreat in the Battle giving Wang Lin a chance to escape

Later Wang Lin returns with late stage soul formation realm and fusing with his original 3 star ancient god body he is able to kill giant demon clan ancestor.
