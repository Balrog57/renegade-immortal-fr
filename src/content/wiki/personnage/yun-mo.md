## Description

Yun Mo (云一墨) was the former Sect Master of the Soul Refining Sect , one of the most feared and powerful sects focused on soul refinement techniques. Although not much is known about his personal history, Yun Mo’s lingering influence continues through the techniques and traditions he passed down. As an earlier-generation leader of the Soul Refining Sect, he helped solidify the sect’s terrifying legacy .

His legacy influences cultivators like Du Tian and later, Wang Lin , who would eventually inherit the soul refinement techniques of the sect.

He found Du Tian has a child, he took him to the soul refining sect and accepted him as his disciple and his successor.
