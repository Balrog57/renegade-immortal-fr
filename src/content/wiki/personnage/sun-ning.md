## History

(Donghua) - Wang Lin (disguised as Xu Mu) was prevented from entering the Sun Family's treasure pavilion by Sun Feng who thought that this foreign golden-core cultivator was new here and had no idea of the treasure pavilion's rules.

Then when Wang Lin brought out a sword he infused with spatial traversing abilities, Sun Feng thought the sword was barely worth anything and attempted to drop it before Sun Ning showed up.

He was interested in the sword that Wang Lin presented, wondering if Wang Lin took his family's ancestral treasure out to sell it but ended up buying it for 3,000 celestial jades.

As he turned to return back to his duties in the treasure pavilion, Wang Lin took out another item that caught his eye, a hand-held mirror infused with heavenly inscriptions that may help him further advance his cultivation.

The cost Wang Lin quoted was 50,000 celestial jades, which would practically empty out his pockets. But if he missed this opportunity then he might regret it forever. So he purchased that item as well.

Then Wang Lin brought out his final item, a hairpin that seemed to come from a celestial immortal (an immortal inheritance of some kind) but the price was too extravagant (100,000 celestial jades).
