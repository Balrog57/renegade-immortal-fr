## Overview

Sun Ruonan is the little sister of Sun Yunshan . She was involved in a complex situation related to the Cultivation Alliance and the control exerted by a figure known as the "Ancestor".

Initially, Sun Ruonan was bewitched by the "Ancestor" and became a member of the Cultivation Alliance . After meeting her brother Sun Yunshan , she tricked him into joining this organization. When Sun Yunshan realized something was wrong and tried to escape, the "Ancestor" appeared and captured the martial uncles that came with him. These uncles had their memories wiped and became puppets, and Sun Yunshan himself had a celestial seed planted in his body, restricting his freedom and placing his life under control.

Wang Lin later intervened in this matter. He held both Sun Yunshan and Sun Ruonan in his hands and was able to remove the green lines (the restrictions) from Sun Yunshan and saved Sun Ruonan from the "Ancestor"’s control. After this, Wang Lin released the short old man who claimed to be from the Celestial Realm and left.Sun Ruonan was among three women sitting opposite the old man in the tower within the miasma, indicating she was held captive or under observation until Wang Lin ’s intervention. After her rescue, she was thrown toward Sun Yunshan , symbolizing her return to her brother and presumably freedom from control.

Sun Yunshan expressed great joy upon seeing Sun Ruonan again but also complex emotions due to the events. The removal of the restrictions was significant and was facilitated by Wang Lin , who gave Sun Yunshan a jade to delay the celestial seed's effect in emergencies.This revealed that Sun Ruonan was manipulated and used by the "Ancestor" as part of a scheme that involved controlling Sun Yunshan and his martial uncles, but thanks to Wang Lin ’s intervention, she was freed and reunited with her brother.
