### Plot

He seen again in second trial with Xu Liqing demanding the young man he bring and accuse him as the third person that mess with restrictions in that trial, unknowingly Wang Lin is the culprit. Wang Lin then release restrictions that attract sky and lightning restriction to attack both of them. With these restrictions they hurriedly escaped to the portal on top of the mountain. Later Wang Lin saw him again in third trial recovering from previous trial, seeing this Wang Lin initiated an attack with Wandering Souls he controls and threatening him to hand over his Ancient God treasures in exchange with his life. He then say the treasure is on Xu Liqing hands and give exit teleportation spell instead. Wang Lin accept the overing but didn't stop attacking him. In the brink of death he taken away by demonized Hunchback Meng and thrown away into the Blood Sea.
