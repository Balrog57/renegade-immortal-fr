## History

Wang Lin first meet him while he was trying to comprehend life and death domain as a mortal. He was one of the 4 chosen of Yunque Zi to becomes the next suzaku. He has the inheritance of a green dragon. Zhou Wutai finnaly becames the 15th Suzaku after Zhuque Zi . It was Wang Lin who decided to passed it to him.
