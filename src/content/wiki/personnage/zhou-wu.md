## Description

One of the major continents on Planet Suzaku. Where the countries Huo Fen, Country of Zhao, Xuan Wu and Tian Yi are located.

Right above this land mass is the Sea of Devils (Xiu Mo Hai) and to the upper left is the Country of Chu (Chu Guo or State of Chu).
