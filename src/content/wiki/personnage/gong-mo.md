## Background

Founded the War God Shrine. Found the Divine Path . The second year after Gong Mo found the Divine Path, there was a traitor, so the news of the Divine Path suddenly spread far and wide. Soon after, the rank 4 cultivation country, Tian Gang , arrived. After secretly talking to Gong Mo, they took it away. Gong Mo then left the War God Shrine and never came back. [ 1 ]
