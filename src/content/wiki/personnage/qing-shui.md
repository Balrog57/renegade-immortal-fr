## Background

He is an ancient Celestial Lord from the before the collapse of the Thunder Celestial Realm . Due to some reason, he went mad and killed countless celestials that included his friends, family and even his wife. Finally, he was defeated by the Celestial Emperor, Bai Fan , and regained his sanity. Ridden with grief and guilt at his actions, he attempted to destroy his origin soul and body. In order to prevent the Ji Realm from resurrecting him, he used his magical treasure, the Sealing Vortex, and sealed his origin soul and body in nine parts. Unable to watch him die, Celestial Emperor Bai Fan placed down a healing formation over the seals in hope that Qing Shui would regain his sanity one day.

Due to the seals and the healing formation, Qing Shui managed to survive the events caused by the Heavenly Madness .

In time he meets Wang Lin and believes he is the next successor to his master's technique, Call The Wind.

### Trivia

Qing Shui is one of the few cultivators who owns the Ji Realm .

Qing Shui is the prince of Clear water and has the Ji Realm. The State of Clear Water was first mentioned in relation to the Ji Realm in Chapter 105.
