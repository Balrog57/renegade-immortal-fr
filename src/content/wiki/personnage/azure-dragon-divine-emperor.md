## Personality

In the past he was noted to have been a reasonable individual. However after consuming the blood of heaven in a bid to survive his 4th Heaven's Blight, he has grown extremely arrogant and dismissive of others.

Still, however, he is shown to be a reasonable man to some degree, as while he usurped the Four Divine Sect entirely for his own, he did not harm Wang Lin nor his friends. He also appears to have faith in Wang Lin despite the strain his own actions have put on their sectoral sense of unity, as he is shown to have "acknowledged" him in some manner.

Having crowned himself Sovereign and taken full control over the entire Brilliant Void Star System, it remains to be seen whether his actions are based on covetous desire or a more benevolent motive.

### History

Sometime after Tuo Sen 's rampage, he defeated the Corpse Sect's Eight Kings and the last dregs of the shattered Cultivation Alliance to force the army of Allheaven out of Brilliant Void.
