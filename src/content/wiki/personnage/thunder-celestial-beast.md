## Appearance

It gave off a terrifying aura and was 100 feet tall. The red light from its eyes were nearly seven feet out of its eyes, and a silky fog surrounded its eyes. The Silver Horn Thunder Beast’s body was surrounded in a sphere of lightning and resembled

a qilin-like beast. It had a silver horn that slowly drilled out from its head under the effects of the lightning.

### Background

When Wang Lin reached the Ascendant stage, he was able to create seals on his God Slaying War Chariot. As a result, the beast inside the God Slaying War Chariot evolved. Wang Lin was initially able to put four seals on the God Slaying War Chariot.
