## Properties

When introduced to dew or water the Bead transforms it into a spiritual liquid that can hasten cultivation and also extend one's youth and lifespan(for this reason, Wang Lin still appears young regardless of how long he spends inside the Bead's Dream Space).

After fully absorbing the water element the Bead provides Wang Lin access to a dream space, where a cultivator can practice while time has slowed down. It must absorb the essence of the five elements of metal, wood, water, fire, and earth before it would identify an owner.

In the beginning, when 10 clouds (essence of Water) had formed on the Stone Bead, the time dilation rate in the dream space is 1:10 {where one hour in the real world correlates to 10 hours in the dream space} and he can remain within for 50 hours. It can only reliably be used after a five hour break between uses.

After Wang Lin 's Original body was destroyed, the Heaven Defying Bead melted and fused with his soul.

The more the essence of the five elements is completed, the more the Time Ratio, and the quality of the spiritual liquid increases as well. After completing 3 of the elements, Wang Lin finds that the spiritual liquid is very similar to celestial spiritual energy.

After gaining all 5 elements, the Bead opens a doorway leading to a mysterious space, but this is guarded by a mystical hand which will attempt to crush the Bead's user if they try to enter the doorway. This doorway leads to the Dao Realm, and entering it allows the user to gain a tremendous enlightenment.

The Bead's symbols then change to Yin and Yang, and it needs to absorb Extreme Yin and Extreme Yang to evolve further. When given sufficient Yang, it reaches out with the same hand from the doorway and can overpower Nirvana Shatterer Cultivators with ease to take their Energy for its own sake.
