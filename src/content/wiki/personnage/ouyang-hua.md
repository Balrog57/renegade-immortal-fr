## Appearance

A white-haired old man with a black cane.

### Personality

Ouyang Hua is described as having a bad temper, particularly during his alchemy refining work, but his obsession with alchemy and dedication make him likely to reach the Soul Formation stage. Despite his age and initial cultivation limitations, he grows into a respected and intelligent leader, deeply loyal and grateful to Wang Lin, who he views as both a savior and ancestor. Ouyang Hua also learns to work with demonic spiritual energy and demon jades under Wang Lin’s instruction, further cementing his role in the tribe’s growth and defense.

### History

Ouyang Hua is an important elder and key figure within the Soul Refining Tribe , a valley tribe protected by a powerful formation that has existed for countless years. The tribe itself has largely forgotten its origins, though Ouyang Hua explains that his ancestors were settled in the valley by Huang Long long ago. The tribe has lived in relative isolation, with the valley functioning as a quiet village before Wang Lin ’s arrival. Most villagers are mortals, and while Ouyang Hua possesses a thin, silk-like aura of demonic spiritual energy in his dantian, his blocked meridians limit circulation and cultivation. Despite this, he controls the protective formation around the valley and can create illusionary bodies.

When Wang Lin arrives, he forcibly helps Ouyang Hua by opening a meridian in his dantian using a direct, painful method. This intervention dramatically increases Ouyang Hua’s cultivation from being stuck at rank 1 to reaching two stars of demonic spiritual energy. Deeply grateful, Ouyang Hua kneels and kowtows to Wang Lin, addressing him as "Supreme Celestial." Following this, he is entrusted with learning and teaching the Soul Refining Spell, which he pledges to pass on to the villagers, greatly enhancing their cultivation abilities.

Under Wang Lin’s guidance, Ouyang Hua leads the tribe in cultivating the soul refining spell and preparing for combat against enemy tribes. He plays a key role in battles, refining the souls of the dead and supporting the defense of the valley. His status within the tribe grows as Wang Lin returns and is honored as an ancestral figure, in part due to a stone statue carved in Wang Lin’s likeness. Ouyang Hua greets him with profound respect and excitement. As a reward for his centuries of dedication and hard work, Wang Lin later gives Ouyang Hua a complete low-quality celestial spell called Soul Refining Ascension, elevating his power despite his abnormal cultivation state.

After Wang Lin departs to attend to other matters, Ouyang Hua continues to lead the tribe, implementing Wang Lin’s orders to disperse members to surrounding tribes to spread cultivation knowledge and resist memory-wiping potions. He is involved in preparations for attacking a cave and leads the tribe through a period of increased aggression and expansion to gain higher-level chants, marking the beginning of a new crusade for the Soul Refining Tribe
