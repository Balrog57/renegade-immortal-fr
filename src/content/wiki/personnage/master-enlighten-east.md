## History

He attacked Master Hong Shan after the latter appeared and severely wounded Esteemed Nan Zhao . He banded together with Fire Sparrow Clan Ancestor , two other Nirvana Void cultivators and, even, Esteemed Nan Zhao to attack him together.

However, Master Hong Shan managed to stall the five until he fell in Grandmaster Yun Luo 's trap and was sneak-attacked by the Sixth Concubine . Furthermore, Ice Boy appeared to continue holding him back. This allowed the Fire Sparrow Clan Ancestor, Esteemed Nan Zhao and a Nirvana void cultivator from Bai Lei Clan to leave the encirclement to kill Wang Lin. However, this only resulted in the Fire Sparrow Clan Ancestor and Esteemed Nan Zhao's death while the Nirvana void cultivator from Bai Lei Clan escaped in terror.

Just as the Outer Realm's morale was at an all time low, reinforcements from the Cloud Sea Star System and Summoned River Star System appeared which caused their morale to reach rock-bottom and forced the Outer Realm's Third-Step cultivators to retreat.
