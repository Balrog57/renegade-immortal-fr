## Background

Sometime in the past he and his brothers, excluding Fourth Uncle , schemed against Wang Tian Shui to successfully steal his portion of their inheritance.

### Book One

He is seen at Wang Tian Shui's home, mocking his brother on how his son, Wang Lin , will fail the Heng You Sect's tests, while also remarking how talented his own Wang Zhou is. Sure enough, Wang Lin does fail and is met with Wang Tian Shan's public mockery and scorn alongside the entire Wang Ckan and their neighbours. When Wang Lin runs away, his brother abd Fourth Uncle force him to reluctantly ask the Heng Yue Sect to look for him.

### Book Two

He is unsurmoniously slaughtered alongside the entire Wang Clan by Teng Huayuan in an act of revenge against Wang Lin for killing his grandson.
