## Background

In the past, he was greatly helped by the previous Lord of the Sealed Realm .

He created the Cultivation Alliance not to control the Brilliant Void, but to allow the cultivators to grow stronger in its cruel environment. He did so to prepare them for the war against the Outer Realm .

After nearing the end of his lifespan, he decided to fuse his origin soul with the planets sealed inside the Cultivation Alliance. He was successful in this endeavor as this allowed him to remain alive and reach the mid-stage of Spirit Void .

### Book Ten

He awoke after Wang Lin stormed the Cultivation Alliance. He was ready to battle Wang Lin before he heard of Master Zhong Xuan 's traitorous acts from the latter. He asked Zhang Xi if this was true but the latter hesitated which only served to strengthen his suspicions. In his rage, he killed Zhang Xi and soul-searched the third Head Elder beside Master Zhong Xuan. Finally, under the pressure of Master Long Pan's gaze, Master Zhong Xuan was forced to confess to his crimes.

Disappointed and regretful, Master Long Pan plead mercy for Master Zhong Xuan from Wang Lin. In exchange, he pledged to cut off his vitality, burn his origin soul to forcefully boost his cultivation to the early-stage of Arcane Void , kill all the Outer Realm cultivators in the Sealed Realm , destroy the remaining Seven-Colored Realms , and take back the Summoned River Star System in exchange for Master Zhong Xuan’s life.

A few decades later, Master Long Pan was fighting against the Heaven Punishment Palace Lord Qing Jiuyan inside Summoned River's Seven-Colored Realm to free Master Hong Shan who trapped there. He was struggling until Wang Lin appeared along with Qing Shui and freed Master Hong Shan from the Dao Fruit. Moreover, they also managed to capture the Heaven Punishment Palace Lord Qing Jiuyan.

### Book Eleven

72 years after Wang Lin was ambushed by Sovereign's Fishing the Moon in the Well, Master South Cloud led the remnants of the Alliance to battle against the Outer Realm . The battleground was near Planet Suzaku where the Inner Realm cultivators fought to defend the Lord of the Sealed Realm's homeland.

The battle was in a deadlock with the Outer Realm's Zhou Jin and Ice Boy facing off against Master South Cloud , Situ Nan , Master Lu Fu and Master Long Pan until the Heaven Master Void God 's avatar appeared. This immediately tilted the balance of power against the Inner Realm cultivators and forced them to retreat inside the formation of Planet Suzaku. When all hope was lost, Wang Lin triggered the Great Void Gate and appeared to lift the spirits of the weary Inner Realm cultivators.
