## Overview

Cao Yidou (曹一斗) was a cultivator from Tian Yun Wang Lin found from the Corpse Sect who became bound to Wang Lin during the latter’s journey to Planet Tian Yun.

Wang Lin made a deal with Cao Yiduo to become his slave for 100 years, promising to gift him a Soul Transformation body after that period. Cao Yiduo agreed, despite the risks, because obtaining a Soul Transformation body was extremely valuable and rare. Wang Lin needed Cao Yiduo's knowledge about planet Tian Yun and intended to use him as a guide. After receiving Cao Yiduo's soul blood, Wang Lin put him inside a soul flag to keep him under control, then proceeded to head toward the Heavenly Fate Sect on Tian Yun, guided by Cao Yiduo's information
