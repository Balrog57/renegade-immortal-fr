## Background

He was asleep inside the statue at the heart of the forest ruin until Wu Yu 's Nascent Soul attempted to possess him, an act which woke him from his slumber.

### Book Two

After a brief confrontation, he ultimatey befriends Wang Lin and gives him an Unusual stone in exchange for his formation. After seeing Wang Lin Cultivate Yin Qi during his stay in the ruin, Adai leads him to Wu Yu. They are attacked by Amu , which prompts Wu Yu to send the two via portal to the Corpse Sect for help.

### Book Five

There are strong hints dropped that he is the young master of the Forsaken Immortal Clan on Planet Suzaku .
