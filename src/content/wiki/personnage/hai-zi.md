## Empyrean Exalt  Hai Zi  is one of the  Celestial

Empyrean Exalts , and is the disciple and daughter of Grand Empyrean

She is currently the only remaining living member of Immortal Astral Continent's Mourning-Death Clan .

### History

She first met Wang Lin when the latter was trying to obtain Wood Essence.

As a way to bypass the seal restricting the Mourning-Death Clan, she was born between a female Mourning-Death member and Grand Empyrean Jiu Di, resulting in a Mourning-Death Soul but an Immortal body. As such, she was able to bypass the restrictions and roam freely.

She is also the true Celestial Imperial Teacher. Born as part of a plan to free the Mourning-Death Clan from the Celestial Clan's Royal Family, her memories were sealed until the death of the fake Imperial Teacher during the confrontation between Wang Lin and the Celestial Emperor Lian Daozhen.
