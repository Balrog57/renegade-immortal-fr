## History

He was previously possessed by the servant of a celestial from the Thunder Celestial Realm . He was freed from his prior condition after Wang Lin caused the death of the aforementioned servant. Moreover, he was also freed from the slave imprint that was forced upon generations of his bloodline. In return, he shared all his knowledge about restrictions with Wang Lin and promised to take him back to his clan to impart the Annihilation Restriction upon him along with giving Wang Lin half of his heart of restriction which is necessary for one to truly master the Annihilation Restriction.

He entered the storage space of a Celestial along with Wang Lin where he found nine quasi-celestial short swords. He helped Wang Lin set up the ambush against Blood Ancestor but left before Blood Ancestor arrived to keep his involvement a secret.

After parting ways with Wang Lin, he was chased by a young master of the Yao Family . He, along with his cousin and his subordinate, toyed with Li Yuan for 13 days until he decided to soul-search him to learn more about the nine quasi-celestial short swords. However, Wang Lin arrived just before he could proceed with the soul-search and killed the three.

The two then went inside a sealed entrance inside the ruined marketplace of the Thunder Celestial Realm and met the sealed Qing Shui . Qing Shui attempted to charm Li Yuan into committing suicide and giving him his origin energy but the latter was saved by Wang Lin.

After escaping from Qing Shui, the two reached the Celestial Pavilion. There, Li Yuan guarded outside while Wang Lin went inside to try his luck at getting a celestial spell.

After coming out, they noticed a mad, now un-sealed, Qing Shui rampaging around. Wang Lin threw Li Yuan out of the Thunder Celestial Realm before Qing Shui closed the gate.

Later, Wang Lin arrived at Planet Annihilation and Li Yuan gave him half of his Annihilation Heart.
