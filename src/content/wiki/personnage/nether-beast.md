**Catégories :** Beasts

## Personality

They have a Timid nature that makes them afraid of everything, even if it is weaker. They don't really have a great mind, they live their life in a dazed state of mind.

### Background

Nether Beast is the Strongest Beast in the world because of their strong body and 2 innate spells, Ethereal Spell and Devour Spell.

Their aura helps them stop other living beings from coming close to them and even a Ji Qiong , the King of Fierce Beast, are afraid of them.

Their strength comes from their inner realm where the time flows differently from outside. There was an adult Nether Beast chased to a corner and was forced to Devour a Star System with a time flow of 100 million years to kill all his pursuer.
