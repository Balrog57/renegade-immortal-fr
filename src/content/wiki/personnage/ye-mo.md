## Background

He is a member of the Ancient Clan who participated during the Heavenly Madness events that took place in Wang Lin's Cave World, he died during the war but before that he had scattered 3000 drops of blood, his body was scattered all over the Cave World. He's a descendant from the Ancient Dao Country's Imperial Family but his bloodline is only slightly superior than the common Ancient Clan bloodline.

Wang Lin was able to find his legacy at the Ancient Graveyard located at the Ancient Star Territory of the Outer Realm . Warning Spoiler

He was the only member of his imperial branch to become an Ancient Order member, possessing 27 Stars. After his fall, they continued to have a minimal presence in the Dao Clan until Luo Chen became the next Ancient Dao Emperor due to Wang Lin and Xuan Luo influence.

### Book Eleven

After Wang Lin gained all eight drops of Ye Mo's Origin Soul Blood, Ye Mo attempted to possess Wang Lin's body and revive. Normally, this would've been simple since the person he would be possessing would be his successor. However, Wang Lin was different. He had obtained the recognition of the Ancient Ancestor and the Ancient Ancestor’s soul blood. As a result, he was an existence even more noble than Ye Mo, so Ye Mo couldn’t succeed.

Later, his heart tried to resist being absorbed by Wang Lin, but Wang Lin finally convinced Ye Mo to yield by promising to find his left eye.
