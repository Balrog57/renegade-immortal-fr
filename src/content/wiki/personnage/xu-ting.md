## History

He participated in the Thunder Celestial Temple 's 108 Celestial Titles trial and took second place in the human, earth, and heaven trials after Wang Lin . He also lost to Wang Lin in the one line to heaven trial so he believes Wang Lin to be his rival as he has never lost to someone his age before. But even more so, he has a dense desire to kill Wang Lin as the latter overshadowed his glory, multiple times.

He accepted the baptism of the Celestial Bestowment pool and gave up his domain so that he could grow more powerful and gain the upper hand against Wang Lin.
