## Background

In the past, he followed Master Ashen Pine into the Seven-Colored Realm the latter had accidentally found.

He entered along with quite a few of his friends but over half of them died there. Once again, he entered the seven-colored realm and this time only he, Master Cloud Soul and Master Ashen Pine made it out alive.

Although the seven-colored realm is an extremely dangerous place, it is also laden withs riches ripe for the taking such as thousands of origin stones that have gone untouched for centuries, among other things.

### History

He participated in Master Ashen Pine private auction, same as Wang Lin . There, he was surprised by the amount of celestial jades Wang Lin had and made plans to rob him soon after he left the city. However, after seeing Wu Qing, who had the same cultivation as himself, being effortlessly killed by Wang Lin, he was extremely terrified of the Wang Lin.

Master Ashen Pine invited him along with Master Cloud Soul, Wang Lin , Duanmu , the Battle Beast Sect 's Chen Tainjun , and Old Woman Zhao .

Inside the seven-colored realm, the six, with the exception of Master Cloud Soul who was made to teleport elsewhere by Wang Lin, defeated a rank 12 fog dragon and obtained its treasures.

Afterwards, Duanmu was captured by one of the Lost Ones while Chen Tainjun left soon after.

On the way, the group faced an ancient restriction which no one was able to break through. Finally, Wang Lin stepped up and broke through. At the same time, he acted as if he had been gravely injured in the act.

This led to Master Ashen Pine leaving Wang Lin alone with him to recover while he moved on with Old Woman Zhao. Soon after the two left, he attacked Wang Lin but contrary to expectation Wang Lin was perfectly fine and easily turned the tables on him. Wang Lin killed him, stole all his treasures, and soul-searched his soul which allowed him to learn the way to leave the seven-colored realm.
