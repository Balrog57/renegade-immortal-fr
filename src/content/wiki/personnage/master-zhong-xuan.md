## Background

In the past, he used Ling Tianhou and Grey-Robe to cause multiple cracks to All-Seer 's Dao Heart after seeing his unprecedented talent; he was afraid that in a matter of decades All-Seer would surpass him and rule over the Cultivation Alliance. A cracked Dao Heart resulted in the All-Seer having to put seals upon his cultivation and devouring his disciples to make progress in his cultivation.

Sometime later, he drove out Master Flamespark from the Alliance Star System . Master Flamespark still holds a deep grudge against the Alliance Star System as he was thrown out of it by Master Zhong Xuan . It led him to scheme for thousands years in order to start a war between the 2 star systems.

{This grudge is likely related to Master Zhong Xuan doing something less than stellar towards Master Flamespark's Dao Partner however this is just speculation as Er Gen just glosses over the matter.}

### History

He trapped Qing Shui inside the Seven-Colored Realm located in the Alliance headquarters after the latter stormed the Alliance headquarters to uncover the secret behind the Heavenly Madness.

Many years later, after the First Battle of the Third Sealed-Outer Realm War, he defected to the Sovereign Council after seeing the Outer Realm 's might. He allowed the Outer Realm full use of the Seven-Colored Realm located in the Alliance headquarters and also suppressed the cultivators of the Alliance Star System.

A few decades later, Wang Lin returned from the belly of the Nether Beast and stormed the Alliance headquarters. He also awoke Master Long Pan and informed him of Master Zhong Xuan's traitorous acts. Under the pressure of Master Long Pan's gaze, Master Zhong Xuan was forced to confess to his crimes.

Disappointed and regretful, Master Long Pan plead mercy for Master Zhong Xuan from Wang Lin. In exchange, he pledged to cut off his vitality, burn his origin soul to forcefully boost his cultivation to the early-stage of Arcane Void , kill all the Outer Realm cultivators in the Sealed Realm , destroy the remaining Seven-Colored Realms, and take back the Summoned River Star System in exchange for Master Zhong Xuan’s life.
