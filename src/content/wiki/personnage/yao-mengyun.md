## History

When she was young, her mother, the most talented person of her generation, was devoured by the ancient lamp 's spirit under Blood God 's control. Afterwards, her mother gave birth to her elder sister named Yao Bingyun and her.

Later, Blood God ordered Yao Yun to bring the two sisters to his hall and declared the two were to be refined into the ancient lamp's spirit. In the end, only she was refined while her elder sister was spared.
