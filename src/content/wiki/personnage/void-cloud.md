## Background

In the past, he went to the Northern Continent and entered Grand Empyrean

### History

The Green Devil Continent's Three Great Sects, Devil Dao Sect , Poison Devil Sect , and Wu Ji Sect , sent him to revert the half Celestial Pill that created the Pill Sea back into a pill and use it to break the barrier dividing the two continents as it would also allow his reputation to skyrocket and allow him to enter the Celestial Imperial Capital in the Central Continent to obtain the qualification to enter the ancestral temple.

He appeared before Wang Lin when the latter was working on breaking through the formation set by the Green Devil Continent to trap and kill the Heavenly Bull Continent cultivators inside the Pill Sea. Wang Lin managed to escape, however, at the cost of Yi Si losing one of his arms. Still, Void Cloud's avatar was also damaged quite severely.

However, even though Wang Lin had managed to escape, he could not calm down his heart from the loss her suffered. So, he sent off Vermillion Bird , Black Tortoise , and White Tiger to the Heavenly Bull Continent before returning to the Pill Sea. There, he infiltrated the Green Devil Continent's headquarters and killed everyone there. He also stole the compass that controlled the restriction upon the Pill Sea.

He then stalked Void Cloud while he attempted to refine the Pill Sea. As the sea had contracted down to 1,000 feet, Wang Lin suddenly attacked with the Blood Slaughter Sword. However, Void Cloud managed to counter it. But, just as he countered the strike, Wang Lin's Fire Essence True Body suddenly attacked with the sword which resulted in Void Cloud losing control of the Pill and it exploded right then and there.

In the explosion, every Green Devil Continent cultivators present died as did Void Cloud's avatar. Also, the two continents were both damaged by the explosion.
