## Overview

The Scattered Devil (散魔, Sàn Mó) is a powerful ancient devil spirit in East Demon Spirit Sea. It originates from the lineage of ancient devils. The entity embodies chaotic devilish energy and black devil flames, radiating an oppressive and violent aura. It usually appears as dense black mist that condenses into a gigantic single-horned devil figure with glowing red eyes and a manic, piercing laughter.

Wang Lin spent more than a century attempting to refine the Scattered Devil into his second origin soul. Although he never fully succeeded, he gained partial control by imprinting a powerful spell left by the Ancient Demon Bei Lou onto its soul. This imprint suppresses its strength and prevents it from escaping completely.
