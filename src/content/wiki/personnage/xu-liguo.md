## Background

Xu Liguo initially had a close relationship with the Corpse Sect , as it suffered a lethal wound and required the services of this sect, which was precisely to offer the corpses of other cultivators to extend life. When Wang Lin found him, he was using the body of an Foundation Establishment as storage for his nascent soul, so he was very weak and easily trapped.

### Etymology

Etymology and meaning of the Chinese name Xu Liguo (徐立国) in detail:

Commonly means slow, gentle, calm, serene.

Also a very old and prestigious Chinese surname, historically linked to the State of Xu (徐国) during the Zhou dynasty, which was located in present-day Jiangsu and Anhui provinces.

Symbolically, “徐” conveys a sense of grace, composure, and steadiness.

People with the surname Xu are often thought of as gentle and steady, embodying a patient and cultivated temperament.

To stand, to establish, to set up, to found, to rise.

In names, it symbolizes independence , ambition , uprightness , and strength of will .

Common in compound names implying “to establish something important” — like virtue, family, or nation.

“立” in names reflects someone who is upright and determined, one who stands firm in principles or goals.

Often represents patriotism, unity, or greatness.

In personal names, it conveys a wish for loyalty to one’s nation, or metaphorically, the ideal of building or protecting a great domain.

“国” gives a grand, noble air — a person destined to contribute to society or uphold national ideals.

### History

Xu Liguo is a Nascent Soul cultivator from the Tian Gang Sect who was refined into a devil by Wang Lin and becomes his servant. Later, he becomes the Sword Spirit of the Rain Celestial Sword when Wang Lin receives its inheritance from Zhou Yi .

When Wang Lin sends the souls of those in the cave world to be reincarnated in the Immortal Astral Continent , Xu Liguo is one of them. Becoming human again, he reincarnates in the Celestial Clan and is seen in the Central Continent , serving Lian Daofei . After meeting with Wang Lin again he serves him once more, but is released by him at the end of the novel.

### Personality

Shameless and silver-tongued, he is always ready to flatter Wang Lin with the hope of reaping good rewards. He is also very treacherous and vengeful, always seeking and debating an opportunity to betray and kill Wang Lin . However, these plans usually fail miserably and has him begging for his life on a constant basis.

He also believes that enjoying life is essential. Women or Men, he wants them all to serve him (well, only the prettiest ones). Being acclaimed and praised is, for Xu Liguo, as important as showing off his status of a devil under a devilishly powerful fiend .
