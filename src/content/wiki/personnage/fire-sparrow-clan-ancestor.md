## History

He kidnapped Liu Jinbiao from the Fallen Land after Wang Lin planted some of his own Vermillion Bird blood inside him. This led him to falsely believe Liu Jinbiao to the be the Sixth Generation Vermillion Bird Divine Emperor .

He handed Liu Jinbiao over to the Third Generation Vermillion Bird Divine Emperor who is one of the Sovereign 's clones. This allowed Wang Lin to infiltrate the Fire Sparrow Clan. He teleported there through his connection to Liu Jinbiao and severely wounded the Sovereign's clones by using the Vermillion Bird feather given to him by the Vermillion Bird Ancestor and the Realm Burning Umbrella spell passed down to him by the Second Generation Vermillion Bird Divine Emperor .

Afterwards, the Fire Sparrow Clan Ancestor took part in the war against the Sealed Realm . After Master Hong Shan appeared, saved Wang Lin, and severely wounded Esteemed Nan Zhao , the Fire Sparrow Clan Ancestor along with Master Enlighten East , two other Nirvana Void cultivators and, even, Esteemed Nan Zhao attacked him together.

For some time Master Hong Shan managed to stall the five until he fell in Grandmaster Yun Luo 's trap and was sneak-attacked by the Sixth Concubine. Furthermore, one of the Five Masters appeared to continue holding him back. This allowed the Fire Sparrow Clan Ancestor to leave and confront Wang Lin who was rushing to kill Grandmaster Yun Luo. However, the former was easily sliced in half by the Heaven-Splitting Axe in Wang Lin's possession.
