## Overview

A character who appears as a woman wearing armor, with a tattoo covering much of her body, though parts of it are obscured by the armor. She is associated with a powerful demonic wind and is identified as a Demon Guardian, specifically “Demon Guardian Wind Song” of Scattered Devil Tai Jia. Her combat abilities revolve around wind and weapon techniques, including the use of a silver dagger and the ability to transform into a cold breeze for rapid, elusive attacks.

She was sent together with De Gui to kill Wang Lin after he directly attacked Devil Tower, the place where the Scattered Devil resides. During her confrontation with Wang Lin, she fights using these techniques but is ultimately forced to retreat after being struck by Wang Lin’s Soul Lasher whip and a powerful palm strike imbued with slaughter energy. Her eyes reveal shock and fear, and in that moment she no longer intends to kill, only to flee as quickly as possible. However, Wang Lin refuses to let her escape and uses the Celestial World Hand Seal , a giant hand that descends from the sky and kills her
