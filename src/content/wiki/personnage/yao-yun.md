## History

In the past, his elder brother, Blood Ancestor 's father, escaped the Allheaven Star System for the Alliance Star System . At the time he was not able to understand the reason behind his brother's actions but afterwards when his father, Blood God , devoured his elder sister and further refined his niece, Yao Mengyun , into the ancient lamp 's spirit, he understood it all.

Afterwards, Blood God poured a demon-ling inside Yao Yun to control him after he tried to rebel and save his two nieces, Yao Mengyun and Yao Bingyun .
