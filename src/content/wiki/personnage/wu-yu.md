## Background

According to himself at least, 300 years ago he came to the Forest Ruins to use the Yin Qi there as a means of breaking through to the middle stages of Nascent Soul, however, his Corpse Puppet, who he calls Amu , rebelled and escaped his control.

100 years later his injuries healed and he went to retrieve Amu , only to find the Corpse Puppet had reached the Middle stages of Nascent Soul and was too strong. He abandoned his body and hid his Nascent Soul inside the statue at the heart of the ruin. There he found Adai , sleeping, and tried to possess his body. While this attempt failed, it did cause Adai to think they were connected.

### Book Two

His abandoned body turned into a Zombie which possesses a Green Core and a consciousness all its own. This Zombie was discovered by Wang Lin who used it to help bring down Teng Li . Two years later, his Corpse Puppet, Amu , wakes and uses the Zombie to track him down.
