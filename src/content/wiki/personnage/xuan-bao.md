## Appearance

He is slightly thin and wears white but this makes him seems like a celestial. His eyes are bright and seemed to be filled with endless depth. He gives off an air of a refined elderly scholar.

### Background

He is a celestial from the Rain Celestial Realm and the junior-disciple-brother of Tian Bao , the creator of the God-Slaying War Chariot.

### History

Xuan Bao appeared during the war between the Alliance and Allheaven Star System after the death of Ji Xiantian . He, singlehandedly, offset the disadvantageous position of the Alliance army by severely wounding the Moongazer Serpent.
