## Personality

He is extremely cold, vicious and utterly unfettered, and as such is usually suppressed by the Restriction and Absolute Beginning Essences.

Wang Lin used his Flowing Time spell to reverse time and send his Slaughter avatar countless years into the past. This avatar was tasked with imitating Wang Lin's life and using its dream dao to divinate and calculate events in Wang Lin's life, essentially acting with autonomy and freedom. This explains certain confusions about Wang Lin's "previous life," which was actually this Slaughter avatar sent back in time to alter events
