## Background

In the past, Master Hollow Wind attempted to kidnap Ling'er , his granddaughter, and use her as a cultivation incubator as she has a water spirit body. However, he dreaded the master behind the Three Chen Brothers.

### History

He attempted to embarrass Wang Lin as he had unwittingly spread his divine sense over the city. However, Wang Lin soon turned the tables on him and injured him. Three Chen Brothers all came to apologize and Wang Lin accepted their apology. Afterwards, Master Yi Chen invited Wang Lin to tea and he introduced his granddaughter, Ling'er, to him.

Soon after, All-Seer attacked Wang Lin because he took the Extreme Yang from the dying Bai Wei . Master Yi Chen, along with the City Lord of Ghost Eye City, went close to witness their battle but two were pushed back and injured from the shockwaves of Wang Lin using the Summon the Rain crystal Qing Shui gave him.

Afterwards, he met Wang Lin when the latter was passing through his home planet after subduing Master Hollow Wind. Wang Lin accepted Master Yi Chen's invitation to stay on Planet Blue Spirit.
