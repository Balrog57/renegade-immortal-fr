## Background

Long Daozi is a disciple of Celestial King Carefree that was punished by Celestial Emperor Bai Fan for breaking celestial law (trying to find clues about Heaven-Avoiding Coffin for his master). His master plead for him and Bai Fan agreed under the condition that he could survive the Celestial Emperor's Call The Wind celestial spell. At the last moment when his entire body collapsed and his bones dissipated, his master can only save his skull and his origin soul and put the two together to guard a treasure from Ancient Celestial Realm called Heaven-Avoiding Coffin. The coffin contains the body of his son and a trace of his son's soul. According to his master, he can also borrow the power of the coffin to reform his body and recover his cultivation.

### Appearance

After being hit by Bai Fan 's Call The Wind, Long Daozi only have his head intact and the rest of his body is gone from the power of the celestial spell. When Wang Lin first met him after escaping from the body of a Nether Beast alongside the enslaved member of Tattoo Clan , his body under the large black robe still only consist of white bones. He managed to recover his body after staying inside Heaven-Avoiding Coffin for a short moment, but the recovered body shattered the moment he came out.
