## Personality

Within the cave world he was portrayed with a mind filled with avarice and lust.

After reincarnating in the Immortal Astral Continent , he maintains his greedy and scamming personality. However, it is shown he also has a good heart as he helped mortal children with meager talents get into sects albeit to receive rewards from those sects.

### Background

It is unknown what cultivation Liu Jinbiao had before submerging himself within his deception, but it is inferred that he was at least Heaven Trampling.

As the cave world's Liu Jinbiao, he found the Sealed Realm Jade while escaping from some people he had scammed.

His dream is for his dao of deception to be recognized as a grand dao, as well as to reach its peak, a dream that stems from his original body and was carried on to his dao of deception avatar.

### History

He used the Sealed Realm Jade to extort sects that don't have Nirvana Shatterer cultivators.

Wang Lin used All-Seer 's clairvoyance ability to divine that he could only survive from Daoist Water with the help of someone named Liu Jinbiao. Right after he did this, Liu Jinbiao's clone stormed the Origin Sect and attempted to extort them which allowed Wang Lin to locate his true body. After a short battle, Wang Lin took the Sealed Realm Jade from Liu Jinbiao and sealed his origin soul along with Xu Liguo .

### Book 12

Liu Jinbiao was reincarnated in the North of the Eastern Continent of the Immortal Astral Continent . He grew up in a small village and left at the age of 10 and took the name of Jin Baiozi. He had a inherent skillset to deceive people and used it to gain fame as a celestial. He would go to a mortal village and proclaim himself as a celestial and the mortal people would worship him, hoping that he could take their children away and make them into celestial. His slogan was "Once one becomes a celestial, even chickens and dogs can rise to the heavens!" When Wang Lin was passing through the Eastern Continent toward the Dong Lin Sect , he noticed a familiar aura and it turned out to be Liu Jinbiao. Liu Jinbiao was using his art of deception on a mortal village and took away some children with the promise of making them celestials. Wang Lin decided to follow them to see what Liu Jinbiao was up to. After Liu Jinbiao took the children away, he revealed to them that he was a conman. He confessed that he could teach them the Dao of Deception in order to get into the Great Sage Sect to potentially become a real celestial. As a result of "recruiting" these children into the sect, he would get various materials from the sect. However, he didn't force the children and gave them a choice. If they wanted to enter the Great Sage Sect, he would teach them the Dao of Deception, and if they didn't he would erase their memory and send them back home.

"Talent is not important. What’s important is for your heart to be determined!" -Liu Jinbiao
