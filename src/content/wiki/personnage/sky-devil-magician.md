## Plot

In Land of Ancient Gods third trial Xu Liqing meet his master again, apparently got revived and demonized as Demon God Tu Si . The truth is his death was planned all along, and actually that is his third time he come to Land of the Ancient God . When he first arrive at fourth realm thousands year ago he won an inheritance treasure golden arm bone and realize that this place contains unimaginable fluctuations of desires. He decide to leave using the arm bone. He then spend years to studying desires until he created the Mysterious Sky Devil Cultivation Method. On the second time he came here, he realize the fluctuation became even larger, he also starting to see cultivators that turned into a demon. After this he make a plan, taking Xu Liqing as disciple to cultivate Mysterious Sky Devil Method, bring him to Land of Ancient God and handed him the inheritance treasure before death, and wait him back to this place again so he can absorb the Ancient God's desires in fourth realm and finally be a sacrifice to open a tunnel that lead to Ancient God's inheritance of knowledge. In the end, he successfully obtained the inheritance crystal albeit only a piece of it and got stopped before he can absorb the knowledge by Duo Mu. Unfortunately all his painstaking plans got crushed, as the crystal turn into pieces by that person. He is then got dragged away by him to search a soul devourer. Unknown to them the place when the crystal got shattered, crystal shards start to reform into a whole of crystal again. Seeing this, Wang Lin take out the crystal for himself.
