## Personality

He seems to have a knack for annoying people as he can talk endlessly for days.

### History

When Wang Lin came to the southern realm to complete for a spot in the Thunder Celestial Temple , Nangong Han was the only person able to fight him. However, because Wang Lin was able to use Spatial Bending, Nangong Han wasn't able to to beat him. Nangong Han later because obsessed with Wang Lin and often times bothered him, hoping to gain some enlightenment on Spatial Bending.
