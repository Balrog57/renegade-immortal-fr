## Background

During the war against the Cultivation Alliance and the Brilliant Void Cultivation Sect , the White Tiger Divine Emperor fought bravely but died in battle; his death, Black Tortoise Divine Emperor 's betrayal and Azure Dragon Divine Emperor 's disappearance ultimately led to the Four Divine Sect 's defeat and decline.
