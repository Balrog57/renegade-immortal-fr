## Personality

On the surface, he was gentle, but in truth, he felt contempt toward Su Dao. He ignored all the people bullying him and maybe even promoted it, indirectly.

### Background

When Su Dao was a disciple of the Dong Lin Sect, he was very timid and weak-willed. This resulted in a lot of people bullying him. This was even more so due the fact that he was very talented which attracted the jealousy of many.

Zhou Li knew that Su Dao was weak-willed so he frightened him with a mean glare before the latter took the test for determining the inheritor to the sect's Cloud Dao Heavenly Art . Due to Zhou Li’s cold gaze, Su Dao's mind wasn’t focused, his speed slowed down and he failed in the test. Thus, he was given the inferior Seven-Colored Celestial Art to cultivate while Zhou Li obtained the Cloud Dao Heavenly Art .

Five months later, Zhou Li placed 9th in the Inter-Sect Competition and became a dao pair with Qin Mei, whom Su Dao had a crush on.

After Su Dao completed his dao, he captured Zhou Li and cruelly murdered him.
