## Appearance

He is a slightly handsome man but he has a evil look to him. As the name of his sect suggests, he wears purple clothing.

### Personality

He has a wicked personality as he is willing to use Lu Yanfei as a cultivation furnace and kill her right after he has stolen her cultivation.

### Background

In the past, he attempted to strong-arm Lu Yanfei into a becoming his cultivation partner. However, his true intentions were to use her as a cultivation furnace.

He went as far as storming the Origin Sect to kidnap her but he and his attendant, Song Wude , were kicked out by the previous Ancestor of the Origin Sect.

### History

He had Song Wude attack the Origin Sect after hearing the news of its Old Ancestor passing away only to end up offending Wang Lin and dying a meaningless death.
