## Method

Cultivators at the Nascent Soul stage begin to assimilate themselves into mortal life so they can find an epiphany for the domain they seek. After Soul Transformation, to improve their domain, the cultivator can choose to further comprehend the domain by themselves or devour another cultivator's domain with similar attributes. The first method ensures stability while improving the domain. The second method has a risk of mutating or changing the domain they originally cultivated into a fusion of two different domains.

### Completion

There are two known paths when the domain reaches completion, the first one is Wang Lin's case, he still retains completed domains after they are completed while he starts to comprehend a new domain. The other case is Liu Mei with her Ruthless Domain which completely evolved into a higher version of that domain after it reached completion.

At the Nirvana Shatterer level, a cultivator can use their insight into Law to evolve their domain(s) into an Essence . From the Essence perspective, a Domain can be viewed as an inferior version of an Essence and also can be said as an Essence seed.
