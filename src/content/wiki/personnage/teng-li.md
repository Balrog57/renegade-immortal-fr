## Personality

He is greedy, as shown by his desire to kill Wang Lin and steal his treasures, as well as self entitled, because he comes from a powerful clan and believes he can break even their own rules if he gives a convincing excuse.

### Book Two

He was asked by his good friend Chen Zhong to kill Wang Lin and Zhang Hu after the former killed the latter's master, Bai Zhan , who was also Chen Zhong's junior brother under Ji Mo he waited to ambush Wang Lin at the inn he was staying at and sent his men after Zhang Hu. After a lengthy chase, Wang Lin outwitted him and stole his Foundation, resulting in him dying a horrific death. However, he promised that his grandfather would avenge this act, which resulted in Teng Huayuan wiping out the Wang Clan and then going after Wang Lin.
