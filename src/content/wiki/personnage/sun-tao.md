## Appearance

He looks like a skinny middle-aged man and gives off the feeling of being a respectable gentleman.

### Background

He was the True-Disciple of Purple Division until Sun Yun suddenly came, defeated him, and took his title of True-Disciple of Purple Division. After the fight, he left Planet Tian Yun and didn't return for almost a millennium.

### History

He reappeared 100 years after Sun Yun disappeared during the battle for the title of the True-Disciple of Purple Division. He barely managed to defeat Wang Lin even with his Ascendant cultivation and regained his title of the True-Disciple of Purple Division.

He, once again, met Wang Lin inside the capital of Sky Demon Country in the East Demon Spirit Sea . At this time, however, his cultivation had dropped from the Mid-stage Ascendant to the peak of Late-stage Soul Transformation due to an ambush by the Da Lou Sword Sect. He became Shi Xiao's helper for the Demon General Competition.

Before Wang Lin went to the Allheaven Star System, when Wang Lin kill Shi Xiao, he warn him to be careful of All-Seer .
