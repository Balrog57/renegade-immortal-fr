## Overview

The Earth Commander-in-Chief of the Sky Demon Country is a prominent figure among the demon generals and commander-in-chiefs. He is particularly noted in the demon general competition, a prestigious event where demon generals compete for the chance to become vice commander-in-chiefs, a rank just below commander-in-chief.In the competition, the Earth Commander-in-Chief is portrayed as highly knowledgeable about the talents of various demon generals. For instance, when Demon General Mo Fei participates, a general highly regarded for his abilities the Earth Commander-in-Chief expresses admiration. He remarks that if Mo Fei could experience the baptism at the dragon lake one more time, he could be considered among the top three vice commander-in-chiefs. This highlights both Mo Fei’s potential and the Earth Commander-in-Chief’s role as an evaluator and supporter of promising talent. He also demonstrates a personable side, humorously acknowledging his fondness for geniuses like Mo Fei.

Beyond competitions, the Earth Commander-in-Chief is actively involved in military campaigns. During the mobilization against the Fire Demon Country, he leads the left army of 10 million demon soldiers toward Songtao city. Mounted on a demonic beast, he maintains a serious and silent demeanor, commanding respect from other high-ranking officials.
