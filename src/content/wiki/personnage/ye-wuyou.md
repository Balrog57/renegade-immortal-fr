## Overview

Ye Wuyou (叶无忧) was the First Generation Vermilion Bird (朱雀子) of Vermilion Bird Star and the founder of the Suzaku Country (朱雀国). Known as the Vermilion Bird Saint Emperor (朱雀圣皇) , he established Suzaku Mountain as the heart of governance and became a historical pillar for all later generations.

### Relationships

Situ Nan – Sworn ally and chosen successor as Second Vermilion Bird.

From a psychological standpoint, Ye Wuyou for Situ Nan is like an older brother, both mentor and friend. Though Situ Nan is arrogant, he deeply values favors received. Ye Wuyou’s kindness and support were so numerous that, despite his nature, Situ Nan—who might otherwise have left—chose to stay after one statement from Ye Wuyou, giving up his own path to guard Suzaku Star for him.

Wang Lin – Although they never met, Ye Wuyou’s legacy directly connects to Wang Lin’s destiny as a later Vermilion Bird.
