## Background

In the past, he was a member of the Ling Dong Clan.

Inside the Tomb, he battled Zhou Jin for one of the two Ancient Leaves.

### History

He and his followers met Wang Lin's group inside the Ancient Order Tomb where he ordered his follower, Xiao Jing , to open the restriction sealing the rank 4 Dao Spirit. His follower attempted to trick him by blowing up the formation and acted as if he was dead. However, Wang Lin had seen through his tricks and ambushed him. Similarly, Ling Dong had also seen through his follower's tricks and waited a while before appearing. Wang Lin acted as if he was caught off guard which caused Ling Dong to enter the formation and fall into Wang Lin's trap.

Wang Lin then had him fight and weaken the Dao Spirit before refining him into his ancient slave using the Ancient God Emperor Furnace and his Dao Spell: Dream Dao.

Afterwards, he teamed up with Esteemed Great Desolation to kill Dao Master Miao Yin 's avatar. After the kill, Esteemed Great Desolation possessed the Dao Master's body while Wang Lin managed to steal almost 30% of his Joss Flames to allow Ling Dong to reach the Third Step.

Sometime later, Wang Lin arrived in the Forest where Greed found Su Dao 's statue and the Ancient Leaf and there he taught Ling Dong the Dao Master Blue Dream 's Dao Spell: Fuse to absorb Dao Master Miao Yin's Joss Flames which allowed him to finally reach the Third Step.

Subsequently, Zhou Jin attempted to ambush Wang Lin only to fall in the latter's trap.
