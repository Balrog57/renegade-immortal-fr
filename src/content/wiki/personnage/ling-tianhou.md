## Overview

Ling Tianhou is the sect leader of the Da Lou Sword Sect on Planet Da Lou . He is the, self-proclaimed, rival of All-Seer but he has yet to beat him even once. But despite all his defeat, he has never given up.

### Appearance

This person was wearing a green robe and his white hair moved without any wind. Although he looked skinny, the aura he gave off was very oppressive. This was especially true for the four illusionary swords behind his back

### Background

In the past, he was friends with All-Seer but something happened inside the East Demon Spirit Sea that they entered. Afterwards, they became enemies.

Thousands of years ago, he was among the three invited to cultivate in the Brilliant Void Cultivation Sect along with All-Seer and Mortal Woman.

### History

When the East Demon Spirit Sea opened, he sent Greed with his disciples to look after them. He also gave his 12 disciples a sliver of his sword energy, each, as a live-saving treasure.

After a few centuries, he, Blood Ancestor , All-Seer and others entered the East Demon Spirit Sea but they instantly found that something was wrong. Soon after, they heard from Greed that Wang Lin stole the main token required to enter Celestial Emperor Qing Lin 's main cultivation cave. They chased after Wang Lin only to see him escape to the Allheaven Star System . They then sent Greed after Wang Lin to the Allheaven Star System.

A few centuries later, when Wang Lin returned to Planet Tian Yun , he was the first to spar with him. He was shocked to find that Wang Lin could easily block one of his spells.

Later, he saved Wang Lin from All-Seer after the former took the dying Bai Wei 's Extreme Yang. He then invited Wang Lin to be an elder of the Da Lou Sword Sect.
