## Appearance

Little Violet had no intelligence and was far from being as smart as Little White . However, this tiger was completely purple and 30 feet long. It gave off a very oppressive aura.

### Background

When Wang Lin and Zhou Ru traveled to an moon from Planet Suzaku , Wang Lin had promise Zhou Ru to get her a larger tiger to replace Little White . In the northern part of the moon, they caught a large tiger and Zhou Ru named him Little Violet. Wang Lin left an imprint on the tiger's soul, making Zhou Ru its master so it couldn't possibily turn on Zhou Ru.
