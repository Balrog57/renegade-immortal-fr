## History

He first met Wang Lin in the Celestial realm while Wang Lin was at the early Soul Formation stage, Zhou Yi helps Wang Lin in exchange for a favor and imprints a slave brand on Sun Tai forcing him to obey Wang Lin's orders.

One of his tasks as Wang Lin's slave was watching over his son Wang Ping in a mortal village and acting as his protector. While originally taking it as a mere job, he eventually warms up to Wang Ping and the life of a mortal, coming to think of him as his own grandson. When Wang Ping expresses his desire to become an immortal, Sun Tai supports him, even telling Wang Lin that with his approval, he could personally teach him and even pass down his abilities.

As time continues to pass, Sun Tai eventually passes away from old age. Before dying, he gave all of his belongings to Wang Lin with his dying wish being that Wang Lin could bring his ashes to Planet Dark Moon to be buried at the village where he was born at the foot of the sky piercing mountain.
