## Overview

His cultivation stands at the peak of early-stage Ascendant , a level far above ordinary demon generals and only one step away from mid-stage Ascendant. However, he has been trapped at this threshold for a very long time, unable to take the final step forward. This prolonged stagnation shaped his temperament into one of restraint and quiet vigilance. Though outwardly calm, he is deeply aware of the difficulty of breaking through, and this awareness makes him especially sensitive to extraordinary existences that defy conventional cultivation logic.

His interest in Wang Lin begins before the demon general competition, during a previous encounter in which Wang Lin injured him despite being in a much lower realm. That clash forced the Xuan Vice Commander-in-Chief to acknowledge Wang Lin as an anomaly, someone whose true combat power far exceeded his cultivation level. From that point on, he no longer viewed Wang Lin with contempt, but with caution and attention, recognizing him as a dangerous variable within the Demon Spirit Land.

During the demon general competition in Sky Demon City, the Xuan Vice Commander-in-Chief’s attention remains firmly fixed on Wang Lin. While most vice commander-in-chiefs and demon generals dismiss Wang Lin due to his late-stage Soul Transformation cultivation, he does the opposite. He openly places a wager with the Yellow Vice Commander-in-Chief , asserting that Wang Lin will take first place. This decision shocks those around him, as it directly contradicts common expectations and highlights his confidence in his own judgment.

The defining moment comes when Wang Lin strikes the demon drum fifteen times . This feat is utterly unprecedented in the history of the Sky Demon Country. Never before has any demon general, or even vice commander-in-chief, caused the demon drum to sound so many times. Each strike carries terrifying soul force and intent, shaking Sky Demon City and causing even high-ranking demon experts to tremble. The fifteenth strike, in particular, pushes the demon drum beyond all recorded limits, stunning the commander-in-chiefs, vice commander-in-chiefs, and countless demon generals into silence.

For the Xuan Vice Commander-in-Chief, this moment is profoundly transformative. As the demon drum resonates with Wang Lin’s soul, it stirs his own long-stagnant cultivation. Insights that had eluded him for countless years surge forth, and he clearly senses that his bottleneck has loosened. Wang Lin’s fifteen strikes do not merely demonstrate power, they reveal a fundamentally different cultivation path, one that disregards established limits and conventions. This realization deeply shakes the Xuan Vice Commander-in-Chief and reignites his pursuit of breakthrough.

After the demon drum falls silent, the Xuan Vice Commander-in-Chief comes to a decisive conclusion: Wang Lin is no ordinary cultivator . He believes that Wang Lin may be the person the Demon Emperor has been waiting for across endless years, an existence capable of reshaping the Demon Spirit Land. When Wang Lin’s gaze later sweeps across the vice commander-in-chiefs, most instinctively avoid eye contact, unsettled by his presence. The Xuan Vice Commander-in-Chief does not. He meets Wang Lin’s eyes directly, and in that silent exchange there is no hostility, only acknowledgment, respect, and a trace of regret for ever having underestimated him.

From that moment on, the Xuan Vice Commander-in-Chief’s attitude toward Wang Lin fully shifts. He recognizes Wang Lin’s strength as something far beyond other demon generals and even comparable to higher authorities. His own cultivation, once stagnant, begins to move again, inspired by the shock of Wang Lin’s fifteen drum strikes. Though his personal history remains largely unknown, his actions and reactions during this event firmly establish him as one of the few high-ranking demon figures capable of recognizing true greatness when it appears.
