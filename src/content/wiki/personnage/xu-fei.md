## Personality

She had a crush on Wang Zhou and shared his dislike of Wang Lin as a result in her teen years. As an adult, she is friendly, allowing a junior brother who cannot fly to cling to her.

### Book One

She appears any time Wang Zhou is present to chime in on his mockery towards Wang Lin , as a result of the love triangle between her, Wang Zhou and Zhou Rui , whose attention he vies for. She chooses to stay and join Xuan Dao Sect after they take Heng Yue Mountain.

### Book Two

A few years after the Heng Yue Sect was disbanded, she and her senior disciple-brother Li Hai stumbled upon Wang Lin's cultivation cave by mistake. Wang Lin recognized her and allowed her to leave unharmed.

### Book Ten

Wang Lin has a dream involving her and Zhou Rui after he consumes three Dao Fruits to complete his Life and Death, Karma and True and False Essences. He encounters them in a boat in the rain.
