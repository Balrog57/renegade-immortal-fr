## Appearance

She was extremely charming and despite having a voluptuous figure she looked graceful.

### Background

She joined the Heavenly Fate Sect at the age of seven.

### History

She sealed Wang Lin for a few days to pay off Zhao Xingsha 's favor. To resolve the enmity between them, she taught Wang Lin Great Teleportation and the incomplete Nine Cycle Celestial Refining Tactic.

She battled Wang Lin for the title of the True-Disciple of Purple Division but she only did so as a sport as she wasn't really all that interested in the position.

Hundreds of years later, when Wang Lin returned to the Heavenly Fate Sect, she told him the secret about All-Seer that he devours all his disciples that reach Illusory Yin . Her real cultivation is at the Illusory Yin stage but she has kept it sealed for centuries in an effort to prolong her life. She is also in contact with Sun Hong and Lu Hai from the Red Division who also have their cultivations sealed.

She asked for Wang Lin's help in reinforcing her seal which has begun to loosen.
