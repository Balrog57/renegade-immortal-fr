## Background

He is one of the most talented cultivators of his generation as he managed to reach Corporeal Yang in a mere millennia.

### History

He met Wang Lin inside the thunder pond in the Southern Domain of the Allheaven Star System. He mistook Wang Lin for a Third Step Cultivator after the latter guided Shengong Hu on the path to reaching the origin of Thunder.

He always regretted that he didn't take the opportunity to acknowledged Wang Lin as his Lord like Shengong Hu.

He was saved by Wang Lin from the mad Qing Shui inside the Thunder Celestial Realm .

He met Wang Lin during the qualifiers for the Celestial Bestowment Tournament. By this point, he had learnt that Wang Lin was not a Third Step Cultivator. He challenged Wang Lin to a duel but easily lost. Although he was no longer fanatic about Wang Lin, he still respected him greatly. He vouched for Wang Lin during the tournament.

He managed to gain slight enlightenment of the first Battle Scroll in eight seconds but was not able to absorb the sliver of source origin energy from it.
