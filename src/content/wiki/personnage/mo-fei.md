## Overview

Mo Fei was one of the most talented and mysterious demon generals in the Demon Spirit Land, renowned even among high-ranking demon cultivators. Three hundred years ago, he held the title of number one demon general , a position earned through overwhelming strength, extraordinary skill, and an innate talent described as being “blessed by the heavens.” His cultivation path was unusual and deeply mysterious, marked by abilities that even other demon generals could not understand. His signature ability, the Great Rotating Disk spell , was powerful enough to reverse and reflect enemy attacks. This spell demonstrated its terrifying might when Mo Fei reflected Mo Lihai’s Hundred Wave Demon Sea technique back at him, overturning the battle entirely and forcing Mo Lihai into defeat.

### History

Mo Fei’s brilliance was further displayed during the demon general competition, where demon talents were tested against the demon drum. He struck the drum six times without sustaining any injury , an achievement that immediately stunned the crowd and established him as a cut above the rest. However, Mo Fei did not stop there. Despite the increasing backlash with each strike, he managed to hit the drum a seventh and then an eighth time. The seventh strike contained such powerful killing intent that his armor exploded, and the eighth shattered multiple protective mirrors, yet he survived by stabilizing his body using demonic seals. These feats were beyond extraordinary—so much so that even veteran demon generals and vice commander-in-chiefs openly admired his strength. [ 1 ]

With unstoppable determination, Mo Fei pushed even further and ultimately struck the demon drum eleven times , a result considered nearly impossible. This accomplishment shocked everyone present. Demon generals grew silent, vice commanders darkened their expressions, and many affirmed that Mo Fei surpassed most experts of his generation. There were widespread expectations that, following his eventual baptism in the dragon lake , his cultivation would break past its current limits and he would ascend to the rank of vice commander-in-chief.

His appearance also reinforced his reputation. Mo Fei possessed an imposing presence, a cold and calm demeanor, and wore distinct armor and a demonic mask. During the drum competition, his mask shattered from the backlash, revealing a handsome face that stunned the spectators. Wang Lin immediately recognized his appearance—Mo Fei looked identical to a mysterious black-haired man Wang Lin had previously encountered in the depths of Hong Prison , raising questions about Mo Fei’s identity and origins.
