## Background

Tattoo Clan accidentally offended the Supreme of the Outer Realm which caused them to take refuge in the Sealed Realm .

However, once inside the Sealed Realm, they had to fight once again as the celestials were eyeing the source origin energy of Tattoo Clan's cultivators and so the celestials started a war with them. Many Tattoo Clan cultivators and celestials died during the war.

Rain Celestial Emperor Qing Lin promised Qiu Yao that he would provide a place for the Tattoo Clan in the Sealed Realm. So, she became his concubine.

Qing Lin praised her eyes that had condensed an sliver of primordial energy and she willingly dug them and gifted them to him.

Qing Lin sealed her inside the Celestial Burial Pool to suppress the monstrous amount of souls there and she willingly accepted it.

### History

The collapse of the Celestial Realm was one of the Outer Realm's plans.The Tattoo Clan Holy Ancestor is only an avatar. Her true identity is the daughter of the Sovereign of the Outer Realm. Her purpose for coming to the Sealed Realm was to destroy all forces within but her secret was found out by Qing Lin.

She arrived near Wang Lin as she felt Ta Shan 's aura on him. She then freed Ta Shan from the Celestial Guard spell and imparted her ancestral life tattoo to him.

She also gifted Wang Lin with the Withered Tattoo spell which can allow a Qi cultivator to gain the power of Tattoos.
