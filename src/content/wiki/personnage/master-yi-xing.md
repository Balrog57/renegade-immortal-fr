## History

He attempted to embarrass Wang Lin as he had unwittingly spread his divine sense over the city. However, Wang Lin soon turned the tables on him and injured him. Three Chen Brothers all came to apologize and Wang Lin accepted their apology. Afterwards, Master Yi Chen introduced him to Wang Lin.

Soon after, All-Seer attacked Wang Lin because he took the Extreme Yang from the dying Bai Wei . He spectated from afar.

Afterwards, he met Wang Lin when the latter was passing through his home planet after subduing Master Hollow Wind.
