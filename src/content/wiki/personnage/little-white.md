## Background

Little White was a demonic tiger that was caught by Zhou Ru in the Country of Chu . He was very proud but was very scared of Zhou Ru because of Wang Lin. He watched Zhou Ru grew up. Later, he became Zhou Ru's best companion and cared for her deeply, even willing to betray Wang Lin for her sake.
