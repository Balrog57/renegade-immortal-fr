## Bei Lou

Bei Luo was the Ancient Demon fragment that controlled the Sky Demon Country inside the Demon Spirit Land .

### Human Form

A bold young man in red robes. He was very handsome and had a demonic sense of beauty. His head was completely smooth without a strand of hair.

### Background

In the past, after the events of the Heavenly Madness , Qing Lin survived with injuries and escaped to his Celestial Spirit Heaven Realm. However, there he found an Ancient Devil and an Ancient Demon waiting to ambush him. He split both of them into nine parts before rushing to the fifth cave to enter closed-door cultivation to recuperate.

### History

Bei Luo helped Wang Lin battle the divine retribution when the latter was breaking through to Ascendant . He later also helped Wang Lin trap the scattered devil, Tie Lan.

In return, Wang Lin helped Bei Luo weaken the fragmented ancient demon under the Fire Demon Country . Wang Lin's involvement helped give Bei Luo a single opportunity which resulted in him being able to devour the fragmented ancient demon under the Fire Demon Country.

To thank him, Bei Luo allowed Wang Lin to enter the diluted version of Shi Realm which allowed him to see the trap inside the Celestial Slaughter Art before it was too late. He also located Zhou Yi's spirit's location inside the Tide Abyss.

Many years later when Wang Lin returned to the East Demon Spirit Sea, he found that Bei Luo had already absorbed a majority of the nine fragmented ancient demons across the land. This allowed him to recover a majority of his cultivation from when he was still whole. Bei Luo entered the fifth cave along with Wang Lin's group.
