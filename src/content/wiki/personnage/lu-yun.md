## Background

Lu Yun was born in the ancient qi refiner world. He became the alchemy boy of the Fourth Generation Vermillion Bird Divine Emperor who taught him qi cultivation.

At the age of 1,327, he gained some achievements as a qi refiner He followed the Divine Emperor and witnessed the glory of my Vermillion Bird Divine Sect.

At age 2,945, he became an elder of the Vermillion Bird Divine Sect.

At age 4,760, he became one of the head elders and passed his first Heaven’s Blight.

At age 6,215, he was given the Vermillion Bird Mark and achieved his first awakening. He defeated everyone else that had awakened and became number one among the awakened.

At age 7,912, with the help of the fourth Vermillion Bird Divine Emperor, he completed his second awakening. From then on, he was selected to be the next Divine Emperor, the young lord of the Vermillion Bird Divine Sect.

At age 11,463, the Tattoo Clan invaded from outside and stirred up the world. Vermillion Bird Divine Sect helped the Celestial Realm battle the Tattoo Clan. Afterwards, the fourth Vermillion Bird Divine Emperor left with the other three Divine Emperors to head to the outside world. Before they left, he was designated the fifth Vermillion Bird Divine Emperor. At that time, he had just passed his second Heaven’s Blight with the help of the fourth Vermillion Bird Divine Emperor.
