## Overview

When she met Wang Lin for the first time, she was a Foundation Establishment student of War God Temple in Huofen Kingdom and the child of Feng Luan. She's attracted to Ma Liang, treating her like a brother or perhaps potential love interest. She was greatly disappointed knowing that Ma Liang died before surrendering her soul blood to Wang Lin. Her mother realized something was wrong with her. That's why she chased Wang Lin to take back her soul blood. Later, after the war to take root in the neighboring country because of the Fire Beast Calamity, she asked Wang Lin to kill Ma Liang's junior sister (childhood friend) and her lover (Zhou An) who had ill-intention towards Ma Liang in exchange of the map Wang Lin needs.

She appears again in the storyline where the inner realm (sealed realm) was in a great war against outer realm. She's currently with the fellow daoist of Summoned River Star System, where most of the cultivators are fellow ladies/women. They're talking about the legend of the Lord of Sealed Realm. Zhou Zihong commented on the possibility that she met The Lord of the Sealed Realm previously as Ma Liang (who got taken over) and Wang Lin happened to be there listening to that conversation.
