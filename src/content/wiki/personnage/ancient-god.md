## Characteristics

An Ancient god doesn't cultivate spiritual energy like a cultivator but absorbs a lot of spiritual energy to strengthen their body instead, they do not commonly need to use weapons because their body is itself the most powerful weapon they'll ever need. The Ancient God's Ancient Clan Stars are located on the forehead and their skin is heavily cracked when they're in giant form.

These beings need to feed on stars or celestial bodies in order to grow. As infants, they arrive in a meteorite and then settle in the nucleus of the star or planet they come into contact with and from there they begin to absorb the energy from it until it is lifeless. As they mature, they usually leave their home planet to consume other celestial bodies.

The size that an Ancient God can have is directly related to the level of their cultivation.

Cultivation: To an Ancient God, the more times their bodies experience reconstruction, the stronger their cultivation and bodies become. Also, with every reconstruction, their souls would also become larger. Among the Ancient Gods, there were no cultivation levels like in the world of cultivators. The Ancient Gods’ cultivation was mainly just their soul and their body.

The body was the weapon and the soul was the hand that controlled the weapon, combining into the almost invincible Ancient God Family.

### Trivia

The Ancient God inheritance is composed of the Inheritance of Power and Inheritance of Knowledge. Only by having the two can one become a True Ancient God Inheritor.

### Other novels

Ancient Gods also appear in other novels of Er Gen - I Shall Seal the Heavens and Pursuit of the Truth .
