## Background

Lei Ji is a Royal Giant Demon from Planet Giant Demon. He fled Planet Giant Demon to keep his life with his people and wandered the Star until he met Ye Wuyou. Ye Wuyou offer him a place, far from the Forsaken Immortal Clan's Sacred Ground, for his peoples help against the Forsaken Immortal Clan , to repay Ye Wuyou's kindness, he entered the war. After a battle, he was injured and ambushed by Corpse Sect's member and imprisoned under Zhao's Corpse Sect's Headquarter.

### History

During his imprisonment, Wang Lin found him but he didn't think he could help him. Later he encounter Wang Lin when the later came to take his Royal Giant Blood that contain Ancient God Blood and try to delay him so he can be freed. The third time Wang Lin come, he submit to Wang Lin and ask him to help him by saying all the truth he know. Later his bloodline is stimulated by Wang Lin's Ancient God Roar and truly submit.

### Trivia

He really like Ye Wuyou's righteous personality but dislike Situ Nan's demonic personality.

He hope Wang Lin can help him take revenge on Planet Giant Demon.
