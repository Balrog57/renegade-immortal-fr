## History

Ancestor of the Huan Family on Thousand Illusion Planet, head planet in the Allheaven Star System’s Northern Domain. He was traveling in the Alliance Star System for thousands of years searching for the heaven defying bead, but met Liu Mei who was cultivating the Thousand Illusion Ruthless Domain instead and accepted her as a disciple. He later adopted Liu Mei and inducted her into the Huan Family by performing the bloodline ritual. The bloodline ritual refines the purest Huan family blood from of tens of thousands of Huan family members, turn that into one thousand blood runes that will imprint on a person, and then finally extract all of the blood in that person’s body. [ 1 ]

When Liu Mei loses her battle with Wang Lin on planet Ran Yun, Huan Wuqing saves her by sending his origin soul through the void. However, Wang Lin says that if he saves Liu Mei, he will wipe out the Huan Family.
