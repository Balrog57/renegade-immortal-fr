## Overview

Sima Yan was a gloomy-faced and ambitious cultivator who served as the vice commander of an army of ten thousand demon soldiers. Operating under Commander Wang Lin , he played a key role in the military affairs surrounding Wang Lin’s rise to command. Known for his calculating demeanor and deep killing intent, Sima Yan’s eventual downfall came as a result of his cruelty, ambition, and defiance against Wang Lin’s authority.

### Personality and Appearance

Sima Yan was characterized by his dark, brooding expression and aura of restrained hostility. Upon first encountering Wang Lin, he was visibly shocked, but his surprise quickly gave way to killing intent. He was cunning, power-hungry, and prone to manipulating those under him. Despite his rank as vice commander, he commanded loyalty from the demon soldiers more than Wang Lin did initially, reflecting both his influence and the troops’ mistrust of outsiders.

### Role in the Army

Sima Yan served as vice commander of the demon army stationed on East Demon Spirit Sea . When Wang Lin assumed command, the demon soldiers refused to obey their new leader and instead followed Sima Yan’s orders. Wang Lin, however, remained calm and unreactive, walking through the regiments in silence before leaving the camp to cultivate outside.

Sima Yan interpreted Wang Lin’s composure as a hidden threat. Fearing an impending crisis, he convened a meeting with the regiment leaders to discuss Wang Lin’s intentions. Over the following days, Wang Lin’s lack of interference made Sima Yan increasingly anxious, as he could sense that his authority was gradually slipping away.

Tension reached a breaking point when Wang Lin killed Regiment Leader Sun and his soldiers after being provoked. Sima Yan accused Wang Lin of slaughtering his subordinates, attempting to rally opposition against him. Wang Lin responded by citing military law, warning Sima Yan to withdraw his accusations within a set deadline. It became clear that Wang Lin had been waiting for the perfect moment to assert his authority and act decisively.

### Cruelty Toward Hu Pao and Thirteen

After Wang Lin’s temporary absence from the camp, two of his followers Hu Pao and Thirteen fell into Sima Yan’s control. Hu Pao was captured for breaking army regulations and thrown into the Dark Prison , where he later disappeared under mysterious circumstances. Thirteen attempted to rescue Hu Pao but was overpowered and nearly killed by Sima Yan, surviving only through the intervention of the chief commander. These cruel acts reflected Sima Yan’s desire to eliminate Wang Lin’s influence and strengthen his own position within the army.

### Death

Wang Lin later confronted Sima Yan directly. Using soul search techniques, he captured Sima Yan’s body and delved into his memories, uncovering the truth about Hu Pao and Thirteen’s suffering. Enraged, Wang Lin unleashed the Celestial Slaughter Art , causing Sima Yan’s body to wither and explode violently. From his remains, Wang Lin refined a life seal , while strands of grey gas emerged and were absorbed into Wang Lin’s possession.

After Sima Yan’s death, further interrogation of soldiers revealed that Hu Pao had been healed and taken away by the demon general , who was collecting cultivators with strong physiques for unknown purposes.
