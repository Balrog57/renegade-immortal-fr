## Chapter 2: Immortals  follows  Wang Lin  (also known as  Tie Zhu ) as he leaves his village for the first time, carrying his parents’ hopes and dreams on his journey to become an immortal disciple. Accompanied by his  fourth uncle , who offers reassurance and guidance, Wang Lin sets out to face the unknown world ahead.

Upon arriving at the Wang family estate , Wang Lin is introduced to the awe-inspiring concept of immortals — beings capable of soaring through the skies and wielding powers beyond mortal comprehension. There, he encounters a young immortal whose cold and piercing gaze leaves him intimidated and uneasy.

Though filled with fear and uncertainty, Wang Lin’s heart also burns with excitement and curiosity. The encounter marks the beginning of his long and arduous pursuit of the immortal path, setting the stage for the challenges that await him.
