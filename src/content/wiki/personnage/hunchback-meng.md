## Appearance

Because his unique cultivation manual, his body got deformed, his back hunched, and his face filled with pustules.

### Plot

He is the main reason why a desolate beast chase and attack the group and make everyone scatter to different place in Land of Ancient God first trial. He entered the earth test of the first trial by his own fault. He stuck in black tower in this trial because he attacked tornadoes but more of the tornadoes come at him instead and block his escape path. He forced to sacrifice his Heaven repelling toad and life treasure Poisonous King Cauldron to pass through the tornadoes. While he focused on escaping Wang Lin used this chance to order the second devil's to rob Meng's bag of holding. This cause him lost of focus and finally dead by King of tornadoes creature. Later he seen again in the third trial after possessed and turn into puppet by Tuo Sen to chase and capture the group.
