### Young Man in Blue

The young man in blue was tall, with sharp eyebrows and deep, dark eyes that shone like distant stars, making it difficult for others to see through him. His mere presence caused the surrounding space to ripple, a sign that he had reached the peak of the mid-stage of Soul Transformation, radiating celestial spiritual energy.

With a wave of his hand, he summoned a white fan into his grasp, enhancing his elegant and refined demeanor, akin to that of a noble and handsome prince.
