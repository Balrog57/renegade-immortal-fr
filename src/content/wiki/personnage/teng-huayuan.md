## Personality

He is an extremely self-centered man and has absolutely no qualms about letting his relatives bully, steal from and kill others. When this backfired upon his grandson and led to his death at Wang Lin's hands, he murdered the entire Wang Clan and sealed their souls into a soul flag to bait Wang Lin out. This act of slaughtering mortals earns him some disdain even from his fellow Cultivators. He furthermore destroyed several of those souls while waiting for the then Foundation Establishment stage Wang Lin. 200 years later when Wang Lin returns as his equal, a Nascent Soul stage expert by the standards of their country and purges his Teng Clan with similar fury, he makes a final bid to pretend his will is broken to buy time until help arrives, unfortunately for him, Wang Lin simply killed him instead.
