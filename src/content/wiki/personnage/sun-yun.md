## Overview

Sun Yun was a prominent figure in the Purple Division of the Heavenly Fate Sect and held the position of Seventh Disciple. A genius of his generation, he rose a thousand years ago to become the top disciple in the Purple Division, replacing the previous sixth disciple as one of the All-Seer 's direct disciples (there's a total of 7 spots, because there are 7 colors of the rainbow aka known as Tian Yun Qi Zi). Sun Yun resided in the Purple Cloud Pavilion, a prestigious location within the division.

About a hundred years ago, Sun Yun betrayed the Heavenly Fate Sect and fled for unknown reasons. Despite this, the sect's ancestor personally pursued him, returning alone after a month. Following this event, the Purple Cloud Pavilion was sealed, and it was declared that its next occupant would be chosen from the seven direct disciples of the All-Seer. Sun Yun's betrayal had a profound impact on the Purple Division. Already considered the weakest among the sect’s seven divisions, it suffered from intense infighting and instability in the position of true disciple. This internal strife was cited as a primary reason for the division's decline in strength and prestige after Sun Yun's departure.

Later, Wang Lin, the new Seventh Brother of the Purple Division, came to live in the Purple Cloud Pavilion. He faced discrimination and hostility from other disciples due to Sun Yun's legacy and the pavilion's mysterious reputation. The pavilion remained heavily guarded, and few dared approach it, underscoring the lingering influence and enigma surrounding Sun Yun.Sun Yun's name continued to inspire both fear and respect. He was rumored to have slain countless people in the Demon Spirit Land and was eventually hunted by the Master and seven seniors of the sect, though he managed to escape. His reputation as a fearsome and devilish figure persisted throughout the sect's history.

Behind his death there is apparently a huge secret regarding All-Seer .

### History

While Wang Lin was assisting Bei Luo to absorb the Fire Demon in the Demon Spirit Land , this person, Sun Yun was mentioned again. As one of the most talented cultivators Bei Luo has ever seen and only the second person to ever receive a gift from Bei Luo (helping one attain true enlightenment of a celestial technique).
