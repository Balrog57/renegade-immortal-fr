## Personality

He is a very vulgar, perverted and lascivious person as he not only likes women but also handsome men and will do anything to have his way with them before killing them.

### History

He participated in the final battle of the Third Sealed-Outer Realm War in the Allheaven Star System . During that time, he deliberately hunted Mu Bingmei due to her beauty and also due to the fact that she was involved with Wang Lin .
