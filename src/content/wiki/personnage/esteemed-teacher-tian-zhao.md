## History

He attempted to stop Wang Lin when the latter stormed the Cultivation Alliance to save Qing Shui . However, he was easily killed by Wang Lin after he released his Celestial Qi.

Wang Lin also took his three Joss Flames Dragon Soul and gifted one to Master Flamespark and one to Shengong Hu while he freed the last one after it killed all the Outer Realm cultivators present in the Alliance headquarters.
