## Background

He did not have any sort of distinguished background but he still managed to grow the Purple Dao Sect into one of the strongest sects in the Rank 5 Region .

Despite his low cultivation, even masters in the rank 9 region are interested in him and hold him in high regards because of his wisdom and intelligence.
