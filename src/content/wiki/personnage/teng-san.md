## Appearance

Teng San didn’t look young. His hair was already white and he looked to be about 40 or 50 years old. If one looked closely, they could see that Wang Zhuo’s wife looked like him

### Background

The reason Teng San left the hidden chamber was to guard his daughter, Teng Xui Xui, on her way to Teng Family City. He did not like Wang Zhuo, but he knew his daughter loved Wang Zhuo immensely. He knew if he wanted to change fate, he had to have enough power. That means surpassing his ancestors. Many years later, he succeed in his cultivation and was able to push his two daughters onto the path of cultivation, but his wife’s life span was already up. The pain in his heart had never diminished in all these years. He has the last drop of his wife’s blood in his cherished sword.

### History

One of the Teng Family's core members (numbered from Yi (one) to Jiu(Nine), he's ranked third so referred to as Teng San (three), but his real name is Teng Hai.

When Wang Lin was approaching him, he threw his daughter, Teng Xiu Xiu and Wang Zhao into an oval ring of light to teleport them to safety so he can battle Wang Lin. If he was allowed to choose the way he would die, he would hope to die along with this sword. With battle intent, he rushed toward Wang Lin but was caught by Wang Lin’s Ji Realm and died on the spot.
