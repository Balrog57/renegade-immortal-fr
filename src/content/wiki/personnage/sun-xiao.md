## Overview

Leader Sun was a regiment leader in the demon army who opposed Wang Lin during the latter’s early command. Known for his arrogance and contempt, he openly insulted Wang Lin by calling him "trash," displaying defiance toward the new commander.

Following Wang Lin’s appointment as commander, Leader Sun’s disdain sparked a violent confrontation. Wang Lin, unhesitating and strategic, suppressed and killed Leader Sun. This action provoked anger among the demon soldiers loyal to Sun, who charged at Wang Lin. Utilizing his Celestial Sword and Half-Moon Blade, Wang Lin defeated many of them, demonstrating his authority and martial prowess.

Leader Sun’s death marked a pivotal turning point in Wang Lin’s control over the demon army. The incident not only subdued immediate opposition but also shifted the relationship between the regiment leaders and Sima Yan, the vice commander, making Wang Lin’s authority widely recognized among the demon soldiers.
