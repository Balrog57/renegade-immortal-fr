## Background

Amongst All Seer 's first generation disciples, he has been in the sect for the longest.

In the past, when he received the title of the True-Disciple of Purple Division, Sun Yun suddenly came and was defeated by him. Then, soon after Sun Yun disappeared.

He attacked Bai Wei (most likely on All-Seer's orders) when he about to separate his dead sister's soul. This caused her soul to get stuck to his. This was further aggravated by cultivating the Celestial Double Desire Art that All-Seer gave him.

### History

Many centuries later, when he received the title of the True-Disciple of Purple Division, Wang Lin suddenly came to Planet Tian Yun and was defeated by him. Subsequently, Wang Lin was defeated by Sun Tao who became the True-Disciple of Purple Division, once again. Still, he had deep hatred for Wang Lin.

Many centuries later, when Wang Lin returned to the Heavenly Fate Sect. he could finally get his revenge against Wang Lin and he was not shocked when he learnt that Wang Lin had reached Nirvana Scryer so when he saw him hold his own against Ling Tianhou . He instantly won fighting against Wang Lin and showed off his great cultivation.
