## Background

He was once an elder in the Cultivation Alliance . He holds a deep grudge against the Alliance Star System as he was thrown out of it by Master Zhong Xuan . It led him to scheme for thousands years in order to start a war between the 2 star systems.

{This grudge is likely related to Master Zhong Xuan doing something less than stellar towards Master Flamespark's Dao Partner however this is just speculation as Er Gen just glosses over the matter.}

### History

Master Flamespark refined 49 fragments of Thunder Celestial Realm into a powerful treasure and used 49 origin souls as its treasure spirit; these included 28 Illusory Yin origin souls, 14 Corporeal Yang origin souls, two Nirvana Scryer origin souls, Blood Ancestor 's and another Nirvana Cleanser 's origin souls, two Nirvana Shatterer origin souls, and the origin soul of the Slaughter Celestial Lord , Rain Celestial Realm ’s rank 6 Celestial Lord.
