## History

Under the possession of the Ancient Demon, Blood God poured the ancient lamp 's weakened-spirit into his daughter's body and then refined his granddaughter, Yao Mengyun , into the ancient lamp's spirit.

He also poured a demon-ling inside Yao Yun , his younger son, to control him after he tried to rebel and save his two nieces, Yao Mengyun and Yao Bingyun .

His eldest son, Blood Ancestor 's father, escaped to the Alliance Star System soon after he learnt that his father has been possessed by an Ancient Demon.

Many years later, when Wang Lin returns from the Outer realm, Wang Lin comes to resolve the feud between them, even though he was under the protection of Master Lu Fu , he still perishes under the hand of Wang Lin, forcing the 6 stars of the Ancient Demon hidding in his body to appear, Wang Lin captures the latter.
