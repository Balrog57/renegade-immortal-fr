## Overview

The Suzaku Tomb is one of the most mysterious and significant locations on Planet Suzaku. Situated deep within Mount Suzaku, the sacred land of the Suzaku lineage, it serves as the entrance to a vast and independent world hidden inside the planet itself. The tomb is sustained and shielded by the immense power of the Cultivation Planet Crystal, which forms an impenetrable barrier around it—accessible only through the entrance at Mount Suzaku.

Within, the Suzaku Tomb extends into a boundless realm complete with its own mountains, rivers, sky, and earth, making it a world unto itself. Over countless years, it has expanded as more soul pieces became absorbed into the Cultivation Planet Crystal. The interior teems with strange lifeforms born from these soul fragments, unstable spatial rifts, and natural calamities caused by the gradual collapse of the crystal at its core.

Entry into the Suzaku Tomb is restricted—Ascendant cultivators are forbidden to enter, leaving only those below the Ascendant stage able to pass through the barrier. However, the tomb is perilous even for them; countless cultivators have perished within its depths, victims of collapsing domains or powerful soul-beast entities. Both Suzaku cultivators and members of the Forsaken Immortal Clan venture inside seeking to reclaim their lost soul pieces, vital for their survival and cultivation progress.

At the heart of the tomb lies the grave and legacy of the First Generation Suzaku Ye Wuyou , along with the profound Suzaku Seal’s dao. Each generation of Suzaku successors has entered the tomb to inherit this sacred legacy. During the war against the Forsaken Immortal Clan, the tomb became a battleground and a center of spiritual and strategic importance for the entire planet.

The tomb’s structure is divided into outer and inner sections—the inner realm being smaller yet far more dangerous. As the Cultivation Planet Crystal continues to fracture, both the tomb and Planet Suzaku itself face escalating instability and collapse.
