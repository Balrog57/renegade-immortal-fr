## Background

Originally from the Immortal Astral Continent, Master Scarlet Soul was sealed away into his hell by Ancient Clan Ye Mo. This didn't last as when Wang Lin escaped from Sovereign's trap during the Outer Realm Inner Realm war, he was released.

At one point in his life he also saved the life of a cultivator named Luo Ming, one of the Celestial Emperor Lian Daozhen's bodyguards.

### Book Thirteen

He founded the Scarlet Soul Sect and after regaining his cultivation, waged war against the entire Cave World. Originating from the Immortal Astral Continent, his power was too much for the Cave World's experts to handle, severely injuring Dao Master Blue Dream and rendering Master South Cloud into nothing more than a mere origin soul.

His goal was to absorb the life essence of all the cave world's inhabitants to push his cultivation base to Golden Exalt (Peak of late-stage Void Tribulant). Afterwards, he planned on returning to the Immortal Astral Continent and become a head elder of a sect. Combining a sect's backing and his personal stash of resources hidden within the continent he claimed to have a high chance of breaking into Empyrean Exalt as well. He would then request to serve under Grand Empyrean Dao Yi, as he knew that his first choice, the Celestial Emperor would surely reject him due to being too weak.

When Wang Lin returns to the Cave World, he believes that he is much stronger than him. However, when he discovers that one of his companions possesses a Golden Exalt sea dragon he begins to panic. He mistakenly believes that Liu Jinbiao is the owner of the sea dragon and Wang Lin's benefactor. He then claims his master is under the protection of Grand Empyrean Dao Yi and requests Liu Jinbiao to allow him to kill Wang Lin. However, Wang Lin proves that it is actually him with all of the power when he forcibly establishes contact with Dao Yi all the way from the Cave World. After seeing Dao Yi act completely subservient to Wang Lin, Master Scarlet Soul spirals into insanity, proclaiming that everything Wang Lin did was fake despite having felt Dao Yi's aura.

After Wang Lin reveals his Grand Empyrean Sun and Lian Daozhen's assimilated soul, his mind completely shatters. He suffers a mental breakdown before Wang Lin kills him using his Grand Empyrean Sun.
