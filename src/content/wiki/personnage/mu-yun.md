Demon General Mu Yun is a notable figure among the demon generals, recognized as one of the top 30 demon generals. He cultivates the "Nine Deaths Nine Lives" cultivation method and, impressively, had reached the "five deaths and five lives" level 300 years ago. His appearance is quite distinctive: he is thin, with sunken eyes and a sickly yellow complexion, and he stands motionless like an ancient pine tree, exuding a dark and ominous aura. His smile resembles that of a corpse, adding to his eerie presence. In a confrontation with Demon General Shi Xiao, who is ranked among the top three demon generals, Mu Yun displays powerful abilities.

He can manipulate five strands of life and death demonic energy, which manifest as five green snakes that twist and extend aggressively toward Shi Xiao. These strands are potent enough to create powerful gusts of wind and emit a foul smell that shocks onlookers. However, Shi Xiao counters Mu Yun's attack with a diamond-shaped light that destroys the green demonic energy strands and forces Mu Yun to retreat, showing that although Mu Yun is formidable, he is not invincible. Mu Yun also has a powerful helper named Tian Lui from the Giant Spirit Sect, who is huge, weighing hundreds of kilograms, and whose steps cause the ground to shake. Tian Lui is capable of emitting celestial spiritual energy and uses it to aid Mu Yun in battle.
