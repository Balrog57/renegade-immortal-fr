## Appearance

She was a beautiful woman, Unsurpassed beauty! The woman was wearing a mask. The woman’s eyes conveyed feelings of indignation and resilience. They also revealed a determination to kill, even if her opponent was a god

Part of her is in a mysterious mask owned by wang baole( protagonist of a world worth protecting)

### Background

Wang Lin's daughter. Real name - Wang Yiyi. It was a silver-haired middle-aged man who brought a cheeky little girl along with him to the Vast Expance Dao Palace. The Vast Expanse Dao Palace put great emphasis on his arrival, and even the Patriarch greeted him personally. In that imagery, the little girl (Wang Yiyi) looked curiously at her surroundings, and the image faded to become an eternity.
