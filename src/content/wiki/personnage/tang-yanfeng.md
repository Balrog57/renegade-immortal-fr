## History

Tang Yanfeng met Wang Lin in the Thunder Celestial Realm and hated him. However, when he saw Wang Lin battle with the Blood God and other more power cultivators, he had believed that Wang Lin (Xu Mu) was an old monster in disguise.

Yang Yanfeng later found out that Want Lin was no more than a mere junior at the Corporeal Yang stage, the fear of Wang Lin disappeared and instead was replaced with spite and hatred.

When Wang Lin later showed up at the Southern Domain 's Thunder Celestial tournament, Tang Yanfeng was the first to attack Wang Lin. However, Wang Lin had already become an early stage Nirvana Scryer and not at the Corporeal Yang as he thought, Tang Yanfeng was beaten miserably with one move. Afterward, a Tang elder showed hostile killing intent toward Wang Lin, Wang Lin activated the destruction energy that was left inside Tang Yanfeng's body and killed him on the spot.
