## Overview

Ancient Demons are categorized by their green skin, clawed hands, and twin horns. They are known for possessing extremely powerful spells that are usually of demonic nature such as Ye Mo's Demonic Spell: The Fiery Wind Turns Into A Mountain.

Like the other branches of the Ancient Clan , their strength is determined by the amount of stars they possess, which for them is found in their left eye.

In the Immortal Astral Continent they are the weakest individually, but middle as a nation. They are also known as the most united of the three branches.
