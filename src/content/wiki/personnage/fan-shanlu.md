## Background

In the past, she was a core disciple of Great Soul Sect but her elder sister, Fan Shanmeng , took her to become Su Dao's concubine.

Many years later, Su Dao and Fan Shanmeng stole a piece of the storage fragment from the Immemorial God Realm . However, during the trip back, Su Dao backstabbed Fan Shanmeng. He took her full cultivation to suppress the fragment and then destroyed her soul. However, she did not die and borrowed her teacher’s spell to tell Fan Shanlu everything. This news reached the members of her faction inside the Seven Dao Sect and thus the war began.

### Book Eleven

During the three years Wang Lin was guarding the Unnamed Wheel Formation , he contacted Fan Shanlu through the jade she gave him centuries ago when he met her in the vortex inside the Cloud Sea Star System .

After destroying the Outer Realm 's Ancient Celestial Realm , Wang Lin killed the Sovereign , once again, sealed the avatar souls of Dao Master Miao Yin and Devil Master Nine Heavens and gravely injured the Seventh Concubine . At this point, Seven-Colored Daoist appeared to reclaim Li Guang's Bow that he had "lent" to Wang Lin. However, Wang Lin immediately crushed the jade Fan Shanlu had given him and was instantly teleported to the vortex inside the Cloud Sea Star System.

There, he learnt about the demise of the Seven Dao Sect from Fan Shanmeng who entered Fan Shanlu's body through the use of a bloodline spell. Fan Shanmeng then lured him onto the Soul Devil Ship where she him put under her Great Soul True Illusion spell. Even Fan Shanlu was put under that spell so that Wang Lin may not notice any flaws. However, he still managed to realize the reality and broke through the spell. Fan Shanlu simply found it impossible to believe that her sister could jeopardize her, as she did.

She left for the Immortal Astral Continent when Wang Lin opened the Cave's exit.

### Book Twelve

She helped Wang Lin enter the hall close to the Great Soul Sect's main earthen fire vein, even at the cost of compromising herself. She did this as she felt a closeness to Wang Lin that she didn't feel to anyone on the Immortal Astral Continent; this was because she had spent most of her life in the Cave which made the Immortal Astral Continent seem like a foreign land.
