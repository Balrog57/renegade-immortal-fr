## Appearance

He wears beast skin clothing and has huge piercings throughout his entire face. He has three needles on his lips that he refined from the three Great Desolation Celestials. They contain a deadly poison.

### Background

His cultivation is quite powerful but his poison cultivation is even more powerful and it garnered him a lot of fame. He has a rivalry with the Second Generation Vermillion Bird Divine Emperor .

### Book Nine

He appeared at the Fallen Land during the Fallen Council competition. He taunted and challenged the Second Generation Vermillion Bird Divine Emperor to a battle. Afterwards, he left.

Soon after, he arrived at the Ancient Order Tomb. Due to not having any avatars, he forcefully threw multiple cultivators inside the Tomb and finally possessed the one that qualified to enter the Tomb.

Wang Lin and he worked together to kill Dao Master Miao Yin 's avatar inside the Tomb. He possessed the latter's body while Wang Lin stole almost 30% of his Joss Flames to upgrade his ancient slave, Ling Dong .

Subsequently, the two were caught by an Ancient God hand and transported further inside the Tomb. This gave Wang Lin a chance to escape from Esteemed Great Desolation as while he seemed like a nice guy, he was merely working together with Wang Lin as they had a common goal.

After Wang Lin's stone platform reached the 9th level, a giant arm rushed towards, crushing everything between itself and the platform. Esteemed Great Desolation's avatar received a direct hit and was instantly destroyed along with his soul.

Enraged, he entered the Ancient Tomb with his original body after the expulsion fog retracted. However, he ended up trapped inside the Tomb after Wang Lin closed it.

### Book Eleven

Over a century later, Sovereign begged Seven-Colored Daoist to allow Dao Master Miao Yin, Devil Master Nine Heavens and Esteemed Great Desolation to escape the Tomb so that they could destroy Wang Lin's Unnamed Wheel Formation and draw his remaining two arrows from Li Guang's Bow .

The three appeared to kill Wang Lin but were intercepted by Dao Master Blue Dream . After three days of battle, Esteemed Great Desolation was injured the most and escaped using a secret spell. His injuries were so bad that his lifespan fell to just 10 years.
