## Background

In the past, she was a part of the Seven-Colored Sect . She was deployed to the Ancient Celestial Realm. She supposedly opened the seal on the Seven Colour Sect's Cave World and allowed cultivators from the Immortal Astral Continent which ultimately resulted in Su Dao 's death and current chaos between the Outer and Sealed Realm .

### Book Eight

Wang Lin first saw her when she was being used as a corpse puppet by the Cultivation Alliance during the war between the Alliance Star System and the Allheaven Star System . He stole her from the Alliance's control during the chaos of the war.

Later, he had her guard him while he cultivated to reach the late-stage of Nirvana Scryer before returning to the Demon Spirit Land .

When Wang Lin encountered the Flower Imperial Concubine Han Yan , she spoke for the first time and her advise allowed Wang Lin to placate the Flower Imperial Concubine. She even gave him the location of Qing Lin 's seal.

### Book Nine

When Wang Lin entered the Seven-Colored Realm inside the Cloud Sea Star System , he fooled Master Ashen Pine and the others and secretly followed them. He broke through the imitation Life and Death Restriction placed by Old Woman Zhao with the help of the Woman in Silver who was able to use the authentic Life and Death Restriction. He tried to soul-search her to understand her origin but was blocked from doing so by Su Dao's seal.

In the Outer Realm , Wang Lin obtained Su Dao's statue from Greed . When he showed the statue to the Woman in Silver, she had a mental breakdown and passed out in sheer terror while yelling, "I didn't open the door!"

### Book Twelve

Immediately after Wang Lin took Lian Daofei and Woman in Silver out of his storage space after entering the Immortal Astral Continent , Lian Daozhen sensed Lian Daofei's aura and teleported the latter to him. He was surprised that she was with Lian Daofei but thought that she could become his maid.
