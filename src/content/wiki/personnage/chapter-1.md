## Chapter 1: Leaving Home introduces Wang Lin, affectionately nicknamed Tie Zhu due to his frail health as a child. He hails from the Wang family, a respected lineage of carpenters in his village. His father, being the second son born to a concubine, was unable to inherit the family trade and chose to settle in the countryside instead.

Despite his weak body, Wang Lin is regarded as a prodigy for his sharp intellect and passion for reading. His parents place great hopes in him, believing he is destined for more than a simple village life. While other children toil in the fields, Wang Lin spends his days immersed in study, dreaming of the vast world that lies beyond his humble home.
