## Background

Once, he went on a rampage and extinguished 13 cultivation families.

### Book Seven

He fought against the Moongazer Serpent , alongside Master Flamespark , Ancestor Shengong , Xiang Yundong , Five-Color Daoist , Blood God , and the old man from Gongsun Family. In the end, they managed to seal the Moongazer Serpent with the help of Master Lu Fu .

### Book Eight

He was the commander during the Northern Battle between the Alliance Star System and Allheaven Star System due to his control over the 18 Lou Fu.

### Book Ten

After learning his ancestor Zhan Xingye 's fate from Wang Lin , he buried the grudge between them and became extremely eager to fight with the Outer Realm , awakening his bloodline's Battle Will in the process.
