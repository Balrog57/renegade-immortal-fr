## Appearance

He looks pale with sunken eyes and gives off a frightening aura.

### Background

In the past, after the events of the Heavenly Madness , Qing Lin was survived with injuries and escaped to his Celestial Spirit Heaven Realm. However, there he found an Ancient Devil and an Ancient Demon waiting to ambush him. He split the ancient demon into nine parts and trapped the ancient devil inside his bottle world before rushing to the fifth cave to enter closed-door cultivation to recuperate.

### History

Wang Lin fought against a scattered devil of Ta Jia in the Ancient Battleground close to Fire Demon Country . Bei Lou helped him trap the scattered devil.

Many years later when Wang Lin returned to the East Demon Spirit Sea and opened the fifth cave, Ta Jia made an appearance. He was hell-bent on killing Wang Lin to absorb the latter's Ancient God bloodline.
