## Background

In the past, he made a deal with Lord of the Sealed Realm to fight for the Sealed Realm when the Realm-Sealing Formation is breached.

### Book Ten

While Wang Lin was battling Daoist Water , he appeared along with Master South Cloud to spectate. However, when Daoist Water attempted to self-destruct the Golden Bell and destroy the Cloud Sea Star System , he rushed to stop him.

He was the first to accept and proclaim Wang Lin as the new Lord of the Sealed Realm.

After the Outer Realm cultivators breached the Realm Sealing Formation, he appeared to battle them. He killed thousands of Outer Realm cultivators in an instant and almost killed Esteemed Nan Zhao . After learning of his identity, Master Enlighten East , Fire Sparrow Clan Ancestor , two other Nirvana Void cultivators and, even, Esteemed Nan Zhao attacked him together.

For some time Master Hong Shan managed to stall the five until he fell in Grandmaster Yun Luo 's trap and was sneak-attacked by the Sixth Concubine. Furthermore, Ice Boy appeared to continue holding him back. This allowed the Fire Sparrow Clan Ancestor to leave and confront Wang Lin who was rushing to kill Grandmaster Yun Luo. However, the former was easily sliced in half by the Heaven-Splitting Axe in Wang Lin's possession.

Ice Boy decided Wang Lin had to be stopped and forcibly sent Esteemed Nan Zhao and a Nirvana void cultivator from Bai Lei Clan out of the encirclement to kill Wang Lin. However, Wang Lin retaliated with a poison sneak-attack upon Esteemed Nan Zhao that resulted in the latter's death. The Nirvana void cultivator from Bai Lei Clan was too terrified to attempt to block Wang Lin and escaped.

After Wang Lin was injured by Li Guang's Boy and escaped along with the Nether Beast, the war turned for the worse for the Sealed Realm. Before the Sealed Realm's Third Step cultivators could destroy the Seven-Colored Realms , the Outer Realm waged another war. Master Hong Shan was ambushed in the the Seven-Colored Realm in the Summoned River Star System and was sealed inside a Dao Fruit.

A few decades later, Wang Lin appeared along with Qing Shui and Master Long Pan and freed Master Hong Shan from the Dao Fruit. Moreover, they also managed to capture the Heaven Punishment Palace Lord Qing Jiuyan .
