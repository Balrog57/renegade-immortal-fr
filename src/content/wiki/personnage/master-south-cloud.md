## Book Eight

He saved three of the Corpse Sect's Eight Kings when they entered the Land of the Ancient God inside the Sea of Devils after hearing about it from Wang Lin . Meanwhile, the other five of the Eight Kings died to Tuo Sen .

### Book Ten

After Wang Lin returned from the Outer Realm , he executed the traitor Daoist Water . During that time, Daoist Water attempted to destroy the Golden Bell he received from the Ancient Celestial Realm to destroy the Cloud Sea Star System and escape to the Outer Realm. Master South Cloud, Master Hong Shan , Zhou De , Cloud Edge and Esteemed Great Void attempted to stop him but could not. Just when they began despairing, Wang Lin's Nether Beast appeared and swallowed the golden storm energy from the destroyed Golden Bell.

After the Outer Realm cultivators breached the Realm Sealing Formation, he appeared to battle them. While Master Hong Shan distracted most of the enemy Third Step cultivators, he attempted to repair the breach on the Realm Sealing Formation. He borrowed Zhou Jin and Ling Dong from Wang Lin to guard him while he did so.

Master South Cloud was assisted by the reinforcements from Summoned River Star System led by Purple Dream who held off the Sixth Concubine while he repaired the Realm Sealing Formation.

After Wang Lin was injured by Li Guang's Boy and escaped along with the Nether Beast, the war turned for the worse for the Inner Realm. Before the Inner Realm's Third Step cultivators could destroy the Seven-Colored Realms, the Outer Realm waged another war. Master South Cloud was injured by Sovereign and was forced to go into closed-door cultivation for decades.

### Book Eleven

72 years after Wang Lin was ambushed by Sovereign's Fishing the Moon in the Well , Master South Cloud led the remnants of the Alliance Star System to battle against the Outer Realm. The battleground was near Planet Suzaku where the Inner Realm cultivators fought to defend the Lord of the Inner Realm's homeland.

The battle was in a deadlock with the Outer Realm's Zhou Jin and Ice Boy facing off against Master South Cloud, Situ Nan, Master Lu Fu and Master Long Pan until the Heaven Master Void God's avatar appeared. This immediately tilted the balance of power against the Inner Realm cultivators and forced them to retreat inside the formation of Planet Suzaku. When all hope was lost, Wang Lin triggered the Great Void Gate and appeared to lift the spirits of the weary Inner Realm cultivators.
