## Appearance

She has a great like for anything associated with the color "red" as she wears red clothes and her residence is also made of deep red stones.

### Background

The young master of Purple Dao Sect , Rudy , attempted to strong-arm her into a becoming his cultivation partner in the past. However, his true intentions were to use her as a cultivation furnace.

He went as far as storming the Origin Sect to kidnap her but he and his attendant, Song Wude , were kicked out by the previous Ancestor of the Origin Sect.

### History

Rudy and Song Wude attacked the Origin Sect after hearing the news of its Old Ancestor passing away only to end up offending Wang Lin and dying a meaningless death.
