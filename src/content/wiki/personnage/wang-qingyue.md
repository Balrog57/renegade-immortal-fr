## Plot

Duanmu Ji ask his help with his Five Element Escape Technique that useful for first trial of Land of Ancient God . After arrive at Chaotic Broken Stars, while waiting the others to arrive he tried to battle his clone that generated form Chaotic Broken Stars formation that always a minor realm higher than the intruder. On stone circular pillars that lead to first trial he escaped with Duanmu Ji and separated with others. They are seen again on third trial struggling with Wandering Souls and captured by revived and demonized Hunchback Meng. He then thrown them into Blood Sea, there they become a demon by Tuo Sen .
