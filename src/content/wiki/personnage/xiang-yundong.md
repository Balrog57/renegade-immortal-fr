## Appearance

Xiang Yundong has a gentle face but with a pair of ruthless eyes. He wears a blood red robe.

### History

He took Wang Lin to Planet Dong Lin and allowed him to be deceived by the Xiang Family ancestor to have almost all of his life force sucked away without even reviving Li MuWan .
