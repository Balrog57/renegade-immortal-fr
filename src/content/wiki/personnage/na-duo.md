## Appearance

He is the 1st external cultivator Wang Lin encounter on Planet Suzaku . He was injured and cultivated for 10000 years in the War God Shrine's Divine Path Cave to be able to recover 30% of his power, Early stage Soul Transformation , to have a chance to return home.

He's the first person with a Ji Realm , Five Colored Ji Realm, that Wang Lin encounter. He explain to Wang Lin how to break past the Ji Realm's bottleneck, to use a Cultivation Planet Crystal. He's surprised by Wang Lin, because he didn't think a waste planet like Suzaku could have a Ji Realm cultivator and a Divine Retribution inducing treasure.

Later, Wang Lin learned from Dun Tian , the ancestor of the Soul Refining Sect , that Na Duo was one of the foreign cultivators who fought with Situ Nan for the Heaven Defying Bead .
