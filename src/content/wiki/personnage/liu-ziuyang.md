## Book Twelve

He was part of the assault force to take control of the Third Meridian among the Heavenly Bull's Seven Meridians . He, along with three other early-stage Void Tribulant cultivators, fought against the Heavenly Bull Continent's three early-stage Void Tribulant cultivators that included Yan Lu and Xu Dongde . While he retreated to heal during the first skirmish between the two sides, he was ambushed by Wang Lin who attacked him with quick barrage of attacks that left him unable to defend himself. Even his life-saving treasure given to him by the Devil Dao Sect's Sect Master was unable to save him as he perished.
