## Appearance

She is a pretty and lively looking girl. While not as eye-catchingly gorgeous as Liu Mei, she still attracted quite a lot of attention from men

### Book One

She had a decent talent for cultivation and was among the best of her generation. She was friendly towards Wang Lin despite him being an outcast.

### Book Ten

Wang Lin has a dream involving her and Xu Fei after he consumes three Dao Fruits to complete his Life and Death, Karma and True and False Essences. He encounters her in the rain and is welcomed onto her boat. Sensing some familiarity, Zhou Rui uses a spell to let him rest.

### Trivia

Her full name, Zhou Rui, is revealed in a dream sequence in Book Ten, a full eight books since her last physical appearance in the story.
