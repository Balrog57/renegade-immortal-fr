## Background

Teng Four’s cultivation was not high. He was only in the early stage of Nascent Soul. After all, he had only received the position of core family member 20 years ago.

### History

He felt very grief stricken because of the family members that had died. One of them was his own younger brother. He left his hidden chamber to get revenge for his little brother. Unfortunately, this was a quest for vengeance he could never complete, because the current him didn’t even notice the cloud above him become more dense.
