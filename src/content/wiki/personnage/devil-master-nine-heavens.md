## Background

In the past, he stormed the Fallen Land but was forced to run with his tail tucked between his legs after facing off against the Vermillion Bird Ancestor . He was seriously injured and is still forced to remain in closed-door cultivation to this day, much like Sovereign .

### Book Nine

He entered the Ancient Order Tomb. He was fastest person to get his stone platform to the 7th level before he was overtook by Wang Lin .

After Wang Lin's stone platform reached the 9th level, a giant arm rushed towards, crushing everything between itself and the platform. Devil Master Nine Heaven's three-in-one avatar received a direct hit and he attempted to fight back against it. Although he still lost his body, his avatar's destruction gave him enough time to barely escape with his soul.

Enraged, he entered the Ancient Tomb with his original body after the expulsion fog retracted. However, he ended up trapped inside the Tomb after Wang Lin closed it.

### Book Eleven

Over a century later, Sovereign begged Seven-Colored Daoist to allow Dao Master Miao Yin , Devil Master Nine Heavens and Esteemed Great Desolation to escape the Tomb so that they could destroy Wang Lin's Unnamed Wheel Formation and draw his remaining two arrows from Li Guang's Bow .

The three appeared to kill Wang Lin but were intercepted by Dao Master Blue Dream . After three days of battle, Devil Master Nine Heaven’s was heavily injured as his Joss Flame Realm was destroyed which injured his mind and resulted in his cultivation level falling to early-stage Arcane Void .

Later, he sent his avatar to kill Wang Lin after the latter used up both his Li Guang's Bow arrows to seal the Outer Realm's Ancient Celestial Realm only to have its body destroyed and origin soul captured by Wang Lin.

Afterwards, Wang Lin sought out Devil Master Nine Heavens in the eastern part of the Ancient Primordial Star System on a crescent-shaped planet. This planet had an irregular shape that was rare and was filled with death aura. The planet was covered in yellow sand. There was no day on his planet, only night. Due it being incomplete, there was no grass or trees, only large rocks that rotated around the planet. Wang Lin sealed his soul and forced him to reincarnate in the Immortal Astral Continent so that he may not become a volatile factor inside the Cave after Wang Lin left.
