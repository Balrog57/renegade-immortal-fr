## Background

He found himself trapped in a Seven-Colored Realm by the Sovereign , who was afraid of his ancestor Old Ghost Zhan and wanted to assess whether or not he was dead.

He remained defiant to the end and this allowed him to form a powerful Battle Will.

### Book Nine

Wang Lin find his corpse inside a Seven Coloured Realm and inherited his Battle Will which later went to become one of his essences.

### Book Ten

Wang Lin informs his descendant Zhan Li Yunzi of his fate, allowing him to bury his grudge with him and Master Lu Fu .
