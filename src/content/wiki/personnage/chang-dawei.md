## Description

Chang Dawei (常大伟) is one of the Outer Region Managers of the Soul Refining Sect , operating alongside figures like Hong Maozi on Planet Suzaku . With a cultivation base at the early stage of Core Formation , he upholds sect laws and monitors the movements of outer disciples and newcomers. Known for his stern demeanor and rigid personality.He was an inner disciple, but because he remained in Core Formation for too long, he was demoted to a manager of the outer region. He hopes to increase his cultivation so he can return to the inner region of the sect. That is why, when Wang Lin breaks through to the late stage of Foundation Establishment in only three month, he becomes jealous. He thinks Wang Lin is cultivating a special treasure or practising a special technique. He then thinks about stealing Wang Lin's treasure so that he can use it to break through faster and return to the inner region, but he underestimates Wang Lin and is ultimately killed by him.
