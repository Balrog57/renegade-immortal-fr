## Trivia

His name "Zhu Quezi" means Vermillion Bird which means Suzaku which indicates that his "name" isn't actually a name but a title. But that begs the question about what is the story behind the name "Yun Quezi" (Zhu Quezi's younger brother) which means Cloud/Cloudy Bird.
