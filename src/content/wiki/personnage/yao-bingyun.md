## History

When she was young, her mother, the most talented person of her generation, was devoured by the ancient lamp 's spirit under Blood God 's control. Afterwards, her mother gave birth to her and her younger sister named Yao Mengyun .

Later, Blood God ordered Yao Yun to bring the two sisters to his hall and declared the two were to be refined into the ancient lamp's spirit. In the end, only her younger sister was refined while she was spared. She vowed that she would save her sister one day.

She took part in the pursuit against Wang Lin in hopes of asking for Yao Mengyun's return as a reward for killing Wang Lin.
