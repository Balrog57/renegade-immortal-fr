## History

He was the First Generation's Ancestor of the Dong Lin sect. Before he fell, he was known as the number one Grand Empyrean in the Celestial Clan , with even the much older Grand Empyrean Jiu Di dreading him. He was also the only one to reach the 19th palace of the Empyrean Exalt trial as an Ascendant Empyrean.

At one point in his life, he journeyed to Mount Gu Dao in the Ancient Clan's territory. The full details of what transpired are unknown, but it was stated that Dong Lin attempted to ascend the mountain (Potentially to challenge Gu Dao). Although he failed to reach the top, he still impressed Gu Dao and was acknowledged by him as the only Celestial to ever attempt the challenge.

He died sometime after this, with his body unable to be found even by other Grand Empyreans.
