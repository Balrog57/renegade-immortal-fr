## Personality

For whatever reason he was not much fond of anything foreign to the Immortal Astral Continent. He sealed 72 such entities away during his lifetime and together with the Ancient Ancestor he established the Law of the Immortal Astral Continent, which kills anyone who tries to enter the continent but lacks the proper bloodline, this includes Cave World Cultivators.

### History

At least once, he attempted to seal or enslave the Ancient Clan Ancestor. He failed, but this resulted in the Ancient Clan Ancestor hating all Celestials, a hatred he passed down his bloodline. Originally, the Celestials had only one continent, however the Celestial Ancestor created the 72 Celestial Continents by sealing away beasts, cultivators and entities that were foreign to the Immortal Astral Continent. It is stated in many dubious legends that his sword was destroyed in at least one such battle, however these legends are numorous and contradicting.

He laid down rules for the Celestial Clan to ensure they remained free, for he believed that's how they should be. Hence he ensured that the Imperial Family would only intervene in their lives to a small degree if any. As such, the various Celestial Clan Emperors over the eons have rarely given out edicts, but when such an edict is given disobedience is seen as betrayal of the entire Celestial Clan.

He created the Empyrean Exalt Trial to help those at the peak of Void Tribulant go further, together with his rival, the Ancient Ancestor, who did something similar independantly, they then made a single superior trial.

After his death, his bodyparts appear to be scattered, his head was with the Celestial Emperor while at least his hand was fused with the Mountain Sea Continent's Mountain Sea Tree Seal.

His head was later taken by Wang Lin along with Lian Daozhen's soul.

### Trivia

In Book 5 - AWWP , it is revealed that he once walked the path of The Immortal and this most likely was the reason for his death at the hands of Allheaven . Therefore, it can be said that he was one of those who could become The Immortal .

In Book 6 - BT , there is information that he drew inspiration for the creation of the Eight Extreme Daos from a totem that depicted a crystal broken into 10 pieces, in which he saw the moment of the birth of the Star Ring.
