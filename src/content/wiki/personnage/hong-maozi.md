## Description

Hong Maozi (红毛子) is the Outer Region Manager of the infamous Soul Refining Sect on Planet Suzaku . With a cultivation base at the early stage of Core Formation , he oversees operations and discipline among the lower-ranking disciples and cultivators in the sect’s periphery. His appearance is marked by a robe glowing with crimson spiritual light and a ferocious, fiery aura surrounding him, reflecting the brutal and soul-centric practices of the sect. While not a top-tier powerhouse himself, his loyalty to the sect's cruel hierarchy and his role in enforcing its dark laws make him a dangerous figure for new cultivators entering its domain.

He refused to let Wang Lin become a disciple of Soul Refining Sect , saying the period for that was long over and he should come back in three years. However, he allows Wang Lin to join the Soul Refining Sect after Wang Lin bribes him, He gives him a treasured sword.
