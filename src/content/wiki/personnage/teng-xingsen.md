## Background

He was the family head of the Teng Clan and reached the Nascent Soul stage in the last 500 years.

He was also Wu Feng Valley’s minister, so his position was very high. Because the Teng Family had an ancestor like him, no one dared to mess with them, even though they weren’t a big sect.
