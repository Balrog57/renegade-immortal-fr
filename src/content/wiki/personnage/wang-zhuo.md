## Personality

At the beginning of the story, he was arrogant and contemptful of Wang Lin due to his superior talent, however, he is ultimately humbled when Wang Lin proves to be far more powerful than he is after four years at the Heng Yue Sect.

He matures well into a calmer and more responsible man, but his heart is filled with hatred when the Wang Clan is exterminated, as well as self-loathing and humiliation at his failure to avenge them and his marriage into the Teng Family who's patriarch is responsible for the purge. He ultimately kills his own wife and then commits suicide to prove his loyalty when Wang Lin returns to purge the Teng Family.

### Background

Cousin to Wang Lin who he has a bad relationship with and acts as his rival. He was accepted into Heng Yue Sect during the first test.

Unlike Wang Lin, he was born with talent as is shown by the fact that it took him 3 months to reach 1st Layer of Qi Condensation . Because of his natural talent, the Wang Family received 2 other slots to have their youngsters join the Heng Yue Sect. These two spots eventually went to Wang Hao (son of 3rd Uncle) and Wang Lin.

After his first death, Wang Lin took his soul and made him take over the body of a not yet born baby from his Fourth Uncle's descendant.
