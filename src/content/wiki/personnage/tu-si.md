## Background

He was at the peak of eight stars. He cultivated the dangerous Flowing Ink Transformation Divine Technique in an attempt to break through to the legendary 9-star stage which allowed his inner devil, Tuo Sen , to take control of his body.

He left his legacy and inheritance inside the Sea of Devils on Planet Suzaku in the Chaotic Broken Stars' Plain called Land of the Ancient God.

He created numerous treasures like the God Slaying Spear or the Trident of All Seer .

### History

Wang Lin entered the Land of the Ancient God and managed to get half of Tu Si's inheritance of knowledge.

### Trivia

Tu Si (and by effect Wang Lin) has the "Royal" Ancient God bloodline but this is only because his lineage comes from Ye Mo (who was a complete member of the Ancient Clan before it split into the god, demon and devil clans). However, if Tu Si goes outside the cave world then his bloodline is common, at best. Moreover, the bloodline of the ancient gods ( Lou Chen ), ancient demons ( Bei Lou ) or ancient devils ( Ta Jia ) inside the Sealed Realm could only be considered inferior in the Immortal Astral Continent where the true Ancient Clan exists.

### Warning Spoiler

He fused with Tuo Sen when Tuo Sen obtained the Full Inheritance and succeeded in cultivating the Flowing Ink Transformation Technique to attain 9 Star.
