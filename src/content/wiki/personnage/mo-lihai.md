### Description

Mo Lihai is a demon general in the Sky Demon Country, known for his significant role in the demon general competition and his close association with Wang Lin.

Mo Lihai is confident in his abilities and driven by ambition. His goal is to place among the top three in the demon general competition, a position that would grant him entry into the demon temple for cultivation and promotion to Vice Commander in Chief. To strengthen his chances, he seeks Wang Lin’s assistance, recognizing Wang Lin’s extraordinary strength and insight.

He maintains a close and respectful relationship with Wang Lin, frequently interacting with him and even inviting him to stay at his mansion to avoid the chaos of the capital during the competition. Mo Lihai values Wang Lin’s companionship and trusts him deeply, showing both camaraderie and deference.Among his subordinates and peers, Mo Lihai commands great respect. When he and Wang Lin arrive in Hong City, nineteen soldiers kneel to greet him. After Mo Lihai introduces Wang Lin as his good friend and vice general, the same honor is extended to Wang Lin, reflecting Mo Lihai’s influence and the loyalty he inspires.

As a warrior, Mo Lihai wields formidable combat techniques, including the Ten Collapse Fist Intent and the Hundred Wave Demon Sea . He also possesses a secret spell called Burning Demon , personally taught by the Demon Emperor. Despite his strength, he once suffered a defeat against another demon general, Mo Fei, an event that left a deep impression on him and fueled his determination to improve.

Cautious and strategic by nature, Mo Lihai avoids unnecessary risks before the competition. He dedicates himself to closed-door cultivation, maintaining peak condition while setting up defensive formations and assigning guards to protect him from possible assassination attempts.His personality balances joviality and seriousness. He enjoys moments of friendship and relaxation with Wang Lin, sharing ancient wine and discussing cultivation techniques, yet he never loses sight of the heavy responsibilities and pressures he bears as a demon general.After his loss to Mo Fei , Mo Lihai places his hopes in Wang Lin to help him succeed in the upcoming competition, revealing his humility and deep trust in their alliance.
