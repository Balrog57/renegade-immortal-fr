## Overview

Kunpeng Zi is a cultivator from the Yellow Division of the Heavenly Fate Sect, known for his long-standing devotion to his Master, the All-Seer, and his role as one of the Heavenly Fate Seven Disciples the core representatives and true disciples of the sect.

### History

Kunpeng Zi was personally acknowledged by the All-Seer, who publicly recognized him as the Yellow Division’s true disciple. During this recognition, the All-Seer bestowed upon him a yellow crystal, symbolizing his position among the Seven Disciples and his status within the sect. The All-Seer further promised that if Kunpeng Zi could uphold the title of true disciple for1,000 years, he would be taught a low-quality celestial spell a rare and valuable form of divine cultivation art and be granted life-saving treasures as rewards.

Having followed the Master for over 2,000 years, Kunpeng Zi displayed deep respect and gratitude for this honor. Despite his humility, he was fully aware of the immense strength of his peers, especially Senior Apprentice Brother Yundao Zi , the oldest disciple of the Yellow Division. Yundao Zi had previously held the true disciple title for 800 years before it was taken by Kunpeng Zi’s Third Brother. Although Yundao Zi was reputed for his arrogance and aloofness, he had since grown indifferent toward competition, living a carefree and detached life within the sect.
