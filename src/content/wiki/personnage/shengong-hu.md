## Background

He is one of the most talented cultivators of his generation as he managed to reach the peak of Corporeal Yang in a mere millennia. He was also the first person in his generation to become a messenger of the Thunder Celestial Temple .

In the past, he only managed to last 15 seconds in the Thunder Celestial Temple's Trial Earth.

### History

He met Wang Lin inside the thunder pond in the Southern Domain of the Allheaven Star System. As Wang Lin had just looked past the door inside the heaven-defying bead, he mistook Wang Lin for a Third Step Cultivator and acknowledged him as his Lord. This led him to being isolated by his family and the Thunder Celestial Temple.

He was saved by Wang Lin from the mad Qing Shui inside the Thunder Celestial Realm .

He met Wang Lin during the qualifiers for the Celestial Bestowment Tournament. By this point, he had learnt that Wang Lin was not a Third Step Cultivator. Although he was no longer fanatic about Wang Lin, he still respected him greatly. He vouched for Wang Lin during the tournament.
