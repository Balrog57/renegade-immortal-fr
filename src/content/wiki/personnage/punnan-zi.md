## Personality

He has seen innumerable brushes with death in his life, imbuing him with a desire for strength.

### Background

500 years ago he was called to Zhao's Tower of Heaven alongside the country's other 22 Nascent Soul Cultivators of the time. They were ordered by a Rank 4 Country to enter the Foreign Battleground . During this period he personally saw the Heng Yue Sect 's Ancestor swallowed up completely by a Giant Demon Clan Cultivator who could not have looked more ordinary at a glance. 500 years later he emerged as the sole survivor.

### Nascent Soul of Punnan Zi outside Heng Yue Sect

After his sect lost to the Heng Yue Sect in their exchange, he showed up and personally seized Heng Yue Mountain. He stole the sect's disciples and any elders willing to join him. He also wanted to recruit Wang Lin , but Wang Lin refused him to protect the secrets on his body.

### Book Two

He is summoned to the Tower of Heaven alongside all the other Nascent Soul cultivators in Zhao in preparation for opening the Foreign Battleground. He later witnesses Wang Lin's fight with Teng Huayuan .

### Book Four

During the 400 years, the Heaven Tower’s Messager, Lin Yi, suppressed all the other first-rank sect and imposed a rule to help Teng Family cultivate a Soul Formation Expert under any means. He was forced to help the Teng Family, although he dislike them, because the Heaven Tower's Messenger wanted to experiment the creation of a Giant Demon cultivator with a cultivation technique (on Teng One, Teng Huayuan's little brother).

Punnan Zi shows up and saves Teng Huayuan by the order set by Heaven Tower Messager, Lin Yi. He shows his dislike in saving Teng Huayuan. Teng Huayuan tells him that if he didn’t, then his Xuan Dao Sect would be in deep trouble with Lin Yi. He manages to take a few of Wang Lin’s moves and was barely able to hang on until Lin Yi’s arrival. Which saves them.

At the end, when Wang Lin controls and was about bring down the wisp of Heaven Tribulation on Lin Yi. Lin Yi uses Punnan Zi as a shield and tries to escape back to his country, but Punnan Zi burns his lifespan energy to temporarily increase his strength to hold back Lin Yi, so he would be brought down with him. He tells Lin Yi that you want to return to your country, he will not allow that and the entire Country of Zhao will not allow that. Lin Yi manages to break free, but it was too late and they both ended up being struck by the wisp of Heaven Tribulation. Punnan Zi was glad that he was able to hold back Lin Yi, giving enough time for Lin Yi to be struck down. He dies with a smile on his face.
