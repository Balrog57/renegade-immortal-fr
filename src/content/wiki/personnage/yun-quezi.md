## Appearance

This person was wearing a red robe, his face was old, he gave off an ancient aura, and a powerful pressure could be felt from him.

### History

He chose Wang Lin as one of the four candidates for inheriting the title of next Suzaku. He gave him a straw hat that conceals the wearer's presence. This hat allowed Wang Lin experience the domains of Soul Formation cultivators which helped him breakthrough to Soul Formation.

After Wang Lin returned to Planet Suzaku during the war between the Allheaven Star System and the Alliance Star System , Yun Quezi followed him to the Immortal Graveyard. There, they reached the 17th floor when Huang Long stopped them from proceeding further.

When Wang Lin returned to Suzaku after being known as the Lord of the Sealed Realm, Zhou Wutai stated that Yun Quezi had passed away as his lifespan had reached its end.

### Trivia

He was appointed as the next Suzaku by Qian Pinghai , the 13th Suzaku. The title was stolen by Zhuque Zi . After he lost some kind of wager and he was sentences to a thousand years of exile.

There is some discussion as to his real cultivation level. Either he has a 10-Leaf cultivation or he is Middle stage Ascendant. He most likely has a normal Qi cultivation because he used to be the disciple of the Suzaku and it would've been hard to hide it from of him. He also is one of the only 2 members of the Forsaken Immortal Clan that have a domain with Mei Ji .
