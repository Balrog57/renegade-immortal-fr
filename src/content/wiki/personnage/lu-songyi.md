## History

Lu Songyi, Shi Fang , and a short elder of the Da Lou Sword Sect were looking for Brillian Golden Fruits. When they met Wang Lin , they decided to attack and injure Wang Lin but failed. Lu Songyi was interrogated by Wang Lin before he was killed.
