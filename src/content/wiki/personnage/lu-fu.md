## Background

He was a Celestial during the days of the Thunder Celestial Realm .

He survived where others died because he is extremely cautious and doesn't take risks.

### History

He first appears when Qing Shui was defending Wang Lin from the Yao Family Ancestor . [ 1 ]

He later fought against Tuo Sen during the war against the Alliance Star System .
