## Background

He was born as the child of a concubine and eventually his brothers, except for Fourth Uncle , conspired against him to steal his portion of the inheritance. After this, he set up a shop in town and became a carpenter.

### Book One

When Fourth Uncle offered Wang Lin a chance to join the Heng Yue Sect , he and his wife threw a party to celebrate. This tragically set up Wang Lin's deep sense of failure when he was not accepted and caused him to leave home. After this, Wang Tian Shui and Fourth Uncle forced Wang Tian Shan to ask the sect for help finding Wang Lin. They were quite happy when he got into the sect thanks to Fourth Uncle's bribes.

### Book Two

With the help of Fourth Uncle, Wang Tian Shui manages to regain his inheritance. He also takes on a few apprentices including Wang Lin's childhood playmate Little Six . He is later slaughtered, alongside the entire Wang Clan, by Teng Huayuan in retaliation for Wang Lin killing his great grandson. His soul was sealed into a soul flag and used as a weapon against Wang Lin. However, Wang Lin managed to retrieve his parents souls.

### Book Four

Finally accepting the reality that his parents are just mortals and allows their souls to enter the cycle of reincarnation.
