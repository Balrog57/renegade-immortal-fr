## Overview

Yundao Zi is the oldest disciple of the Yellow Division within the Heavenly Fate Sect , a long-standing cultivator who has followed the All-Seer for more than 2,000 years. Once a prominent figure among the sect’s disciples, he held the true disciple title for 800 years before it was taken by his Third Brother. Despite his strength and seniority, Yundao Zi is distinguished by his lack of competitive spirit and carefree nature, preferring peace and detachment over rivalry and ambition.

Physically, Yundao Zi is described as looking unusually thin, almost like a skinny monkey, giving him an eccentric and distinctive appearance. Though his outward form may appear frail, his cultivation and experience command respect among his peers.

During the selection of the Yellow Division’s true disciple, Yundao Zi voluntarily stepped forward and relinquished his claim to the position. He humbly acknowledged that the disciple most suited to bear the title was their Second Brother, Kunpeng Zi , who had received a celestial treasure from the All-Seer . This act reflected Yundao Zi’s humility and fairness, showing his willingness to yield to those he deemed more deserving rather than clinging to prestige.

When the All-Seer announced Kunpeng Zi as the new true disciple and revealed the yellow crystal a sacred symbol of the Heavenly Fate Seven Disciples Yundao Zi lowered his head respectfully and remained silent. However, at that moment, his eyes briefly revealed a trace of fear before returning to calmness, a subtle reaction noticed by Wang Lin, indicating the deep reverence and awe he felt toward his Master’s authority.

Though he once held great status within the sect, Yundao Zi’s true character lies in his tranquil acceptance, humility, and non-attachment to power. He embodies the image of an elder cultivator who, after centuries of experience, values inner peace over worldly recognition.
