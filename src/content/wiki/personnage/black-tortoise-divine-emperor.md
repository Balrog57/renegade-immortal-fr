## Background

During the war against the Cultivation Alliance and the Brilliant Void Cultivation Sect , the Black Tortoise Divine Emperor turned traitor which, along with the Azure Dragon Divine Emperor 's disappearance, led to the Four Divine Sect 's defeat and decline.

### History

Many years later, Wang Lin stormed the Cultivation Alliance to save Qing Shui and destroy the Seven-Colored Realm inside the Alliance headquarters. There, he saw 100,000 mountain peaks each with a cultivator on top, a Black Tortoise that could cover the entire area inside the Seven-Colored Realm with the Black Tortoise Divine Emperor sitting on its head, and Qing Shui sealed atop the highest mountain peak with a seven-colored finger pointing to his forehead.

After seeing all this, Wang Lin realized that the 100,000 mountains turned into 10 thorns to seal Qing Shui’s body. The cultivators on the 100,000 mountains used their blood to nourish the Black Tortoise and their killing intent to stimulate Qing Shui. This Black Tortoise was, in fact, alive and was being used to trap Qing Shui’s soul. The purpose of all this was to create the slaughter essence with Qing Shui being the furnace for creating the slaughter essence.

The Black Tortoise Divine Emperor betrayed the Four Divine Sect because the Cultivation Alliance managed to find the Black Tortoise body somewhere. The Black Tortoise was most likely one of the four great generals under the Ancient Celestial Emperor . It contained the aura of the Black Tortoise clan, and as a descendant, the Black Tortoise Divine Emperor had to betray the Four Divine Sect since only this kind of temptation would be enough for him to become a traitor.

Qing Shui came to the Seven-Colored Realm to find the reason for his madness. He must have found something that caused him to become crazy again. That seven-colored finger is the root of all of this. The red gas it absorbs is slaughter essence.
