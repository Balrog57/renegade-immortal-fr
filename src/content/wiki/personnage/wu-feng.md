## Wu Feng  is one of the  Celestial

Grand Empyreans and one of the Immortal Astral Continent 's Nine Suns . He is said to be extremely picky with allowing cultivators to follow him, rarely recruiting anyone below Ascendant Empyrean .

It has been said that he became a Grand Empyrean through the dao of martial arts.

### History

He travels to the Mountain Sea Continent with Dao Yi and Jiu Di and witnessed Wang Lin 's faith spell Sundered Night. Like Dao Yi , he was interested in recruiting Wang Lin , but was wary of his origin and wanted to confirm he was not the cultivator Xuan Luo had come to retrieve many years ago.

When Wang Lin passed the 10th palace of the Empyrean Exalt Trial , he personally came to recruit Wang Lin with his real body to show his sincerity.

When Wang Lin passed the 13th palace and was exposed by Lian Daozhen for using his soul armor, he backed up Wang Lin. He went against Lian Daozhen in order to gain the favor of Wang Lin, knowing that even if he failed in recruiting him he would at least be able to forge a good relationship with Wang Lin .

He later works together with Gemini, Jiu Di, and Dao Yi to stop Lian Daozhen from receiving the Celestial Ancestor 's inheritance. When Wang Lin takes the Celestial Emperor's soul, he receives the Celestial Ancestor's ears from him.
