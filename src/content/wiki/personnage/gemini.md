## She was originally the strongest  Celestial

Grand Empyrean , possessing strength that even Jiu Di was wary of. However, during her last reincarnation, her soul split into Han Han and Wa Wa and she lost much of her strength. Furthermore, her two halves were not yet mature enough to fully use their potential as a Grand Empyrean without causing irreparable harm to themselves.

Sometime ago, she employed the Heaven Sect 's Ancestor - Empyrean Exalt Mu Rong - who was from the same generation as Great Soul Sect's Luo Yunhai .

### Book Eleven

When Wang Lin broke through to the Third Step and destroyed the Cave World's Tribulation, he encountered Wa Wa when he projected his divine sense out of the Cave to the Immortal Astral Continent. (edit : Book Eleven chapter 1617, she said her name is "Doll"(WaWa))

### Book Eleven

Later, he encountered Han Han and Wa Wa, again, when he entered the Ancient One altar inside the spatial crack near the Cloud Sea . He also saw Greed with them when they inadvertently helped scare off Seven-Colored Daoist for him.

### Book Twelve

She aids Wang Lin greatly throughout his time in the Immortal Astral Continent . She is also the only Grand Empyrean to do so without any ulterior motive. Wang Lin recognizes this when the other Celestial Clan

Grand Empyrean 's come to recruit him and picks her solely due to this karma, much to her (Han Han and Wa Wa's) delight.

Gemini helps Wang Lin once again later on by engaging Lian Daozhen while he dealt with the Immortal Imperial Teacher . In order to combat the her fellow Grand Empyrean, she used a special technique that allowed Han Han and Wa Wa to fuse back into her original form. Though the fusion was unstable, it allowed her to contend with the Celestial Emperor until the unexpected arrival of Jiu Di , Dao Yi , and Wu Feng .

She was also the only Grand Empyrean to not be affected by the Ancestral Curse of the Celestial Ancestor as she chose not to fight Lian Daozhen with the others for the Celestial Ancestor's Head.

When Wang Lin pilfered the Celestial Ancestor 's head from Lian Daozhen after revealing that he was actually alive, he gave her the Celestial Ancestor's eyes. According to Jiu Di they would be able to fully restore the power she loss during her split and even make her stronger.

However, when Wang Lin demanded to take Lian Daofei she reluctantly opposed him along with the other Celestial Grand Empyrean 's. She informed him that Lian Daofei was needed to maintain the seal on the 72 Foreign Beasts that were sealed by the Celestial Clan and if he were not there, the entire Celestial Clan could be destroyed.

### When the other  Celestial Clan

Grand Empyrean 's go their separate ways to recover from the backlash of the Celestial Ancestor 's Ancestral Curse, she admits to Wang Lin that she knew about his ties to the Ancient Clan , to which the latter thanks her for doing so much for him despite knowing the truth.

### Book Thirteen

When Wang Lin completes his Grand Empyrean Sun, she celebrates in the form of Han Han and Wa Wa.

She later appears with the rest of the Nine Suns in her true form due to sensing the great battle between Wang Lin and Gu Dao . She agrees with Jiu Di 's request to pour their powers together to reverse time and witness the events that had transpired before their arrival, and is shocked to discover that Wang Lin had defeated Gu Dao and was now the strongest being in the Immortal Astral Continent .
