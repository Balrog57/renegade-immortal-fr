## Appearance

Teng One's whole body was black and there were no muscles in his body at all. On his head were only a few patches of hair. The whole person looked very terrifying. His eyes had a ghostly light.

### Background

Teng One was a legend in the Teng Family. He is the decendant of Teng Huayuan 's younger brother. He was the only core Teng family member that no one dared to challenge and the only person that held the name Teng One for the past 400 years.

### History

When Wang Lin used his Ji Realm on Teng One, Teng One was able to block it and only lost his arm. This shocked Wang Lin greatly because this was the first time the Ji Realm wasnt able to kill someone in the same realm. With Wang Lin's immense battle experience, he was able to out whit Teng One causing him to flee. Teng One realize that he was no match for Wang Lin. In his attempt to escape, Wang Lin hit him with a giant hand and destroyed him. He left a golden drop of blood behind. This golden blood turned out to have traces of the Ancient God 's bloodline.
