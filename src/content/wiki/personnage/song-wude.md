## Background

In the past, he was beaten by the previous Ancestor of Origin Sect when he attempted to kidnap Lu Yanfei at Rudy's behest.

### History

After hearing the news of the death of the previous Ancestor of Origin Sect, he stormed the place, once again, attempt to to kidnap Lu Yanfei. However, Wang Lin killed him before he could harm anyone.
