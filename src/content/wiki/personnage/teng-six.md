## Personality

Although his cultivation level was only Nascent Soul, his cultivation method allowed him to transform murderous aura. Since he transformed all of the murderous aura in his body, he gained a new ability. This ability has saved his life many times. He was a very decisive person.

### Background

Teng Six was not young. 200 years ago, he was already a core Teng Clan member, but had lost his title to a challenger. Luckily, he didn’t die and went into years of closed door cultivation. After he came out, he went to where the then Teng Six was cultivating and killed the challenger that beat him before. This was all done without the permission of Teng Huayuan .

### History

When Wang Lin was approaching him, he used a forbidden technique and burned his soul to fly faster and get away. During the fight, Wang Lin used Xu Liguoo and the 3rd devil appeared to protect his bronze mirror. Wang Lin sent out a black gas and completely devoured Teng Six's soul.
