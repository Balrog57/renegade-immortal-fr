## Background

He genius was from Puli’s Insect Demon Sect, and he was a late stage Nascent Soul cultivator. Then for some reason, he stole the sacred text of the Insect Demon Sect and betrayed his sect.Eventually, under the pursuit of a Soul Formation cultivator, Zhang Bai escaped into the Nine Ghost Desolate Valley. After escaping from the Soul Formation cultivator, he lived there for 100 years. No one knew how he had lived through those 100 years, but by the time he came out, he had reached the Soul Formation stage. What was even scarier was that he had tamed countless poisonous insects. He killed his way to the Insect Demon Clan. Surrounded by his mass of insects, even Soul Formation cultivators died to him. He was able to obtain a Rain Cauldron and become one of the people to enter the Celestial Realm.

### History

In the Celestial Realm, he traveled unopposed until he messed with someone he couldn’t mess with and failed to frame Wang Lin . In the end, he lost several clones before being forced to return using the Return Cauldron. He was the first person to from Planet Suzaku to return. When he returned, he landed in the Sea of the Devils and because he had lost too many insects, he began killing again in order to replenish his insects. He destroyed countless sects as he passed them and finally came to the Country of Zhao where he saw the Cloud Sky Sect . During his attack on the Cloud Sky Sect, he was beaten and killed when Wang Lin arrived.
