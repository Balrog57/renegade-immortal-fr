## Appearance

She was very beautiful, almost to a demonic level. Even though Liu Mei didn’t lose to her in appearance, she lacked an unspeakable feeling. She was like a flower of the world that would bloom and wither along with the world.

She was said to be above the rest of Qing Lin's seven concubines.

### Background

She was only Imperial Concubines who did not betray Qing Lin.

### History

She stopped Wang Lin and company from entering the fourth minor celestial cave in the East Demon Spirit Sea. She is far stronger than even the likes of Qing Shui and Master Flamespark .

She only allowed Wang Lin to enter after he used the Stop Spell and showed her Qing Shuang 's corpse.
