## History

Soon after Wang Lin left the Fallen Land and grievously wounded the Sovereign 's avatar at the Fire Sparrow Clan , she helped Zhou Jin track him down. The two stayed on Wang Lin's track as he ran towards the Ancient Order Tomb. On the way, Wang Lin killed all the members of a branch of the Esteemed Heavenly Wolf Clan which incited Zhou Jin's hatred. Wang Lin used that to assault the latter with his Ethereal Flame . He also used that opportunity to kill Feng Zhiman once and exhausted one of her three lives.

Subsequently, the duo accidentally entered the fog surrounding the Ancient Order Tomb which resulted in Feng Zhiman dying once again and being left with her last life. Luckily, her last life allowed her to gain the qualification to enter the Tomb. The two got separated after entering the Tomb. Feng Zhiman ended up being teleported to the same location as Wang Lin. She tried to unite the others there against Wang Lin but before she could do so, he displayed his might and cowed all of them. Later, Ling Dong's group appeared and tried to break open the restrictions sealing the rank 4 Dao Spirit which resulted in the entire group separating.

After leaving that location, Wang Lin found Dao Master Miao Yin and Esteemed Great Desolation fighting and he decided to lend a hand to the latter. Wang Lin struck like lightning and killed the Dao Master's followers and kidnapped his maid in an instant. Then, he worked in tandem with Esteemed Great Desolation to injure the Dao Master so severely that Esteemed Great Desolation managed to possess his avatar and Wang Lin managed to steal almost 30% of his Joss Flames to upgrade his ancient slave, Ling Dong .

Later, Wang Lin unsealed Feng Zhiman and learned the secrets of the Sealing Extermination Clan from her. He took her clan symbol and decided to release her after exiting the Tomb.
