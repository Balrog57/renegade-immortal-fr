## Book Seven

In the past, he was a mortal scholar that helped Wang Lin by accident in his search for enlightenment. Afterwards, he was accepted by Wang Lin as an honorary disciple but he only cultivated his soul. He has completely given up spells and only cultivates domain. This is a cultivation method that didn’t exist in this world. It is similar to how the ancient gods only cultivated their bodies, but Xie Qing only cultivated his domain.

### Book Ten

He doesn't have the ability to fight or any cultivation level but he is enlightened in the way of the heavens such that even Nirvana Scryer cultivators are inferior to him. His domain had reached the level of Nirvana Cleanser and he even caught a glimpse of laws.

### Book Eleven

While the war between the Inner and Outer Realm raged, he regained his memories from his previous reincarnation and understood his identity. So he escaped from Planet Qing Lin to another planet in Allheaven . He hid himself and pondered over his situation for over a century.

### Spoiler

Finally, he decided to commit suicide and give his soul to Wang Lin instead of letting it fall in the hands of Seven-Colored Daoist or Old Ghost Zhan . His death allowed Wang Lin to take in the third soul and open the core of the Cave World.
