## Appearance

He was a burly and well built man. He specialized in strength and not magic/spells. He fought with honor and didn't use any tricks or schemes.

### History

When Wang Lin was trying to escape the Forsaken Immortal Clan , Chi Mu caught up to him. He was very interested in Wang Lin because he believed Wang Lin was very strong and could be his match. However, he ended up being defeated when Wang Lin took out the God Slaying War Chariot and he was killed by the beast spirit.
