## History

He met Wang Lin when the latter had just entered Peng Lai . Wang Lin saw him trading celestial jades for origin crystals so he approached him to do business. In fact, Ouyang Long was just a proxy for his Uncle-Master, Master Ashen Pine .

He also invited Wang Lin to Master Ashen Pine's private auction on the latter's behalf.
