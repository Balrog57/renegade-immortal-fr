## Appearance

She wore a light purple, cotton dress. Her eyes shined like the stars and there was faint, plum-colored lipstick applied to her elegant lips. There were several shining butterfly discs on the right side of her forehead.

### History

She tricked Wang Lin into entering the Immortal Graveyard and attempted to trap him inside. Immediately, Wang Lin turned the tables on her and threw her into the void.

She was captured by Qian Feng and used as his cultivation furnace. Later, she managed to absorb Qian Feng 's cultivation at his death and escaped Suzaku.

She somehow ending up on Planet Five Elements. Soon after, she was chased out by the Heavenly Water Palace and reached the Blood Planet of Blood Ancestor while on the run. Subsequently, she was taken in as his concubine.

Afterwards, she went to investigate a dying planet under the Blood Ancestor's dominion. This planet was being occupied by Wang Lin's original body. She was scared off by him.

Later, Blood Ancestor harmed Wang Lin's avatar to the extent of sending it into a coma. Enraged, Wang Lin's original body attacked Blood Planet to get revenge. Zi Xin died when she tried to step in the passage inside the Blood Pavilion before Blood Planet destruction by having her body decaying and origin soul collapsing.
