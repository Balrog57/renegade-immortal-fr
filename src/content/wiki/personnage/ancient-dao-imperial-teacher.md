## Appearance

The Ancient Dao Imperial Teacher is noted to bear a physical resemblance to both his Cave world Counterpart and it's master Su Dao, he answers in turn that he is in some way both of them. After using Wang Lin's Slaughter Essence to open the Immemorial God Realm, he possesses ten colors instead of seven.

### Background

Although this All-Seer has forgotten his origins, he knows he was not always a Treasure Spirit and he wanted to find a way to escape and return home, as well as ask why he was condemned to such a fate to begin with.

Eventually, he saw a Heaven Trampling Cultivator grieve over his beloved and enter a Dao Dream to try and fix it. After numerous cycles, that Cultivator always woke up upon reaching the Boundary Compass. Eventually, that cultivator broke the Compass, thus freeing the All Seer, who from then on schemed to escape the Dream by devouring that Cultivator to seize his body in the real world.

His resemblance to Su Dao and the Cave World All Seer strongly imply that he was connected to them, in fact he notes that he can be called either one.
