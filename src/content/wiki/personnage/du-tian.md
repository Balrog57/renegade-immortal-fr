## Description

Dun Tian was a Soul Transformation cultivator of the Soul Refining Sect and the last ancestor of the Soul Refining Sect on Planet Suzaku before it was destroyed by the Fourth Generation Suzaku. At the peak of the Soul Transformation realm with the One Billion Soul Flag in his hand, he was able to fight against Ascendent cultivators.

He chose to help Wang Lin when he lost his base of cultivation by fighting and almost dying against Giant Demon Clan Ancestor and Li Yuan Feng because on the day of his senior brother Nian Tian’s death, Wang Lin just so happened to have arrived at the sect. He felt it was a coincidence that it happened and so he thought Wang Lin might be a sign and the person to help Soul Refining Sect become a Rank 6 Cultivation Country. Dun Tian helped him on the condition that he takes the position of sect leader and bringing the sect to new heights, giving him in addition Soul Refining Sect’s treasure, One Billion Soul Flag.

When he was younger, he went to Planet Tian Yun and cultivated there for a while. He admired how big Planet Tian Yun was and how much more spiritual energy and cultivation resources they had compared to Planet Suzaku. If he had the time, he would have gone there again and steal from the sects there.

The moment the fourth soul appeared, it released a blinding, golden-purple glow. Inside the glow was only one thing: a thin needle

The fourth soul was the Fourth Ancestor of the Soul Refining Sect. He was a rare person who had three gold elements as his spiritual roots. If a cultivator has one spiritual root, then they can cultivate. If a cultivator has two, then they have some talent. If a cultivator has three, then they are considered a genius. If a cultivator has four, then they are considered peerless. If a cultivator has five, then they are considered complete or unbeatable.

During Situ Nan reign, he broke through to Ascendent. When the four cultivators from outside of Planet Suzaku came and wanted the Heaven Defy Beard, he assisted Situ Nan and managed to kill one of them. But this resulted in the Fourth Ancestor being severely injured and leading to his death later on. The Soul Refining Sect refined his soul with a special technique that turned it into something that could threaten even late stage Ascendant cultivators.

### Appearance

He was wearing a blue robe with hair extending to his waist. This person’s face was grey and he looked very old, but his eyes shined like the stars.
