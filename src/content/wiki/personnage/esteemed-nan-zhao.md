## History

He attacked Wang Lin soon after the latter appeared. He forcibly teleported Wang Lin to his Joss Flame Realm where he tried to kill him. If not for Wang Lin's life-saving Ancient God spell, he would be dead. Soon after Wang Lin his spell, Master Hong Shan appeared, saved Wang Lin, and severely wounded Esteemed Nan Zhao. This forced the Fire Sparrow Clan Ancestor , Master Enlighten East , two other Nirvana Void cultivators and, even, Esteemed Nan Zhao to attack him together.

For some time Master Hong Shan managed to stall the five until he fell in Grandmaster Yun Luo 's trap and was sneak-attacked by the Sixth Concubine. Furthermore, Ice Boy appeared to continue holding him back. This allowed the Fire Sparrow Clan Ancestor to leave and confront Wang Lin who was rushing to kill Grandmaster Yun Luo. However, the former was easily sliced in half by the Heaven-Splitting Axe in Wang Lin's possession.

Ice Boy decided Wang Lin had to be stopped and forcibly sent Esteemed Nan Zhao and a Nirvana void cultivator from Bai Lei Clan out of the encirclement to kill Wang Lin. However, Wang Lin retaliated with a poison sneak-attack upon Esteemed Nan Zhao that resulted in the latter's death. The Nirvana void cultivator from Bai Lei Clan was too terrified to attempt to block Wang Lin and escaped.
