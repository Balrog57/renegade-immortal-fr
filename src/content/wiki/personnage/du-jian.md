## Background

In the past, he saw All-Seer devour Sun Yun through a mountain goblin. After he entered the Heavenly Fate Sect, he cultivated a technique that could absorb spiritual energy from beasts. he was in the back mountains when he found a mountain goblin that was nearing the end of its life. As he was absorbing its spiritual energy, he extracted some of its memories.

### History

He entered Tide Abyss inside the East Demon Spirit Sea along with Wang Lin , Murong Zhuo , Zhao Yixuan and Xu Fei (of the Corpse Sect ). He attempted to guide Wang Lin into a trap but pushed into the trap by Wang Lin and ended up with just a weak origin soul.

Afterwards, Wang Lin refined him into a Celestial Guard using Qing Lin 's technique. He offered him freedom after he worked for 1000 years. Alas, Du Jian self-exploded himself in a battle against Blood Ancestor to allow Wang Lin to escape.
