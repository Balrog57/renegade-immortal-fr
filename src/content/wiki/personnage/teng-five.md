## Appearance

Teng Five was a very handsome middle aged man. It could be said he looked very much like Teng Li from back then.

### Background

During the Teng Clann crisis, he was flying toward the main Teng city for shelter and protection. He was accompanied by two beauties.

### History

When he was only 10,000km away from the Teng Clan, he saw black clouds appear in the sky. When the black clouds descended, it killed him and took away his Nascent soul.
