## Description

It is one weakest among the six main continents of Planet Suzaku. There were countless cultivation countries inside Kong continent but most of them were Rank 1 or Rank 2 countries with some Rank 3 countries and even fewer Rank 4 countries. After all, the resources on this continent were too poor. The country of Zhao was at the very edge of the Kong continent. It was one of the smaller Rank 3 countries. In fact, it was only half the size of Hou Fen . Xue Yue is another Rank 4 cultivation country. They are at the northern border of the continent.
