## Background

When the Heaven Defying Bead appeared and divided the Celestial Realm into two, he remained, and still remains, within the inner realm's Celestial Realm in closed door cultivation. His nine concubines turned traitor and currently rule over the outer realm's Celestial Realm. The Lord of The Sealed Realm was his servant.

### Warning Spoiler

His real name is Su Dao , he is the master of the Seven Colour Sect and he is the Seven Coloured Immortal Venerate who created the Cave World they ruled over in the first place.

He stole a Heavenly Dao to breathe life into his cave and used every inhabitant to produce Joss Flames for him. He also created Divine Retribution to kill any Cultivator who has the will to defy his heaven.

When the door to the Immortal Astral Continent was opened the residents of the Cave World found themselves fighting against Celestial Clan

### Lian Daofei  and  Ancient Clan

During these events he and his sect were seriously damaged. He was forced to separate his soul into ten parts, which are three main souls (represented by his cultivation power , perception of the natural law and his memory ) and seven spirits including: Situ Nan , Mu Bingmei , Qin Lin , Qing Shui and Xie Qing alongside 2 others.

His sect was dismembered and those who survived used the body of Ye Mo as a means of rehabilitation.
