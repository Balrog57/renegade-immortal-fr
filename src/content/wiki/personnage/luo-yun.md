## Overview

Luo Yun cultivated the path of the Heavenly Ghost, and possessed his own ghost spirit, which he used both for combat and protection. He encountered Wang Lin within the East Demon Spirit Sea, this area was known for harboring ancient demons and serving as a deadly trial ground that opened every 5,000 years. Luo Yun possessed a demon spirit crystal, an object of immense value that drew Wang Lin’s attention.

During their confrontation, Wang Lin performed a soul search on Luo Yun’s origin soul, causing him unbearable pain and rendering his soul almost transparent. Wang Lin then reached into Luo Yun’s dantian to seize the demon spirit crystal. Despite Luo Yun’s warnings that his master would seek revenge, Wang Lin remained unshaken and ultimately killed him, sealing his origin soul within a soul flag.

Before his death, Luo Yun revealed key information about the Heavenly Ghost Sect and its dark rituals. He explained that the sect’s ghosts were created using the blood of mortal pregnant women and the essence of powerful men, with the cultivator’s Nascent Soul serving as the medium. The ritual took forty-nine years to complete, binding the heavenly ghost to its master for life. Luo Yun’s own ghost was the weakest of nine such entities, and due to incomplete refinement, it betrayed him in the end.
