## History

Shi Fang, Lu Songyi and a short elder from the Da Lou Sword Sect came to search for the Brilliant Golden Fruit. He was the head elder in the group. When they spotted the Brilliant Golden Fruits, they saw Nine Multitude Bugs surrounding the fruit and guarding it. However, in order to lure the bugs away, he decided that they would use the younger generation they met to be the bait. When they saw Wang Lin obtain the Brilliant Golden Fruit, his eyes were full of greed and he planned to take it away from Wang Lin. Shi Feng's group attached Wang Lin, they knew they weren't his match, but he didn't think Wang Lin would battle them to the death because they thought Wang Lin would have to expend a lot of energy to fight the three elders. When Wang Lin destroyed his body, his soul escaped and then possessed Guo Xingyi . In fear, he tried to reason and plea with Wang Lin, but Wang Lin still ended up killing him.
