## Description

The Celestial Brush is a supreme celestial treasure imbued with vast celestial spiritual energy and heavenly dao. Appearing as a golden ink brush, it releases radiant golden light when activated, carrying an overwhelming sense of majesty that commands respect even from celestials.

Its true power lies in its ability to draw celestial symbols and spells, with each stroke requiring the wielder to split a portion of their origin soul.These strokes act as anchors for celestial intent, and when seven strokes are completed, they fuse into a complete celestial spell symbol capable of exerting immense pressure and altering the surrounding environment. The appearance of an eighth stroke represents a qualitative transformation, marking a higher level of mastery and spell completion.

Wang Lin obtained the Celestial Brush after defeating an old cultivator who had acquired it in the Celestial Realm and used it to enlighten lower-realm cultivators. Upon taking possession of the brush, Wang Lin quickly comprehended its structure and celestial energy flow, surpassing its former owner in mastery within a short time. His use of the brush demonstrates not only his strength, but also his exceptional intelligence and adaptability in controlling high-level celestial treasures.

As a core celestial artifact, the Celestial Brush is both powerful and dangerous. Improper use risks severe damage to the origin soul, while true mastery allows the wielder to inscribe spells that embody heavenly dao itself
