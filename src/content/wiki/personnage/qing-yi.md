## Overview

Qing Yi shares a deep and affectionate bond with Wang Ping . Their relationship develops gradually, with Qing Yi willingly accompanying him through hardship and uncertainty.

Wang Ping, in turn, shows her great tenderness and affection, and their connection eventually matures into a lifelong companionship. Out of devotion, Qing Yi chooses to age alongside Wang Ping , wishing to share his mortal years rather than seek immortality alone.

Throughout her life, Qing Yi faces numerous challenges — including being expelled from the Sun family and captured by others. Nevertheless, Wang Lin supports her and vows to help her reach the Nascent Soul stage .

With Wang Ping’s protection and Wang Lin’s guidance, Qing Yi continues her cultivation journey, overcoming adversity through perseverance and love.

After years of struggle, Qing Yi grows older yet retains her gentle and sentimental nature. She values emotion and companionship over ruthless ambition, expressing a desire to remain “a person of feeling” rather than a cold and detached celestial.

Her story concludes peacefully — she and Wang Ping settle in a quiet mountain village , living out their remaining years together in serenity and mutual affection.
