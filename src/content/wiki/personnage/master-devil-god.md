## Personality

He is an extremely cautious man to point of being timid. He never does anything which he does not have 100% confident of completing. This results in him never leaving behind any tracks of his presence.

### Background

Back on his cultivation planet, he killed hundreds of thousands of cultivators over thousands of years before he left. The most startling thing was that none of the victim's families knew that he was one who killed their family member.

### History

He layed-low during Qing Shui and Wang Lin 's assault on the Slaughter Realm. He only acted when he saw Wang Lin being injured by Master Empty Fantasy . Alas, he was killed by Qing Shui's Ji Realm .
