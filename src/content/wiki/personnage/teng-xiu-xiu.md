## Background

Teng Xiu Xiu was Teng Three 's beloved daughter. She was in love with Wang Zhuo and wanted to follow him no mater what. However, she knew that her father did not like him very much and would always disagree with her when it came to Wang Zhuo.

### History

During the Teng Clan's crisis, she was thrown in a light ring with Wang Zhuo to escape from Wang Lin. She knew that her father would die but she didn't know who was behind everything. When Wang Zhuo and her were teleported a safe distance away, she clung onto Wang Zhuo and pleaded him to stay with her. However, Wang Zhuo still had hated in his heart for the Teng Clan and couldn't make up his mind. He knew he loved her but how could he betray the ancestors of his clan? Teng Xiu Xiu was pushed away by Wang Zhuo and she begin leaving, but Wang Zhuo had a change of heart. When Wang Lin appeared, Wang Lin told them to go far away and he spared both their lives. However, Wang Zhuo decided to swiftly and painlessly end Teng Xiu Xiu's life as well as his down life so that they could die together.
