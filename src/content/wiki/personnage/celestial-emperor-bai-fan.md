## Background

He was a member of the Immortal Astral Continent 's Dark Moon Sect before he was brought to the Ancient Celestial Realm by the previous Lord of the Sealed Realm.

Before his death, he seemed to have figured out that Seven-Colored Daoist had been interfering with Qing Shui's life to grow the Slaughter Essence inside him. Thus, he died while laughing at the heavens or, more accurately, at the silhouette of Seven-Colored Daoist.
