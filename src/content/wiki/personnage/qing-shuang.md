## Overview

Qing Shuang is the daughter of the Celestial Emperor Qing Lin. The first time she appeared in the Rain Celestial Realm, she was a female corpse, carried on the back of the infatuated Zhou Yi and loved her then fondly called Ting'er. She laid motionless in a tower built by Zhou Yi for 2000 years and required large amounts of celestial spiritual energy every 1000 years.

She acted when Zhou Yi's soul was about to die and used all her celestial energy to transform his soul into the Sword Spirit of one of the four Rain Celestial Sword. After this was done, she became motionless and fell to the ground. Wang Lin used the Tower given to him by Zhou Yi to catch her and two of the Rain Celestial Swords before she hit the ground.

### Book 12

She was reincarnated in the Immortal Astral Continent in the Earth Sect . Because Wang Lin had secretly erased and rewrote all of her memories (for selfish reasons), she was very much in love with Qing (reincarnated Zhou Yi ). When Wang Lin met her and Zhou Yi again, he regretted tampering with her memory.
