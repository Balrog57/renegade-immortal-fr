## Background

He lost to the Xuan Dao Sect during one of their previous exchanges with Heng Yue Sect 20 years ago, but managed to impress them.

He and Brother Zhang were rivals for the seat of Core Disciple ten years ago, however, he ambushed and injured Zhang Dekun at some point before a match with one of his relatives, causing Zhang Dekun to lose and Lu Song to win the competition. Lu Song told Zhang Dekun that if he wasn't satisfied by this result, he could challenge him in ten years.

### Book One

He represents Heng You Sect as their 3rd fighter against Xuan Dao Sect's Xu Mu. He is superior, but is ultimately victor by foul when he is ambushed by Zhou Peng .

### Book Two

He is among the few disciples who remains loyal and leaves with the Ancestors when the Heng You Sect is taken over by the Xuan Dao Sect. He is happy when Wang Kin turns Renegade, as that means he is now the Head Disciple again.
