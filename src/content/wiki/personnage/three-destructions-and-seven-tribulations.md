## Description

The Ancient Clan 's Three Trials Seven Calamities are like the cultivators’ five Heaven’s Blights. If they survive, their strength would increase greatly, but if they fail then they’d die.

The more powerful the Ancient, the later the trials would come. Although an ancient would normally experience the first trial at six stars, some ancient gods experienced it at seven stars. There was even a very small portion of Ancients that faced their first trial at eight stars. Tu Si was one example. As for the third trial and the final three calamities, they were the highest trial an ancient god would face.

The trials are hardest for Ancient Gods and easiest for Ancient Demons, this is because Ancient Gods are the strongest while Ancient Demons are the weakest, with Ancient Devils in the middle.

The First and Second Destructions and all four associated Tribulations will be experienced three times each total, first for one's own branch, then twice more as one attempts Connecting Fusing and Refining the other two branches. The Third Destruction and Fifth Tribulation can only be triggered after this, while the Sixth and Seventh Tribulation requires one to visit the Ancestral Temple of one of the three Countries.
