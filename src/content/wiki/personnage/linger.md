## Appearance

She is extremely beautiful with silky, long black hair and a voice as sweet as honey. She was big eyes that always seem like they're sparkling.

### Personality

She is quite curious of everything as anything new that she sees attracts her interest immediately. She is also very innocent despite being a Nascent Soul cultivator due to being sheltered. This is because she has a water spirit body which would easily make her the target of people looking to use her as a cultivation incubator. She has most likely never killed a soul which leaves her with a pure heart, still.

### Background

In a past incarnation, she was Mu Ling, the priestess of the God Slaves of Moon Clan.

In her current incarnation, she has had vague dreams about her previous life since she was young but she has never told anyone about this.

### History

She met Wang Lin in Ghost Eye City on Planet Tian Yun . Wang Lin felt a sense of goodwill towards her for unknown reasons. At the time he thought that it must be because she has a water spirit body like Liu Mei which caused him to be slightly sentimental. He gifted her the hat that Yun Quezi gave him and two small chiming bells that she put on her wrist.

Afterwards, he met her on Planet Blue Spirit when he was invited to stay by Master Yi Chen. A few days later, Wang Lin refined the Azure Ancient God Shield and caused a scene as a enormous Ancient God silhouette appeared in the sky. Ling'er saw the Ancient God silhouette and that night she had the same dream again, except this time she saw the dream until the end.

A few days later, she visited Wang Lin to ask about her dreams. She made the gesture of summoning an Ancient God that she saw in her dreams and Wang Lin's five stars appeared without his control. After seeing the stars she passed out. She woke up the next day with all her memories from her past incarnation. Wang Lin sent her away as he was in the process of reaching the Shi Realm .
