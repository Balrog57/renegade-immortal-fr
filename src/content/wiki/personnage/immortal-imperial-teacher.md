## History

He plotted along with the genius of the Mourning-Death Clan, the Great Soul Sect's founder, against the Celestial Royal Family to get their freedom. Eventually receiving the rank of Imperial Teacher, he became the Sect Master of the Dao Sect in the Central Continent as well as Lian Daozhen 's attendant.

### Book 12

He was the Imperial Teacher and servant of the Celestial Emperor, Lian Daozhen . His entire clan was enslaved to serve the Celestial Ancestor and his descendants. He secretly schemed against Lian Daozhen to obtain Wang Lin's body in order to break the seal placed on him and his clan. He tried to tempt Wang Lin by offering him the inheritance and power of the Celestial Ancestor . When he captured Wang Lin, he thought he had destroyed Wang Lin's soul, but Wang Lin tricked him by going into the Heaven Defying Bead . The Imperial Teacher was able to absorb the Celestial Ancestor's power and inheritance, but it only ended up benefiting Wang Lin when Wang Lin took back his body. At the last moments of his life, he remembered that he wasn't the true Imperial Teacher and in fact it was Hai Zi .
