## Appearance

In the past, he was a full-grown 8-star adult ancient god. Currently, his appearance is that of a juvenile ancient god.

### Background

Most Ancient Gods have a beast known as the Moongazer Serpent inside them. This beast acts as a parasite inside the ancient god's body but it does not cause any harm to the ancient god so its left alive. However, in case an ancient god dies the moongazer serpent would almost surely die alongside it, except in the rarest of rare case that it survives.

However, Lou Chen's story is a bit different: somehow the moongazer serpent inside him managed to incapacitate him and managed to seal him inside itself for the purpose of absorbing the ancient god bloodline to survive and endlessly create smaller moongazer serpents inside itself.

### History

After escaping the Thunder Celestial Realm, Wang Lin found that Blood God had issued a Heaven Order for his assassination. Among his pursuers was Yao Bingyun who could also use Spatial Bending which left him no choice but to lure his pursuers towards the dormant moongazer serpent to agitate it into slaughtering his pursuers.

Inside the moongazer serpent, Wang Lin found a location where the life-force of captured people was being used to birth new moongazer serpents. Further inside he saw a young ancient god with six shattered stars. Lou Chen told him to bring his clansman (i.e. Wang Lin's original body).

During the war between Allheaven Star System and Alliance Star System , Wang Lin entered the void along with the Moongazer Serpent, Qing Shui , Yi Muzi and Wudo Chan . There, he managed to meet Lou Chen and challenged the latter to complete his Ancient God coming-of-age ceremony.

During the battle, a Moon Devourer Clan cultivator from the Outer Realm broke the agreement and interfered with their battle in an attempt to absorb Lou Chen, Wang Lin, Qing Shui and Moongazer Serpent to boost his cultivation and purify his bloodline. However, the Heaven Defying Bead activated and aborbed his crescent moon mark leading him to retreat. Alas, Lou Chen and Moongazer Serpent had already exited the barrier separating the Outer and Sealed Realm.

His Moongazer Serpent reappear after Wang Lin obtain the Ancient Order Inheritance. Warning Spoiler

He reappear in the Immortal Astral Continent's Ancient Dao Country's Imperial Capital, after Wang Lin kill Ancient Dao Emperor. He obtain Wang Lin's Soul Blood to attain the Emperor Level and is chosen as the next Emperor by Xuan Luo.

He was separated from the Moongazer Serpent by Xuan Luo when he was taken out of the Immortal Cave.
