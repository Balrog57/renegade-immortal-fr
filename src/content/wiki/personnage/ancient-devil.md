## Overview

Ancient Devils are categorized by their green skin and clawed fingers, similar to the Ancient Demons. However, unlike the latter, they possess one horn on their head instead of two. They are known for being able to create weapons and armor from their own twisted qi.

Like the other branches of the Ancient Clan, their strength is determined by the amount of stars they possess, which for them is found in their right eye.

In the Immortal Astral Continent, their strength is middle ranked individually, but as a nation they are the strongest.
