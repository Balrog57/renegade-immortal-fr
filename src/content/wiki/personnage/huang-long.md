## Overview

Huang Long is the sect head of the Heng Yue Sect, a once-powerful cultivation sect that has fallen on hard times over the past 500 years. He is portrayed as a calm and experienced leader who is deeply concerned about the declining strength and future of his sect. During an exchange event with the Xuan Dao Sect, Huang Long observes the new disciples and feels worried about the appearance of powerful members from the rival sect, especially those with strong single-element spirit roots
