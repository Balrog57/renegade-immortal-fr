## Personality

Zhang Hu is a reasonably friendly but equally ruthless young man. He showed Wang Lin the ropes around the sect, but decisively killed Liu when the 'Weasel' tried to dispose of him. He also has no problem killing witnesses to protect himself if need be.

### Book One

Zhang Hu and Wang Lin bond over the latter's baked potatos and Zhang Hu tells Wang Lin all about the sect on his first few days. One day Wang Lin stumbles upon him and Liu having an argument that escalates to Liu wanting to silence Zhang Hu, even threatening his family. Wang Lin steps in and saves Zhang Hu's life, but Zhang Hu decides to flee the Heng Yue Sect before the deed is discovered.

### Book Two

After he left, he consumed some herbs and broke through to Qi Condensation 's 1st Layer. He was then discovered by Bai Zhan , who made him his disciple to help him collect Blood Essence. On one particular day, however, he leads the Vulture Escort to attack the Mighty Escort Service and by chance runs into Wang Lin again. Wang Lin then kills Bai Zhan, thus setting Zhang Hu free of the poison he used to control him. Fearing that Ji Mo , Bai Zhan's master, will come after them, the two seek asylum in the Teng Family City. Soon after, the duo are attacked separately and Wang Lin is forced to flee the city, thus leaving Zhang Hu's fate unknown but likely bleak.
