## Appearance

He has a middle aged look to him and black hair. He has three Ancient Devil and Demon stars in his eyes and two Ancient God stars on his forehead.

### Personality

Like Yun Na, who was created from the 9th and last Essence Blood drop, he believes himself to be Ye Mo's son and thus he feels entitled to Ye Mo's Inheritance, which provokes him to challenge Wang Lin to a death duel.

### Background

Upon his death Ancient Order Ye Mo scattered 3000 drops of blood which created the three Ancient Clans of the Cave World. However he also spat out 9 drops of Essence Blood, which created 9 Ancient Clansmen who identified as his children. The Sovereign, under Seven Colour's direction, killed eight of these beings and refined their Essence Blood to create him. However he remains incomplete as the last drop of Essence Blood was hidden in Allheaven as the Dong ling Family's Female Ancient God.

### Book Eleven

He is summoned by the Outer Realm to fight Wang Lin in the hopes of at least damaging his Unnamed Wheel Formation and luring out one of his two remaining shots of Li Guang's Bow. He promptly engaged Wang Lin in a death duel. His presence was also sensed by the female Ancient God in Allheaven. He was ultimately trapped inside Wang Lin's True and False illusion and was cut in half by Wang Lin, his eyes remained clouded until the very final moment.

### Trivia

Each of his eight stars is actually one of Ye Mo's nine Essence Blood drops.

Ye Mo's Daughter's blood drop is shown to be able to turn into 7 Ancient God Stars and back again, explaining the Child's massive disparity in power relative to his visible Cultivation Level.
