## Appearance

She has black hair and attractive features.

### Background

In the past, she was created by the Ancient Celestial Sovereign from a drop of Ye Mo 's heart blood. The Celestial Sovereign also created an Ancient Devil Elder and an Ancient Demon Elder but they both died in their sleep due to the Cave World's core being closed for so long.

### Book Seven

She ordered Dong Lingtai , the Xiang Family 's ancestor, to spare Wang Lin after she sensed that he was an Ancient God.

### Book Ten

After Wang Lin returned from the Outer Realm , he went to Planet Dong Ling to get revenge on Dong Lingtai for almost killing him and causing irreparable damage to Li Muwan 's soul.

After he killed Dong Lingtai, a member of the Dong Ling Sect suddenly appeared through a portal and attempted to pass a death sentence upon him but was almost killed by a single punch from Wang Lin. He was spared by Wang Lin as he sensed the aura of the 7-star Ancient God from inside the portal.

### Book Eleven

She sensed the aura of Ye Mo's Child when he appeared to fight Wang Lin while the latter guarded the Inner Realm with his Unnamed Wheel Formation .

Afterwards, she gave Wang Lin her drop of Ye Mo's heart blood as he was the first to enter the Dong Ling Sect inside the second flower. Initially, she stated that it was due to her orders being so but she admitted that she wished to leave the Cave World and go to the Immortal Astral Continent and, thus, wanted Wang Lin to be strong enough to open the door.

### Trivia

Xuan Luo notes that female Ancient Gods are extremely rare existences within his Dao Clan and considers making her Wang Lin's maid. This is regardless of her roots as well as her giving up her Ancient God powers to Wang Lin almost completely.
