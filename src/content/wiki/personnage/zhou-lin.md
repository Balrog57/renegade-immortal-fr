## S1 EP39 Donghua First Appearance

Ninth generation disciple Zhou Lin of Cloud Sky Sect. He was a disciple of Li Muwan and the master of Wang Lin when he joined the Cloud Sky Sect using his newly formed avatar.

He was the examiner during the new disciples' entrance examination. Wang Lin deliberately chose him to be his master by observing that Zhou Lin wanted a water element pill from the pill furnace.

He then went to closed door training to absorb the water element pill after leaving Wang Lin with a jade containing his dozens of years of experience in alchemy and some pill recipes.
