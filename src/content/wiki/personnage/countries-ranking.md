**Catégories :** Countries

## Countries

The Countries are basically a ranking from 1 to 7. These rankings are handled by the Alliance that contains all countries. A lower rank country is backed by a country one rank higher than the country, with rank 1 as the lowest. A higher rank country control more than one country one rank below them, with the rank 3 the lowest.

### Rank 1 - 3

These guys clean up the battlefields of where Rank 4 to 5 countries fight in the name of Rank 6 and 7 Countries. Here the Rank 1 - 3 Countries can gain treasure to allow them to advance to higher ranks and become stronger. The Higher ranked country can't interfere with the Rank 1 and 2 country, they can only let them develop but for Rank 3, they can order them.

### Rank 4 -5

They are backed by Rank 6 and 7 Countries and fight in the name of them on these foreign battlefields.

### Rank 6

These countries own the entire planet they are on. There is one Rank 6 country per planet and all the lower ranked countries on that planet are under their rule.

### Rank 7

These countries own entire solar systems I assume since they are a combination of Rank 6 countries. If a Rank 6 country cultivates another Rank 6 country, that first Rank 6 country owns the second and eventually becomes a Rank 7 country by having multiple Rank 6 countries underneath it.

### Requirement

Rank 1 : Have a Foundation Building Expert.

Rank 2 : Have a Core Formation Expert as the highest cultivator.

Rank 5 : Have a Soul Transformation Expert.

Rank 6 : 1000 Wins in a row in the Foreign Battleground, and cultivate their planet in a true cultivation planet (A lot of countries of ranks 1-5).

Rank 7 : Having cultivated multiple Rank 6 countries.
