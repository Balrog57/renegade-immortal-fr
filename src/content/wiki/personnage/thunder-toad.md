## Appearance

Blue skin with yellow criss-cross veins, red eyes and its whole body is surrounded by lightning sparks.

### Background

First creature capture by the Beast trap of Wang Lin. Loyal even without the beast Trap.

Because of Wang Lin, he became one of the 9 Accompanying Thunder around the Thunder Essence and replace the 8th type of Eternal Accompanying Thunder from ancient time.
