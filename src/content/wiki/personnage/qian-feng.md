Wang Lin used him for taking back his soul from Cultivation Planet Crystal . Because if you want to take back your soul from this item, you should pay a price, and this is paid by Qian Feng, with his life.

Qian Feng used this sword against Wang Lin. But Tuo Sen took it from him and used it for breaking his seal in the Ancient God body. This sword's rusts was actually blood of a 9-star ancient god.
