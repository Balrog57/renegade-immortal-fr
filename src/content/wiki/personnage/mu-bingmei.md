## Background

In order to cultivate her sect's most profound and powerful spell, one must obtain the Thousand Illusion Ruthless Domain and evolve it into the Ten Thousand Illusion Demon Domain.

Mu Bingmei decided to use a shortcut wherein she made nine Avatars of herself and sent to distant parts of the Alliance Star System to each cultivate a part of her grand dao.

She sent Liu Mei to Planet Suzaku where she had a fateful encounter with Wang Lin .

### Book Eight

Somehow, she became Zhou Ru 's Teacher after she left Planet Suzaku . Before Wang Lin left the Alliance Star System , he visited the Brilliant Void Realm to leave Zhou Ru under Qing Lin . At that time, he ended all his past disputes with Liu Mei (and in effect with Mu Bingmei).

### Book Nine

During the 100 years when Wang Lin was stuck in the Seven-Colored Realm , Tuo Sen escaped from the Sea of Devils . He nearly destroyed the Allheaven Army, the Four Divine Sect , the Cultivation Alliance , and the Corpse Sect . He also killed every one from the Brilliant Void Realm except Zhou Ru, who was with Qing Lin, and Mu Bingmei on whom he left an imprint. Mu Bingmei escaped to the Cloud Sea Star System where she met Wang Lin and told him about Tuo Sen's escape. Wang Lin also absorbed Tuo Sen's imprint which helped to further stabilize his sixth star.

### Book Eleven

When Wang Lin invited others to reincarnate on the Immortal Astral Continent, she ultimately chose to remain inside the Cave World. She appeared only to see Wang Lin off.

### Book Thirteen

After Wang Lin returns to the Cave World, he puts himself and Mu Bingmei in a Dao Dream for one lifetime together. Mu Bingmei lives as Liu Mei for this life time, becoming the mother of Wang Ping in a mortal life. After they wake, Wang Lin asks her to close her eyes. When she opens her eyes she departs with a Wang Lin who she cannot tell whether it is the original or an avatar, leaving the Cave World for parts unknown.

### Trivia

It is implied that the Wang Lin Mu Bingmei departs with was created by Wang Lin using his True and False Domain, as he describes there being "No real answer" to whether or not it is the real him or an Avatar.
