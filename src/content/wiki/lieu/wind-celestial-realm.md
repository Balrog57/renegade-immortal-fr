## History

In the past, the Wind Celestial Realm was ruled by Lan Yun, the Wind Celestial Emperor. His younger sister, Flower Imperial Concubine Han Yan , later married Qing Lin , linking the Wind Celestial Realm to key events in the broader cultivation world.

The realm has endured partial collapse following the fall of the Ancient Celestial Realm but has remained relatively intact compared to the Rain and Thunder Celestial Realms, thanks to the presence of mosquito beasts that dominated and protected its inner lands.

### Structure

The Wind Celestial Realm consists of three inner continents, which form the core of the realm. These inner continents are surrounded by a mysterious protective force, preventing easy access and preserving their ancient energy.

Encircling the inner three continents are nine outer continents, arranged in concentric layers like guardians. These outer continents are distinguished by their colors, from outermost to innermost:

### Gold – nearest to the inner core

Each layer contributes to the realm’s mystical energy network, creating a layered defense and a unique environmental ecosystem.

### Notable Features

Giant Stone Gate: The largest inner continent features a colossal stone gate, hundreds of thousands of feet tall, resembling the Heaven Defying Bead’s gate. Wang Lin fused with this gate to gain insight into the Shi Realm, the law of creation.

Mosquito Beasts: Dominant creatures that patrol the realm, maintaining its integrity and deterring unprepared cultivators.

Vortex Winds: Constant, intense winds form vortexes that sweep across the continents, influencing cultivation and movement.

### Cultivation Significance

The realm is a source of celestial spiritual energy and Joss Flames, vital for cultivators aiming for higher stages such as Soul Formation.

Wang Lin consolidated the fragments of the Wind Celestial Realm, using it as the foundation for the new Celestial Realm.

The inner continents’ mysterious protective force ensures that only cultivators of sufficient power can access the realm’s core treasures and knowledge.

### Current Status

Wang Lin is actively restoring and consolidating its fragments as the root of the new Celestial Realm.

The realm remains dominated by mosquito beasts and intense vortex winds, with the inner continents still holding significant ancient energy.
