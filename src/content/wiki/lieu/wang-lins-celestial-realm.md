## History

Wang Lin took the land from the Wind Celestial Realm , expanded it with the dust left behind in the Rain Celestial Realm after Qing Lin reconstructed it, and then used the silver sea of the Lightning Celestial Realm to create a boundary that only Cultivators at the Soul Formation stage can cross. After that he connected this realm to his Unnamed Wheel Formation to ensure that the Joss Flames and Celestial energy from the Outer Realm is sent here. The cultivators of the Sealed Realm then took pieces from the planet souls all over the Sealed Realm to create a connection so that Soul Formation Cultivators could be sent here upon breaking through their peak.

This world was created to strengthen the Sealed Realm and Wang Lin asked Situ Nan to pass down the truth that they're living in a cave world both here and on every planet in the Sealed Realm for future generations as an insurance policy in the event that he fails to defeat the Seven Dao Sect . After defeating the Seven Dao Sect, Wang Lin forcefully reincarnated the Third Step Outer Realm Cultivators onto the Immortal Astral Continent, and then sealed the Cultivation of everyone else of the Outer Realm so that their Cultivation could never reach a threatening level again. Wang Lin then left the Cave World for the Immortal Astral Continent, while the Grand Empyrean Xuan Luo took the souls of Situ Nan , Red Butterfly , Zhou Li , Zhou Ru , Thirteen , Qing Shui , Qing Lin , Qing Shuang and Big Head to be reincarnated there.

Long after Wang Lin left the Cave World , a new sect named the Scarlet Soul Sect emerged in the Sealed Realm and began launching assaults on the New Celestial Realm. After Wang Lin returned, he destroyed it thoroughly with his disciple Thirteen .
