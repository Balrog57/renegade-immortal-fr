## Overview

Planet Thousand Illusion is a significant location with a complex background and powerful families. It is part of the Allheaven Star System’s northern domain and is protected by three major cultivation families: Qian, Huan, and Xu. Among these, the Qian family is the largest and most powerful, controlling the northern and eastern regions with three Second Step cultivators. The Huan family is second in both size and strength, with two Second Step cultivators—one being the current ancestor and the other having died thousands of years ago. The Huan family lineage traces back to a Celestial from the Thunder Celestial Realm, and they possess an inherited celestial treasure as well as a family-protection jade containing a celestial spell. The Xu family is mysterious and feared by the other two families, possibly linked to a famous family on Planet Dong Lin.

The planet itself contains a powerful spiritual energy system. Wang Lin used a method to transform all the spiritual energy on the planet into celestial spiritual energy, causing a temporary depletion of ordinary spiritual energy. He then extracts the planet’s soul—a profound and ancient aura formed over countless years. This extraction causes the planet to tremble, spirit veins to collapse, and shockwaves to spread, resulting in severe damage.
