## Structure

The Star System's Cultivators are almost overwhelmingly female and they all wear military style uniforms, implying they serve organisations of that nature rather than Sects or Clans. They ride into battle on bolts of lightning.

There is a forbidden region of the Star System which consists of a Cultivation Planet surrounded by a vortex. This area is home to a Third Step Cultivator named Zhou De .

There are approximately 5 Seven Coloured Realms in this Star System, through which the Outer Realm invaded during the second battle of the Third War of the Sealed and Outer Realms . The Star System was completely conquered during this battle.
