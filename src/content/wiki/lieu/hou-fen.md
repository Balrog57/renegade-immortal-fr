## Location

The rank 3 cultivation country, Hou Fen, was at the southern part of planet Suzaku and was to the south of the Sea of Devils .

### History

Because Wang Lin's Heaven Defying Bead absorbed the Main body of the fire spiritual beast, all of the beast's "children" caused massive chaos in Hou Fen, resulting in the area no longer having viable spiritual energy. The Nascent Soul realm experts of the four main sects tried to use a country sealing array to seal the beast in the volcanic range, but they failed which resulted in the cultivators of Hou Fen waging war with the country of Xuan Wu .
