## Description

This planet served as a major trading hub for cultivators. After leaving Planet Suzaku , Situ Nan and Wang Lin visited this world, which was regarded as a true treasure trove due to the wide variety of resources and items available for exchange. During his time here, Wang Lin obtained the Void Wood Stone, an essential material that aided in repairing his Star Compass .

It was also on this planet that Wang Lin first encountered Bai Wei . In addition, he came into possession of Snow Ink, which was handed to him by Situ Nan, who had stolen it from other cultivators.
