## Overview

The Lightning Celestial Realm is one of the four ancient Celestial Realms, alongside the Wind, Rain, and Thunder Celestial Realms. It originated from the Ancient Celestial Realm, which was shattered in a catastrophic event long ago. Unlike some of the other realms, the Lightning Celestial Realm remains relatively intact and serves a unique protective role for the newly reformed Celestial Realm created by Wang Lin. The realm is associated with the Summoned River star system.

The realm is visually represented as a vast, luminous silver river flowing through space. This “silver river” consists of countless small fragments of light, forming an eternal, flowing reflection that distinguishes it from other celestial realms. Its brilliance and beauty make it a striking and iconic part of the celestial map. The realm also serves as a protective boundary, enclosing and safeguarding the continent of the new Celestial Realm.

### Current State

While other realms, such as the Thunder Celestial Realm, are heavily fragmented and in ruins, the Lightning Celestial Realm is considered completely open and relatively stable. Despite the irreversible destruction from ancient calamities, the silver river persists and functions as a protective barrier around the newly reformed realm. Its structure allows Wang Lin to control its flow and strengthen its embedded restrictions, enhancing its defensive capabilities.

### Role in the New Celestial Realm

The Lightning Celestial Realm forms a natural protective barrier for the newly reformed Celestial Realm, centered on the Wind Celestial Realm continent. The silver river divides mortals from celestials, preventing cultivators below the Soul Formation stage from entering. This function preserves the realm’s exclusivity and ensures the safety of higher-level cultivators within the new order. Wang Lin has enhanced the silver river’s protective power, creating a rotating aura around the Wind Celestial Realm continent.
