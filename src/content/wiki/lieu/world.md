## The World (Vast Expanse)

Warning Renegade Immortal, Pursuit of Truth and I Shall Seal The Heavens Spoilers

The World is called the Vast Expanse and is a Starry Sky contained within The Universe. In order to leave this Starry Sky and/or create their own, a Cultivator must reach the Fourth Step equivalent in their respective continent. The Immortal Astral Continent, Devil Realm Continent and Nine Mountain and Seas are all landmasses existing within this Starry Sky each with their own independent Cultivation systems. This makes the Vast Expanse the overall main setting for all three of Er Gen 's first works. The ruler and founder of the Vast Expanse is named Allheaven.

There are many vast landmasses and continents in the World seeming to float in infinite space. The Heaven Trampling Bridges are said to exist over all or perhaps just multiple such Continents.

### Immortal Astral Continent

Is the main landmass that exists outside of the Cave World and is part of The World, it is the home of the Ancient Clan and Celestial Clan .

### Other novels

The Vast Expanse also appears in other novels of Er Gen - Pursuit of the Truth and I Shall Seal the Heavens .

during the timeline of Renegade Immortal, the Devil Realm Continent of Pursuit of Truth should already exist and the Paragon Immortal Realm has yet to be destroyed and remade into the Nine Mountain and Seas of I Shall Seal the Heavens.
