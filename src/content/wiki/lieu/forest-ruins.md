## Forest Perimeter

the forest surrounding the city is home to a powerful fog. There are many dangers hidden within this fog, most noteably is the immature growth of "Blue Line Vines" located therein. These vines are particularly deadly as noted by Situ Nan, a mature one is known to have the power to intimidate Soul Formation level Cultivators and at least one Rank 2 country was utterly annihilated by one in the past. Should they sense blood or Qi, they will attack to envelop their target and devour it alive. They also utilise a potent acid that even the Teng Fanily's Golden Armour can be dissolved by.

### Ruins

Deep within the forest lay a ruined city that intermingles metal structures and growing vegitation. There are towers in this ruin containing giant stone beads. These towers absorb light from the sun and after collecting enough will activate the beads. The beads then release a pillar of light that heals anything that walks into its path. At night, meanwhile, the ruins emit powerful Yin Qi which is of great use to Cultivators. What happened to the ruins is unknown, but according to what Wang Lin saw, it's likely the one ir so million inhabitants died quickly, as many were in the middle of eating at the time. Further in, past the centre square, the destruction becomes far greater and a swamp divides the city. There are many honnycone-shaped holes in this swamp that produce large amounts of Yin Qi at night.
