## Overview

The Central Continent is the primary continent of the Celestial Clan and as such contains vast Celestial Energy that permeates throughout its borders. At the center of the continent is the Imperial City, whose majesty surpasses that of any of the Royal Capitals in the Ancient Clan .

The Imperial City is controlled directly by the Celestial Royal Family, specifically, the Celestial Emperor and by extension the Imperial Teacher. Despite this, powerful Celestials such as Jiu Di have a degree of autonomy and authority within it.

The Imperial City spans a large amount of territory, comparable to the size of a continent. It is divided into five quadrants, North, East, South, West, and Central. Each quadrant is ruled by a Mansion Family, with the exception of the central which houses the Imperial Palace and the Royal Family. There are also no mortals in the Imperial City as the Celestial Energy is so thick that it is impossible for them to live.

### Central - Royal Family

Between the East and West quadrants is Mount Emperor , which is home to Grand Empyrean Jiu Di . Due to his presence, it is revered and respected by the Celestial Clan just as much as the Imperial Family.

### Dao Sect

Within the continent there is only a singular sect, which is tied directly to the Celestial Royal Family. In the sect, the current Celestial Emperor is the undisputed ruler and the current Imperial Teacher is the Sect Master. The name of the sect is constantly changes, with each subsequent generation Celestial Emperor adjusting it to their liking upon their coronation and inheriting the Celestial Ancestor 's Eight Extreme Dao.

Under Lian Daozhen , this sect was known as the Dao Sect .

### Deep beneath the underground of the city

It is rumored by many in the Celestial Clan that the primary focus of the sect currently is to suppress the power and influence of Mount Emperor and Grand Empyrean Jiu Di .

### Imperial Palace

The Imperial Palace is the primary residence of the Celestial Royal Family and is where the current Celestial Emperor commonly resides and cultivates when not conducting official business.

Within the palace is a secret cultivation ground where one can receive the inheritance of the Celestial Ancestor . It is also where the seals containing the 72 Beasts that were sealed away by the Celestial Ancestor are located.
