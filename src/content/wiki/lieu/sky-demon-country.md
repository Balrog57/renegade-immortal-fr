## Sky Demon Country

"In the Sky Demon Country, there is the Demon King Kun Xu. Below him are the eight great Demon Generals, 10 million demon soldiers, and more than 100 cities. That is the power of one country!"

### Hundreds of Demon Generals  – Primary participants in the competition

8 great Commander-in-Chief, 8 Vice Commander-in-Chief, 100+ Demon Generals and 10 million Demon Soldiers.

### Structure

More than 100 cities such as the Ancient Demon City .

### Description

The Sky Demon Country is one of the nine great countries of the Demon Spirit Land , standing out as one of the most militarized, disciplined, and fearsome powers in the region. Unlike ordinary cultivation lands where individuals pursue strength independently, the Sky Demon Country operates almost entirely under a military-based system , where personal cultivation, political status, and survival are inseparably tied to military rank and command. Cultivators who enter the Demon Spirit Land must affiliate themselves with one of the nine countries, and without commanding troops or holding military status, even powerful individuals find it nearly impossible to survive long-term. This structure emphasizes collective strength, formations, and large-scale warfare over lone heroics.

At the heart of the Sky Demon Country lies its capital, Sky Demon City , a massive and awe-inspiring metropolis designed with both strategic and mystical intent. The capital is composed of nine interconnected cities arranged in the pattern of an eight divinatory trigram , with the royal palace positioned at the exact center. A river encircles the palace, crossed by eight bridges that connect it to the surrounding cities, symbolizing both control and balance. The entire city is permeated by a faint green mist known as dragon energy , a natural phenomenon that subtly enhances cultivation and reinforces the city’s status as a sacred and strategic stronghold.

Sky Demon City is protected by an extraordinary defensive system. Eighteen transfer arrays are positioned around the capital, each heavily guarded by demon soldiers and strictly regulated according to rank. Only ten of these arrays are accessible to high-status figures such as demon generals, vice commander-in-chiefs, and commander-in-chiefs, while ordinary cultivators are restricted to lower-tier arrays. This rigid separation of access reinforces the strict class hierarchy of the Sky Demon Country and ensures that authority is visibly and spatially enforced at all times.

The military structure of the Sky Demon Country is its defining feature. At the base are countless demon soldiers , forming massive armies capable of executing large-scale formations that overwhelm even powerful lone cultivators. Above them are hundreds of demon generals , each commanding significant forces and wielding formidable cultivation and combat techniques. Demon generals are not merely officers but battlefield elites whose strength can influence the outcome of regional conflicts. However, even they are bound by strict military law—open life-and-death battles between demon generals are forbidden, and conflicts are instead resolved through political maneuvering, competitions, or sanctioned engagements.

Above the demon generals stand the eight vice commander-in-chiefs , elite figures considered the immediate successors to the commander-in-chiefs. Their authority is immense, and only vice commander-in-chiefs are permitted to challenge commander-in-chiefs for their positions. Every three hundred years, the Sky Demon Country holds a grand demon general competition , where demon generals fight for promotion into vice commander-in-chief positions. These competitions are not merely tests of strength but intense political events, involving alliances, secret assassinations of helpers, and strategic manipulation, reflecting the brutal yet regulated nature of the demon military system.

At the highest military level are the eight commander-in-chiefs , who each oversee vast portions of the army and directly answer to the supreme ruler. Above them stands the Demon Emperor , who personally commands the Sky Demon Country during major wars. Unlike symbolic rulers, the Demon Emperor takes to the battlefield himself, embodying the Sky Demon Country’s belief that authority must be backed by overwhelming personal power. During large-scale conflicts, such as the war against the Fire Demon Country , the Sky Demon Country has mobilized armies numbering thirty million demon soldiers , launching multi-directional invasions that devastate entire regions over decades of sustained warfare.

The Sky Demon Country enforces strict military law , which governs not only soldiers but all cultivators within its territory. A lone cultivator who kills too many demon soldiers will inevitably draw the attention of demon generals and eventually commander-in-chiefs, making survival nearly impossible. Formations, numbers, and coordinated tactics dominate warfare, ensuring that individual strength alone rarely determines outcomes. This system discourages reckless slaughter and reinforces obedience to the country’s authority.

In addition to its military might, the Sky Demon Country possesses ancient sacred locations and artifacts that further solidify its dominance. Dragon Lake , located near the capital, houses the Ancient Demon Temple , a sacred site tied to the origins and faith of the demon race. The imperial palace also contains relics of immense power, such as the Emperor’s sword , whose movements alone can cause panic among high-ranking officials. These ancient elements reflect the deep historical roots of the Sky Demon Country and its connection to primordial demonic forces.

The country’s recent history has been marked by prolonged warfare, internal tension, and divine retribution events tied to the Dao of the heavens. Despite suffering losses and upheaval, the Sky Demon Country has maintained its military dominance and structural integrity. Specialized forces and tribes, such as the Soul Refining Tribe , further enhance its power through unique cultivation methods involving soul fragments and large-scale soul formations, making them feared by both allies and enemies alike.
