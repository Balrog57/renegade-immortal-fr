## Five Flowers Eight Doors Formation

After the entrance lay a Furnace, and those who enter said Furnace will have their faces appear on it. This is the defense mechanism of the Cave World. Within the Furnace are a series of giant flowers, each serving a unique purpose, this is called the Five Flowers Eight Doors Formation.

### The Second Flower contains a frozen world and is where  Ancient God

Yun Na is located, originally there were two other guards, an Ancient Demon and an Ancient Devil , but they died in hibernation. The Dong Lin Pool was located here.

### Third Flower - Unknown

The Third Flower is grey and wilted, long since dead.

### Fourth Flower - Ancient Tomb

The Fourth Flower is pitch black and sits in a decaying black ocean. Upon entering the flower, one shall enter Ye Mo's Ancient Tomb.

### Fifth Flower - Eight Doors

The final flower contains the Eight Doors, only of which one leads to Immortal Astral Continent.
