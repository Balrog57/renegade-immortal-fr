## Overview

The Fire Demon Country is one of the nine major countries within the Demon Spirit Land , a realm consumed by demonic power and ancient conflict. It has been embroiled in an extended and devastating war against its neighboring state, the Sky Demon Country, a conflict that has raged for nearly a century. The endless battles have left the land scarred—cities reduced to ruins, fields soaked in blood, and the air thick with death aura and demonic miasma, turning the entire country into a bleak and desolate battlefield.

The capital city of the Fire Demon Country now lies mostly deserted. Even the Demon Emperor has personally entered the frontlines, leaving behind a silent capital enveloped in dense demonic energy and death fog. The ancient demon of the Fire Demon Country, much like its counterpart in the Sky Demon Country, has sealed the capital with a powerful formation, restricting access except to the Demon Valley, the sacred and mysterious region at the city’s heart.

Amid the chaos, the Soul Refining Tribe —a ruthless and power-hungry faction—rose to prominence. Exploiting the weakened state of the Fire Demon Country, the tribe expanded rapidly through warfare and soul absorption, using soul fragments and forbidden refining arts to strengthen themselves. After centuries of bloodshed, they became a dominant force, even surrounding the Fire Demon capital itself. Their leaders possess formidable spiritual might, and their rise has dramatically shifted the balance of power across the Demon Spirit Land.
