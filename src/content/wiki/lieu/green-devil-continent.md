## Background

The continent was allegedly created when the Celestial Ancestor sealed the Green Devil Scorpion that practiced Water based cultivation and used his imprisoned origin soul to birth celestial energy into the continent to allow cultivators to thrive on the continent.

### Book Twelve

The Green Devil Continent's Three Great Sects, Devil Dao Sect, Poison Devil Sect, and Wu Ji Sect (author literally added a "Devil Restriction Sect" afterwards so idk which of these 4 are the 3 great sects XD), initiated war against the Heavenly Bull Continent . They planned to revert the half Celestial Pill that created the Pill Sea back into a pill and use it to break the barrier dividing the two continents. To this end, they sent Void Cloud , one of the Eastern Continent's Four Geniuses, there to refine the sea back into the pill and break the barrier as it would also allow his reputation to skyrocket and allow him to enter the Celestial Imperial Capital in the Central Continent to obtain the qualification to enter the ancestral temple.

Void Cloud planned to explode the pill close to the Heavenly Bull Continent which would immediately destroy the barrier around it and disintegrate much of the continent. However, Wang Lin's interference resulted in the pill exploding right in the middle of the two continents and, thus, damaged both continents.

Still, it managed to destroy the barrier around the Heavenly Bull Continent and so the Green Devil Continent's Three Great Sects marched their millions of cultivators towards the Heavenly Bull Continent. They also sent advance parties filled with a few dozen Void Tribulant cultivators and mostly Third Step or Heaven's Blight cultivators.
