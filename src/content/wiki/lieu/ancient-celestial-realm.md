## Inner Celestial Realm

The Inner Realm's half of the Celestial Realm is where the Ancient Celestial Emperor is said to currently reside in closed door Cultivation, the reality however is that it is empty. This Ancient Celestial Realm is currently closed off and inaccessible. The Lord of the Sealed Realm hails from here and is a servant of the Celestial Emperor. It is filled with Ancient Clan bodies turned to stone who serve as Messengers of Heaven when needed, with a watery sphere for the Self Retribution at the middle, where the entrance is. It is from here that Divine Retribution originates from and thus it opens its doors only to inflict Divine Retribution upon a Heaven Defying Cultivator. This world has Nine Suns, like the Immortal Astral Continent, but each is just an instrument of Divine Retribution .

The Third Sun represents force that can suppress all life, Heaven's Might.

The Seventh Sun contains the enslaved souls of the Ancient Clan , and allows them to serve as Divine messengers.

The Eighth Sun contains the self retribution, which is the final true Divine Retribution and forces a Heaven Defying Cultivator to fight a perfect copy of themselves.

The Ninth Sun contains the fate of every living being that ever existed inside the Cave World, ensuring they cannot escape unless they seize it back.

### Outer Celestial Realm

Unlike the Inner Celestial Realm, the outer realm is completely open and accessible to the people of the Ancient Primordial Star System . It is currently governed by the Eight Ancient Imperial Concubines who have since betrayed the Ancient Celestial Emperor. While the Four Divine Sect's first generation Divine Emperors who served as the Celestial Emperor's generals and also came from the Outer Realm, the facts that they settled in the Sealed Realm with no visible complaint from the Celestial Domain's people and the Vermilion Bird Divine Sect's First Generation Divine Emperor established the Fallen Land in the Outer Realm, it can be assumed that they remain loyal to the Celestial emperor or at the very least oppose the Concubines.
