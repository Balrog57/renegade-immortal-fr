## Overview

The Rain Celestial Realm is one of the four ancient Celestial Realms, alongside the Wind, Thunder, and Lightning Celestial Realms. Originating from the Ancient Celestial Realm, it was fractured and damaged by the catastrophic collapse of that original domain. Despite its fragmented and unstable condition, the Rain Celestial Realm remains a crucial site for cultivation, celestial activities, and the study of elemental laws associated with rain and storms.

This realm is linked to one of the legendary Celestial Doors , ancient gateways to the Ancient Celestial Realm whose origins remain mysterious. The Rain Celestial Realm contains celestial spiritual energy purer than ordinary spiritual energy but weaker than true cultivation energy, which is used to open the Celestial Gate and support specialized cultivation techniques. The realm resides within the Brilliant Void star system, later referred to as the “Alliance,” and continues to serve as a hub for powerful cultivators seeking lost treasures and ancient spells.

The realm is highly fragmented, with numerous floating landmasses and platforms that remain from its former unified territory. Its environment is dominated by perpetual rain, clouds, and storms, which shape rivers, mud, and local flora and fauna. The realm also contains ancient formations and structures such as celestial temples, altars, and relics of its former grandeur. Spatial cracks and tremors persist, signaling ongoing instability and making exploration perilous but rewarding due to the potent energy present in these zones.

### Cultivation and Resources

The Rain Celestial Realm is rich in celestial spiritual energy, making it a prime location for cultivators. Special artifacts, such as the Rain Cauldron , enable safe entry and energy harnessing. Various sects, including the Giant Demon Sect and the Corpse Sect , actively dispatch disciples to the realm to collect energy, treasures, and powerful celestial artifacts. The realm is also the source of potent spells, such as “Summon the Rain” and “Call the Wind,” which allow manipulation of elemental forces and weather phenomena. The Rain Celestial Sword and its associated sword hymn are among the realm’s most coveted spiritual artifacts.

Severely damaged during the collapse of the Ancient Celestial Realm, the Rain Celestial Realm lost much of its original power and coherence. Efforts by figures such as Qing Lin have sought to restore stability using golden lines of law and celestial spiritual energy, gradually reconstructing fragments of the realm. The realm has been a battleground for numerous conflicts, including attempts to rescue the Azure Dragon Divine Emperor , and continues to be a stage for life-and-death struggles involving powerful cultivators and factions.

### Notable Locations and Features

Rain Celestial Realm Gate: A massive golden door inscribed with the character for “Rain” and marked with a powerful handprint spell, serving as a key entry point.

Fragments and Platforms: Floating remnants of the original landmass, forming platforms for cultivation and battles.

Giant Altar and Temples: Centralized locations for gathering celestial energy and hosting rituals.

Spatial Cracks and Rifts: Areas of instability that both threaten and empower cultivators, rich with celestial spiritual energy.

### Connection to Other Celestial Realms

The Rain Celestial Realm is one of the Four Great Celestial Realms , each associated with different star systems: Wind (Cloud Sea), Rain (Brilliant Void/Alliance), Thunder (Allheaven), and Lightning (Summoned River). These realms occasionally interact, particularly when ancient celestial gateways open, drawing cultivators from across star systems to compete for resources and power.
