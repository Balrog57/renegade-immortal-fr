## The Trial

It is divided into 19 stages, the first 9 assess Empyrean Exalts while the 10th assesses whether or not one is worthy of being called an Ascendant Empyrean. As such, every trial up to ten has the practicing Celestial face a Starsystem that will hurl a number of planets at them. They must destroy these planets simultaneously, meaning they must prove they can combine enough spells to destroy those planets at once. At a certain level, golden planets, as tough as several, begin to appear. Finally, the tenth trial itself has 99 planets, which demands combining at least 99 spells to destroy. Because of this, it is commonly said only an Ascendant Empyrean can combine 100 or more spells. Anyone who can pass the tenth stage is then bestowed the title of Ascendant Empyrean personally by the Celestial Ancestor's shadow, and their name is recorded on the Ascendant Empyrean List. The 11th to 19th Trials then are specifically for Ascendant Empyreans and pit them against the 72 entities that the Ancestor sealed to make the 72 Celestial Continents.

### Rewards

From the 11th Trial onward, one can obtain one of the Extreme Daos, the Celestial Ancestor obtained from the Immemorial God Realm.

Furthermore, in theory, anyone who passes the 19th trial is qualified to become a Grand Empyrean in the future. However only the late Grand Empyrean, Dong Lin, is known to have done so, and he is noted to have accordingly achieved that level "by his own merits", unlike many other Grand Empyreans, who somewho "got lucky" and never completed the trial to begin with.
